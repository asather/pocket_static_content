1 killed, 17 wounded in Cleveland biker clubs shooting, police say

A man was killed and 17 other people wounded after gunfire erupted at a party involving multiple motorcycle clubs Saturday night in Cleveland, police said.

Police responded to the scene at about 11:30 p.m. and found a 48-year-old man dead with multiple gunshot wounds, police spokeswoman Sgt. Jennifer Ciaccia said Sunday.

The shooting began after a fight broke out; some people were ejected from the party and later returned, firing on other attendees, some of whom fired back, Sgt. Ciaccia said.

Many of the men and women who were wounded left the scene in private vehicles and arrived at hospitals throughout the city, she said. Their injuries ranged from minor to serious.

“At this time, it is believed that a total of 18 people were shot,” Sgt. Ciaccia said.

The shooting remains under investigation.
