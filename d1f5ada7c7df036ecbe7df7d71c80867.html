Port Authority to resume regular hours for customer service reps starting Monday

Port Authority’s customer service will resume regular hours Monday for riders who need help by phone, through Twitter and via a live chat.

Hours for customer service had been limited to 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. each day as workers transitioned to working from home during the COVID-19 pandemic.

On Monday, those hours will be extended to the regular hours of 6 a.m. to 7 p.m. on weekdays and 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. on weekends and holidays. Customer Service can be reached by calling 412-442-2000, on Twitter @pghtransitcare or via live chat at www.PortAuthority.org during those hours.

The agency’s Downtown Service Center, 623 Smithfield St., will continue to have limited weekday hours of 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. and no weekend or holiday hours. Anyone entering the center must wear a mask.
