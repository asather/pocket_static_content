Pittsburgh police searching for rape suspect

Authorities are searching for a convicted killer who is wanted on rape charges in connection with an incident Sunday in Pittsburgh. 

Police said Michael Cooper, 48, is from the Knoxville/Mount Oliver area. He is described as 5 feet 9 inches tall, weighing 225 pounds, with brown eyes.

Those who see Cooper or know of his whereabouts should call 911.

An arrest warrant was issued charging him with rape, sexual assault, recklessly endangering another person and other offenses, according to court records.

Cooper has prior convictions including first-degree murder and numerous assaults and burglaries, court records show.
