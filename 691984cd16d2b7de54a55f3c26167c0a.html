One transported to hospital in Forbes Ave. crash

One person has been hospitalized Thursday evening following a five-vehicle crash in Uptown, authorities say.

The crash occurred at Forbes Avenue and Van Braam Street. The county Twitter page announced the collision at 6:43 p.m.

Dispatchers said the scene has been cleared by 7:15 p.m and there was one person transported to the hospital.
