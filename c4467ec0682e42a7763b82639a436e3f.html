West Carson closed between Corliss and West End Circle due to water main break; boil water advisory issued

West Carson Street between Corliss Street and the West End Circle is shut down due to a water main break, and a boil water advisory has been issued for several western neighborhoods.

The 20-inch water main break occurred at the intersection of West Carson and Steuben streets about 7:10 p.m., according to emergency dispatchers.

Pittsburgh police are advising drivers to find an alternate route.

The break was isolated and water pressure returned to approximately 2,000 residents in portions of several western neighborhoods at 10:30 p.m., according to Pittsburgh Water and Sewer Authority officials.

However, the authority is advising that residents in Crafton Heights, Duquesne Heights, Elliott, Esplen, Ridgemont and Westwood flush and boil their water as a precaution.
