SWAT called to residence in Larimer; one man arrested

A male was arrested Friday night after SWAT and police arrived at the scene responding to reports of a domestic dispute at a residence on the 600 block of Lenora Street around 9 p.m. in the Larimer neighborhood, according to Public Safety spokesperson Chris Togneri.

When Zone 5 officers tried to make contact with the male, police say he refused to exit the home.

SWAT was then called to the scene and neighboring residences were evacuated.

Police say the man climbed through a window and onto a roof of the two-story house. Around 10:30 p.m., SWAT officers gained access to the roof and took the man into custody without incident, Mr. Togneri said. 

The man was arrested on an outstanding warrant for an unrelated domestic incident.
