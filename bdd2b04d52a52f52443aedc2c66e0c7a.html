Missing Northview Heights girl returns home

Pittsburgh police said the a missing 12-year-old girl from Northview Heights, whose disappearance prompted a call for the public’s help, “returned home safely.”

Gyslene Niyonizigiye was last seen in the North Side neighborhood around 1 to 2 p.m. Saturday when she is believed to have visited a friend’s house there. 

A tweet by Pittsburgh police announced her return around  12:30 p.m. Sunday.
