Fire breaks out at Sunny Jim’s in Kilbuck

A fire broke out at Sunny Jim’s Tavern in Kilbuck on Monday night. 

Firefighters responded to the incident at the restaurant on the 200 block of Camp Horne Road at approximately 9:50 p.m.

It is not currently known if there were any injuries or how the fire began.
