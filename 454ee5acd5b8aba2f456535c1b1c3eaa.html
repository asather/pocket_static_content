Man charged in October shooting death of 24-year-old in McKeesport

Allegheny County police on Wednesday charged a man with criminal homicide nearly a year after the shooting death of a 24-year-old man at a Family Dollar in McKeesport.

Master William Thornton, 18, of McKeesport, also was charged with criminal conspiracy, robbery and possession of a firearm by a minor.

Police allege that he and a second person got into a fight with Dwayne Fuller at the store on Versailles Avenue in October. Mr. Fuller was shot during the altercation and died at the hospital.

Authorities said they are still searching for the second suspect.

Mr. Thornton on Wednesday was taken to the Allegheny County Jail where he is being held without bail, court records show. 

A preliminary hearing is set for Aug. 5 at the City Court building in Downtown.

Anyone with information concerning this incident is asked to call the Allegheny County Police tip line at 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477). Callers can remain anonymous.
