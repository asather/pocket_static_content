Authorities ID body recovered from Mon River

A body that was retrieved from the Monongahela River on Sunday evening has been identified as the person who authorities searched for in the river on May 20.

The body of Kenneth Subotich, 55, of Munhall, was recovered around 6:15 p.m. Sunday near the the 4700 block of East Carson Street in Baldwin Borough.

He had been in the river since Wednesday, when authorities were told that someone had fallen or jumped from the Homestead Grays Bridge.

The Allegheny County medical examiner’s office has yet to release a cause or manner of Mr. Subotich’s death.
