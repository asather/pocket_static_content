Matchbox Twenty coming to S&T Music Park in August

Matchbox Twenty will return to S&T Music Park on Aug. 26 with special guest The Wallflowers.

Matchbox Twenty fanclub members can purchase tickets beginning Tuesday at 10 a.m. Tickets go on sale to the general public starting Friday noon at LiveNation.com.

Citi cardmembers will have access to purchase presale tickets beginning Wednesday at 10 a.m. through Thursday at 10 p.m. at www.citientertainment.com.

This is Matchbox Twenty’s first tour since 2017, when they reunited for the "A Brief History of Everything" tour to mark the 20th anniversary of debut album “Yourself or Someone Like You.”

Rob Thomas said, “I’m proud to be a part of the greatest pop rock band in the world and I can’t wait to play with them again. Stepping on stage with Matchbox and sharing those songs with the fans feels as natural as breathing to me at this point.”
