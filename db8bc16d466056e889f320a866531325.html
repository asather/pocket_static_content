Court suspends Swissvale attorney

The state Supreme Court has suspended an Allegheny County lawyer, Michael Bruce Greenstein, for five years retroactive to Aug. 9, 2018.

The disciplinary board of the high court said Mr. Greenstein, of Swissvale, misappropriated about $61,000 in client funds. Mr. Greenstein runs Greenstein Family Law Services.
