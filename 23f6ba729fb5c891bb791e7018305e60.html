Basketball hightlights: Thomas Jefferson, Trinity guards eclipse 1,000-point milestone

Both Thomas Jefferson senior guard Alyssa DeAngelo and Trinity senior guard Riley DeRubbo eclipsed the 1,000-point plateau Monday when the two teams met to battle for second place in Class 5A Section 1.

And when the dust settled, it was the Hillers who finished directly behind undefeated Chartiers Valley after the first half of the section slate.

DeRubbo finished with a game-high 20 and Courtney Dahlquist scored seven of her 13 in the fourth quarter as No. 2 Trinity (10-2, 6-1) broke open what was a tight game at halftime and rolled to a 61-50 victory over No. 3 Thomas Jefferson (10-4, 5-2) in their first meeting of the season.

DeAngelo finished with nine points to give her 1,003 in her career, but only had two points in the second half. Graci Fairman led the Jaguars with 16.

DeRubbo now has 1,002 points in her career.
