'In Pursuit With John Walsh' to feature 2008 Clairton shooting suspect James Garland Watts

On Easter Sunday in 2008, just before 10 p.m., authorities say James Garland Watts entered a home on Clairton’s Baker Avenue and shot Shawnta and David Lee.

The wife died shortly thereafter. David Lee survived, and he told authorities who shot them.

In the 12 years since, Watts has eluded capture. He’s the subject of “In Pursuit With John Walsh,” an Investigation Discovery program airing at 10 p.m. Wednesday, March 43/4.

Callahan Walsh, John’s son and a co-host of the show, visited Clairton and Pittsburgh for the segment. It runs in the second half of the hour-long program and features interviews with friends, relatives, and federal and Allegheny County authorities.

In the segment, viewers are asked to be on the lookout for Watts. A confidential tip number is 1-833-378-7783 or at InPursuitTips.com.
