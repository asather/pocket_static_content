Police arrest 14-year-old in McKees Rocks shooting over cigarette

McKees Rocks police have charged a 14-year-old boy in connection with the shooting of a man multiple times last week during a dispute over a cigarette.

The boy, who was not identified, was taken to Shuman Juvenile Detention Center in Lincoln-Lemington.

Police are seeking two more suspects.

The incident occurred Feb. 22 around 4:19 p.m. in the 100 block of Munson Avenue.

Police said the victim was walking home from a store when three males approached him asking for a cigarette.

The man refused, and a fight broke out. One of the assailants pulled a gun and shot the man in the hand and upper body, police said.

The victim ran away. He was taken to a hospital in stable condition.

Police arrested the teen on Thursday.

Anyone with information can call Allegheny County police at 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477).
