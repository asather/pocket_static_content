Traffic alert: Motorcyclist injured in Parkway East crash

A motorcyclist was injured Monday morning in a crash involving a vehicle on outbound Interstate 376/Parkway East that caused traffic backups from Downtown through Oakland.

The crash — reported between the Grant Street on-ramp and the Forbes Avenue/Oakland (Exit 72A) off-ramp — shut down two lanes of eastbound traffic, according to PennDOT. The far left lane remained open.

State police were alerted to the crash around 11 a.m.

Police said the motorcycle driver suffered injuries, and responders were still on the scene as of 12:15 p.m. The extent of the injuries was not immediately known.
