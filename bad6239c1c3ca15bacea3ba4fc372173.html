Police: Man killed in West Mifflin shooting was trying to break up fight between armed mom and her son

The man killed Saturday night in West Mifflin was trying to end an argument between another man and that man’s mother, Allegheny County police said.

County investigators said there was a party at the residence in the 100 block of Palmer Lane when, around 10:20 p.m., Anthony Lamarr Orosco, 21, of Pine Knot, Ky., was shot in the chest.

According to police, an argument broke out during the party between Dante Terrell King, 19, and his mother. “Both King and his mother produced firearms,” police said.

Mr. Orosco “attempted to calm the argument,” police said when a struggle ensued for Mr. King’s firearm. Mr. Orosco was shot during that struggle.

He was taken by ambulance to UPMC McKeesport hospital where he was pronounced dead shortly after 11 p.m.

Police said Mr. King has been charged with criminal homicide and taken to the Allegheny County Jail.

A preliminary hearing has been scheduled for Feb. 19.
