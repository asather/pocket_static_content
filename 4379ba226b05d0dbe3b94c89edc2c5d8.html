Police: Man kills father inside Harmar RV, turns self in

A 22-year-old man fatally shot his father early Wednesday morning inside a recreational vehicle in Harmar and then turned himself in, police said.

Shane Howard is facing charges of homicide and tampering with physical evidence, according to Allegheny County police.

Mr. Howard killed David Howard, 43, with a shotgun during a fight in the 700 block of Guys Run Road, police said. He turned himself in at the Harmar police station around 3:17 a.m.

Harmar police found the elder Mr. Howard on the floor of the RV.

After the shooting, police said, Shane Howard removed weapons from the RV and was picked up by a friend, who then convinced him to surrender.

No further information was immediately available.
