Port Authority postpones remaining community engagement meetings due to COVID-19

Port Authority has postponed four remaining community engagement meetings, including one scheduled Wednesday in Oakdale, due to concerns about the COVID-19 virus.

The agency has completed five of nine meetings designed to get public comment on issues including the agency’s long-range service plan, fare policy and development of a mobile payment app. Wednesday evening’s meeting had been scheduled at Pittsburgh Technology College.

The other canceled meetings had been set for March 18 at the Bethel Park Community Center; April 1 at the Springdale Veterans Association and April 23 at University of Pittsburgh’s Alumni Hall in Oakland.

Those meetings will be rescheduled, but the authority said riders also could watch a previous session and offer comments online at portauthority.org/GetTogether.

For more information on COVID-19, go to www.alleghenycounty.us/coronavirus.
