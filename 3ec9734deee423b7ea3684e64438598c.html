Steelers linebacker Ryan Shazier reportedly listing Fox Chapel home for nearly $3 million

Ryan Shazier is moving out.

Realtor.com’s Randy White reported Tuesday that the Steelers linebacker is putting his Fox Chapel home up for sale with a hefty $2.95 million asking price.

He purchased the property for $2.6 million in 2017, soon after the spinal injury that has put his football career on pause. Now it appears he’s looking to unload the spacious 9,000 square foot home -- which features seven bedrooms and 7.5 bathrooms -- for a tidy profit.

Shazier and wife Michelle welcomed a son, Lyon, to their growing family in January of 2019.

He has not played with the team since his 2017 injury, but he remains an official member of the roster. The Steelers placed him on the reserve/retired list.
