Girl last seen on North Side is found safe

This story was updated June 4 at 10:45 p.m.

A 12-year-old girl reported missing on Thursday was found safe. 

Police reported Destiny Stine — who was last seen on Pittsburgh’s North Side — had gone missing earlier in the evening. She was reported found around 10:15 p.m.
