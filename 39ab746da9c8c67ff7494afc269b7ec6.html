Police investigating after two stabbed in Middle Hill

Pittsburgh Police from Zone 2 responded to a report of two adults who walked into a local hospital with stab wounds just before 1:30 a.m. 

The male victim arrived in serious condition; the female victim was in stable condition.

Officers investigated a possible crime scene in the 700 block of Junilla Street, in the Middle Hill section of Pittsburgh.

There are no arrests.
