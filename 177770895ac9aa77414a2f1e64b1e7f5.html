One person in critical condition after shooting in Knoxville neighborhood

One adult male has been transported to a local hospital in critical condition after reports of a shooting in the Knoxville neighborhood. 

Pittsburgh police, Mt. Oliver police and EMS responded to a ShotSpotter alert on the 200 block of Knoxville St. around 5:29 p.m. Friday, according to Public Safety spokesperson Chris Togneri.

First responders found one adult male at the scene with multiple gun shot wounds. The male was then transported by EMS to a local hospital in critical condition, according to Mr. Togneri.
