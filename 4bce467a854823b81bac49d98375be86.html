Vickey's Soul Grill Desserts in Monroeville ordered closed by health department

Health inspectors shut down Vickey’s Soul Grill Desserts at Monroeville Mall for operating without a health permit and other food safety violations.

Besides no permit, the facility lacked a three-bowl sink to clean and sanitize utensils as well as sufficient ventilation and required commercial-grade cooking and refrigeration equipment, according to an inspection report by the Allegheny County Health Department.

The operator of Vickey’s couldn’t be reached for comment.
