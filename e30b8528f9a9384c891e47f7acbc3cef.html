Deluxe version of Mac Miller's 'Circles' is coming, with two new songs

A deluxe version of Mac Miller’s “Circles” will be released on CD on March 6, according to the late rapper’s family.

The edition, which will feature two new songs, “Right” and “Floating,” will hit streaming services on March 20 followed by the vinyl on April 17.

“Circles,” billed as the sixth and final Mac Miller album, was released to positive reviews on Jan. 17 and charted at No. 3 on the Billboard 200.

Vulture called the record, produced and completed by Jon Brion, Miller’s “purest, most compelling artistic statement.”

According to Billboard, “Circles” had the fifth highest debut this year (based on opening-week equivalent album units):

1. BTS, “Map of the Soul: 422k

2. Eminem, “Music to Be Murdered By”: 279k

3. Halsey, “MANIC”: 239k

4. Justin Bieber, “Changes”: 231k

5. Mac Miller, “Circles”: 164k
