Firefighters battle 2-alarm fire in Whitehall

Firefighters on Sunday night battled a two-alarm fire in Whitehall.

The commercial structure fire was reported on the 2700 block of Saw Mill Run Boulevard just before 10 p.m., dispatchers said.

Route 51 was closed in both direction in the area as crews responded to the blaze.

There are currently no reported injuries.
