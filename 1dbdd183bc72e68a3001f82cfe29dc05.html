1 killed in two-vehicle crash on Route 8 in Richland

A person was killed Wednesday in a two-vehicle crash on Route 8 in Richland, according to KDKA-TV, the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette’s news partner.

The crash happened just after 9 a.m. at Route 8 and Sandy Hill Road.

The name of the victim has not been released and it’s unclear if there were other injuries.

KDKA said the crash involved a car and a tractor-trailer. Police have not released a cause.
