Detectives investigating deadly shooting in Wilkinsburg

Allegheny County homicide detectives on Wednesday are investigating the shooting death of a 16-year-old in Wilkinsburg.

Authorities were notified of the shooting around 9:08 p.m. and arrived at the scene near Laketon Road and Robinson Boulevard.

First responders found a teenage boy who had been shot. Medics attempted lifesaving measures, but the victim died from his injuries, Wilkinsburg police said.

The victim has not yet been identified.

Wilkinsburg police said they were working with witnesses who were at the scene.

County police asked anyone with information about the incident to call their tip line at 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477). Callers can remain anonymous.
