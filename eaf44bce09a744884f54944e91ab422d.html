Highmark partners with referral network to help those facing food, housing crises

Highmark Inc., Allegheny Health Network and Gateway Health are partnering with Aunt Bertha, a Texas-based online social care referral network, to help people facing a food shortage, a housing crisis or transportation.

The Aunt Bertha directory has geographic-specific information on social service agencies and community-based organizations throughout the U.S., including COVID-19-specific services.

The directory can be found at any of three Highmark Health websites: Medical and Community Resources page at HighmarkAnswers.com, AHN.org/coronavirus or GatewayHealthPlan.com/coronavirus. 

The service is free and there are no income restraints, nor is the service restricted to Highmark members.

Highmark Health spokesman Dan Onorato said the partnership was already in the works before the COVID-19 outbreak but “at this critical time, we understood the importance of fast-tracking our implementation of the Aunt Bertha tool.”
