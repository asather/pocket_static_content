Police ask for help finding missing woman

Pittsburgh police are asking for help finding a missing 27-year-old woman.

Jamillah Freeman Pryce was last seen in Oakland on Wednesday. Police said she is about 5 feet, 2 inches tall, around 155 pounds, with brown eyes and black “twisted” hair.

Detectives with Pittsburgh police’s Special Victims Unit say Jamillah was last seen wearing a light red jacket, a peach colored shirt, multicolored leggings and black shoes.

Anyone with information on her whereabouts is asked to dial 911 or call the SVU at 412-323-7141.
