Local restaurant supplier Clean Care shuts down hospitality unit

Lawrenceville-based Clean Care — a linen and textile laundry service for restaurants, hotels and hospitals — shut down its hospitality division Friday and laid off some 100 workers.

Business dried up due to the mandatory statewide shutdown of restaurants and bars during the COVID-19 crisis, chief operating officer Linda Burgman said.

The company, which has been in business since 1933, continues to operate its healthcare division, she said. That unit also employs about 100 people.
