Homicide detectives investigate fatal shooting in North Braddock

Homicide detectives on Wednesday investigated a fatal shooting of a 19-year-old in North Braddock.

Around 3:40 pm, dispatchers were notified of a shooting on the 700 block of Baldridge Avenue. First responders arrived on scene and found two people who were shot: a 19-year-old man, and a 20-year-old man who was located a block away.

The 19-year-old died at the hospital after both victims were transported. His name has not yet been released.

Allegheny County police are investigating the incident.
