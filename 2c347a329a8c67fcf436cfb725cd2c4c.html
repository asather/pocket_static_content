Motorcyclist hospitalized after accident on Route 65 near McKees Rocks Bridge

Pittsburgh police responded to a crash between a car and a motorcycle along Route 65 on Monday night.

When police arrived at the scene near the McKees Rocks Bridge, they found the female motorcyclist unresponsive. She was later taken to the hospital in critical condition. Neither the driver of the car nor the front-seat passenger were injured.

According to the police statement, the driver of the car was pulling out of a driveway on Route 65 when the motorcyclist hit the bumper of his vehicle.

The investigation is ongoing.
