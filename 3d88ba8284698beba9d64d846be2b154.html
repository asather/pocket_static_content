Two North Allegheny schools closed Thursday after water main break

The North Allegheny School District will close two schools on its Marshall campus Thursday due to a water main break.

Marshall Elementary and Marshall Middle schools will be closed for classes and after-school activities, according to an alert on the district’s website.

The elementary school’s orchestra concert that was scheduled to be held at NASH is now canceled. The YMCA before- and after-school programs at the elementary school are also canceled, according to the alert.
