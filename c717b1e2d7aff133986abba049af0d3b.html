'Back in Bloom' at Phipps

Phipps Conservatory and Botanical Gardens welcomes back its Summer Flower Show and visitors on Saturday with “Back in Bloom.” For safety’s sake, all Phipps staff and visitors must wear masks unless they have a medical conditions. Also, all tickets must be reserved in advance at phipps.conservatory.org/tickets. 

Timed tickets will ensure that there are no crowds as visitors admire the flowers in the Victorian greenhouse and gardens in Oakland. 

Signs have been placed on the floors throughout the conservatory to remind guests to stay 6 feet apart and there will be no hands-on exhibits. When in doubt, do not touch. Information: phipps.conservatory.org.
