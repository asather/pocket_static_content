Mayor Peduto, public safety officials to livestream 2 p.m. COVID-19 press conference

Pittsburgh Mayor Bill Peduto and public safety officials will hold a 2 p.m. press conference on the city’s COVID-19 response.

The update will include general information about city activities, and no major announcements are expected, according to the mayor’s office.

The event will be available via Facebook Live. The mayor’s office Facebook page can be found at facebook.com/city.of.pittsburgh.
