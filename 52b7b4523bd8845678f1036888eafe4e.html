Six more employees at Smithfield Foods plant in Arnold test positive for COVID-19

Six more employees at the Smithfield Foods Inc. meat processing plant in Arnold tested positive for the novel coronavirus, according to Pittsburgh Post-Gazette news partner KDKA.

According to United Food and Commercial Workers Union Local 1776, the total number of employees at the plant that have tested positive is now at 12.

The union represents just over 100 people at the plant and about 35,000 workers throughout the state.

Smithfield Foods closed its South Dakota pork processing plant in South Dakota last month until further notice after hundreds of employees there tested positive for the disease there.

Company officials could not be immediately reached for comment.
