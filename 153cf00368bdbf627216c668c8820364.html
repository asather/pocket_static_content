The Killers, Johnny Marr coming to the Petersen in October

The Killers will make a rare Pittsburgh appearance, playing the Petersen Events Center in Oakland on Oct. 8 with Johnny Marr, the former guitarist for The Smiths.

Tickets go on sale at 10 a.m. March 20 via Livenation.com.

The Las Vegas rock band will be touring behind the new album “Imploding the Mirage,” due on May 29.
