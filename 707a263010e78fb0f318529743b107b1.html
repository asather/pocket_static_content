Hall & Oates, Alice Cooper coming to S&T Bank Music Park

Daryl Hall & John Oates will play S&T Bank Music Park on July 28 with Squeeze and KT Tunstall.

Tickets go on sale at 10 a.m. Friday at Livenation.com.

Pre-sale for VIP and general tickets start Tuesday and extend through Thursday at 10 p.m. local time. Citi cardmembers will have access to purchase presale tickets beginning Tuesday at 10 a.m. through Thursday at 10 p.m. at www.citientertainment.com.

Also announced: Alice Cooper will play the venue with Lita Ford and Tesla on June 26. Tickets go on sale at noon Friday.
