Shooting in Wilkinsburg leaves female in critical condition

A female victim was in critical condition after being shot Wednesday morning in Wilkinsburg.

The shooting occurred around 10:38 a.m. in the 600 block of Holmes Street.

First responders found the victim inside a home. She was taken to the hospital.

Allegheny County police homicide detectives are investigating.

No further information was available.

Anyone with information can call 1-833-ALL-TIPS.
