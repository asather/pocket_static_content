Police investigate shooting in Clairton

Two people were shot in Clairton late Monday in the area of Wylie Avenue and Henry Street.

The shooting occurred around 11:22 p.m. Responding officers found a 16-year-old female shot in the ankle and a 25-year-old male shot in the thigh. Both were conscious and alert.

The victims were transported by ambulance to local hospitals for treatment.

Allegheny County Police Department's Homicide Unit was called to assist the investigation.

Anyone with information is asked to call the County Police Tip Line 1-833-ALL-TIPS (255-8477). Callers can remain anonymous.
