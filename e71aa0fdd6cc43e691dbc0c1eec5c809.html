The Commonheart will play Stage AE 10th Anniversary Show in August

When Stage AE opened back in late 2010, the first two shows at the North Shore venue were Girl Talk and Wiz Khalifa.

Now, Stage AE taps back into that excitement with The Commonheart, one of the city’s premier bands and soul-rock force, playing a 10th Anniversary Show on Aug. 8 on the outdoor side.

Tickets are $10 and go on sale Friday at ticketmaster.com.

Girl Talk’s 10th Anniversary Show on May 1 sold out immediately.
