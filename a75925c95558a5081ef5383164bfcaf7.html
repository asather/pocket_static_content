Pence to visit Greensburg

Vice President Mike Pence will be making an appearance in Greensburg on Thursday.

Mr. Pence will visit the Greensburg Police Department to host a “Cops for Trump” event, according to President Donald Trump’s campaign.

The event is scheduled to start at noon.
