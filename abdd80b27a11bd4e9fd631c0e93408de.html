Kennywood cancels fall events

Though Kennywood Park is slated to open to the public on July 11, some of its classic events will be missing from the calendar.

The park announced on its Facebook page Tuesday that Phantom Fright Nights, Happy Hauntings and Fall Fantasy have all been canceled this year due to COVID-19 related concerns.

They are also postponing the celebration of the Jack Rabbit roller coaster’s 100th anniversary until next summer.

According to the park’s Facebook post, guests will still be able to visit the park in the fall, but with limited hours. The park will be open Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays from October 2 through November 1.

Currently, Kennywood still plans on holding the Pierogi Fest on Sunday, Sept. 7 and the Holiday Lights festival that runs from November 27 until December 20.

Visitors are required to follow guidelines put in place by the CDC. Specifics about the guidelines and additional information about other events at Kennywood can be found on their website.
