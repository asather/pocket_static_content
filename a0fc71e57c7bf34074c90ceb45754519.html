Homicide police investigating after man found dead in Mon River

This story was updated at 8:55 p.m. on May 24, 2020.

Homicide detectives are investigating after a body was found in the Monongahela River Sunday night near Pittsburgh’s Hays neighborhood.

Authorities were dispatched just before 6:15 p.m for a body in the river near the 4700 block of East Carson Street.

Firefighters found a deceased man in the river, and Pittsburgh EMS River Rescue retrieved the body and transported it to their boat house on the North Shore.

The man’s identity has not yet been released; the Allegheny County medical examiner will determine the cause and manner of death. 

The call came in as part of a mutual aid request from Baldwin Borough.

Allegheny County police asked anyone with information on the incident to call their anonymous tip line at 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477).
