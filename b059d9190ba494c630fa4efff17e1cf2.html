Man shot along Brownsville Road in Carrick

A man was shot early Tuesday morning along Brownsville Road in the Carrick neighborhood of Pittsburgh.

Police responded to a 911 call around 1:49 a.m. for four shots fired in the parking lot of a church in the 1900 block of Brownsville Road. 

Officers found a 32-year-old male grazed by a bullet in his stomach about a block away from the scene.

The victim was transported to a local hospital in stable condition.

No arrests were reported. Officers continue to investigate.
