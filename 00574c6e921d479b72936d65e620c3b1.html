Homicide police investigate deadly home invasion in Brentwood

Allegheny County Police on Wednesday are investigating a home invasion that led to a fatal shooting in Brentwood.

A home invasion on the 100 block of Bellcrest Avenue was reported around 10:00 p.m. Authorities arrived and discovered the homeowner shot the intruder, police said.

The person was pronounced deceased at the scene.

Homicide detectives are investigating. The decedent has not yet been identified.
