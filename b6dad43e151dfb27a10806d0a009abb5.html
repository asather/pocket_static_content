City's Commission on Human Relations executive director to leave job

The executive director of Pittsburgh’s Commission on Human Relations will leave her post at the end of this week.

Megan Stanley, who has been executive director of the commission since June 2019, will head to a new role in the King County, Wash., Department of Community and Human Services.

Ms. Stanley served as the commission’s acting director from December 2018 to June 2019.

Jam Hammond, the commission’s outreach and intake coordinator, will serve as acting director until the position is filled.

The commission is empowered under the city code to investigate complaints of alleged discrimination in employment, housing, and public accommodations within the City of Pittsburgh.
