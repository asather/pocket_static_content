Coraopolis singer/songwriter, 12, on 'America's Got Talent'

Some of Pittsburgh’s talent will be on display nationwide on Tuesday night.

Singer/songwriter Ashley Marina, 12, of Coraopolis, is expected to be featured this week on Season 15 of the NBC network reality competition stalwart, “America’s Got Talent.” Now in its fourth audition round, the show taped some performers before the pandemic shut down Hollywood. Others are shown in promos for the show performing without a live audience. 

“America’s Got Talent” airs locally on WPXI at 8 p.m.

Videos of some of Ashley’s songs can be found on her website, IAmAshleyMarina.com and on Facebook.
