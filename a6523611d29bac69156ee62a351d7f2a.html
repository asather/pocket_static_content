One dead, one injured in Westmoreland County crash

One person was killed and another was transported to a hospital after a two-vehicle crash in Irwin.

The collision occurred on U.S. Route 30 near Colony Drive, according to Westmoreland County dispatch. The call came in around 9:05 p.m.

A motorcycle and another vehicle collided in the westbound lanes, and one person became entrapment in the vehicle. That person was taken to Forbes hospital, dispatchers said.

Both the eastbound and westbound lanes were closed Saturday night while authorities reconstructed the scene.

The coroner was called to the scene around 10 p.m.
