NEA grants boost 10 individual works and programs in Western Pennsylvania

The National Endowment of the Arts winter grant announcement Wednesday included $1,140,000 for Pennsylvania — $220,000 in the western part of the state — among the $27.3 million bestowed upon specific arts projects nationwide.
