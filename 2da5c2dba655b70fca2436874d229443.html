Weekend football betting guide: Pitt is a double-digit favorite for a third time

Looking to place a wager on some football games this weekend? The Post-Gazette has all the information you need to make picks in key contests, locally and nationally. Here’s the outlook for games Oct. 1-5

Odds are current as of Wednesday afternoon, per DraftKings. Historical information is from Odds Shark.

N.C. State at Pitt

Spread: Pitt, -14

Moneyline: Pitt -590, N.C. State +410

Over/under: 47.0

Facts and figures: The Wolfpack have lost against the spread in six of its past seven games as underdogs. … The over has cashed in five of N.C. State’s past six games. … The teams have met just once previously as conference opponents, with N.C. State covering as a 10.5-point favorite at Heinz Field in 2017. … The Panthers are 3-5-1 against the spread as home favorites since the start of last season. … Pitt has not yet covered as a favorite in a conference game this season.

Thursday night: Denver at New York Jets

Spread: Denver, -1

Moneyline: Denver -103, New York Jets -112

Over/under: 39.5

Facts and figures: The Jets have lost six of their past eight games against the spread, a skid that includes defeats in each of their first three games this season. The Broncos, by contrast, have won five of their past eight games against the spread. … Each of the Jets’ three games this season has finished with a total above 39.5. Two of the Broncos three games have not. … Denver has won five of the past seven meetings in the series straight up.

Sunday night: Philadelphia at San Francisco

Spread: San Francisco, -7

Moneyline: San Francisco -286, Philadelphia +245

Over/under: 45.0

Facts and figures: The Eagles have lost each of their past four games against the spread, part of a 3-8 skid that dates to November of last season. … Philadelphia has won six of the past meetings straight up. … San Francisco is 3-5-1 against the spread as a favorite of at least seven points since the start of last season.

Monday night: Atlanta at Green Bay

Spread: Green Bay -7.5

Moneyline: Green Bay -315, Atlanta +270

Over/under: 57.5

Facts and figures: The over has cashed in six of Atlanta’s past seven games. Four of those games have seen totals go over 57.5 total points. … Three of the teams’ past five meetings have gone over 57.5 points, but all of them have reached a total of at least 54. … Green Bay has won five of its past seven games against the spread.

College football Saturday: No. 13 Texas A&M at No. 2 Alabama

Spread: Alabama, -17

Moneyline: Alabama -910, Texas A&M +575

Over/under: 51.5

Facts and figures: Alabama has won the seven of the teams’ eight meetings as conference opponents by an average of 18.4 points per game. … Six of those eight games have gone over a total of 51.5. … The Aggies have lost four of their past six games against the spread.

No. 7 Auburn at No. 4 Georgia

Spread: Georgia, -6.5

Moneyline: Georgia -250, Auburn +200

Over/under: 45.0

Facts and figures: The Bulldogs have won six of the past seven meetings by an average of 8.8 points per game. … Six of those seven games have gone under a total of 45 points. … Auburn was 3-1 against the spread as an underdog last season.
