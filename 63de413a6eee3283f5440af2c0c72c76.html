Penn State Blue-White scrimmage will air nationally

Penn Stateâ€™s Blue-White spring scrimmage will be broadcast to a national audience in 2020. The school announced Friday afternoon that Fox Sports 1 will carry the game, slated for Saturday, April 18 at 1:30 p.m. It will also be streamed on the Fox Sports app. 

Admission is free for all fans. Parking is free as well for 2019 season ticket and parking permit holders, though they must display a white pass that was mailed to them last summer. Those who did not have season tickets or parking permits for last season will be charged $20 for a spot. 

Penn State said an estimated 61,000 fans turned out for the event last season, and the game has drawn 51,000-plus for nine of the past 11 years.
