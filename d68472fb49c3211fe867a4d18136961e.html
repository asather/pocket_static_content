Search continues for fallen hiker at McConnells Mill State Park

First responders are continuing their search on Sunday for a man who fell into a creek Saturday at McConnells Mill State Park in Lawrence County.

Searchers are looking for a 38-year-old man from Meadville after he was reported to have fallen into Slippery Rock Creek around 2:30 p.m. Saturday. His name has not be released publicly.

The man was reported to have fallen between the Mill and Eckert bridges at the park, according to park ranger Tyler Johnston.
