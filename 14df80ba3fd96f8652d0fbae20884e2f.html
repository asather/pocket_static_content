WATCH: Pittsburgh pro sports and the coronavirus pandemic

The COVID-19 pandemic has put much of the professional sports world on hold for the foreseeable future. League operations have been suspended and players have been sent home, and it’s unclear just how long the holding pattern will last.

In the video interviews below, three of the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette’s pro sports insiders — Matt Vensel (Penguins/NHL), Jason Mackey (Pirates/MLB) and Brian Batko (Steelers/NFL) — share their thoughts on the cancellations, daily changes and what could lie ahead for your favorite teams and leagues.
