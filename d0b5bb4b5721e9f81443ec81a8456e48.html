Pittsburgh Wrestling Classic postponed due to coronavirus concerns

Amidst all the cancellations and postponements of major sporting events across the country because of concerns over the spread of COVID-19, the “Rose Bowl of Wrestling” is the next to follow suit.

The Pittsburgh Wrestling Classic, an annual high school All-Star event pitting the best seniors from the WPIAL and Pennsylvania against top seniors from across the country, has been postponed. The event was scheduled for Saturday, March 14, at Fitzgerald Field House.

The co-directors of the event, Kraig Nellis and Philip Mary, said in a statement that the organizers “will make every effort to reschedule the event” at a later date.

This year’s competition was scheduled to pit the WPIAL All-Stars against an all-star team from Maryland in the undercard matchup, while the Pennsyvlania All-Stars would face off against the United States All-Stars in the main event.
