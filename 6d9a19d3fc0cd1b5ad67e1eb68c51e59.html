Allegheny County announces first COVID-19 cases

This is a developing story. Please check back for updates.

Allegheny County has announced the first COVID-19 cases.

The county made the announcement Saturday afternoon but provided no immediate details.

County Executive Rich Fitzgerald and the county health department’s incoming director Debra Bogen will provide more information later Saturday.

The county’s news release specifically referred to “cases,” not just a single instance of a presumptive positive test for the virus.

The event will be livestreamed on the county’s Facebook page at 2 p.m. from the county health department.
