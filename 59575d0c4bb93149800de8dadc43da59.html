Thoms Run Road remains closed after water main break; service to resume Wednesday evening

Residents near Thoms Run Road who have been without water since a line break Tuesday will receive service again Wednesday evening.

Thoms Run Road however will remain closed between Presto-Sygan and Prestley roads until further notice, said Gary Lobaugh, spokesman for Pennsylvania American Water.

The 30-inch main broke just after 3:30 p.m sending water gushing into the air.
