Drag racer at Keystone Raceway in Westmoreland County killed

A drag race driver was killed Friday afternoon when the vehicle he was operating on the track at Keystone Raceway in Derry Township made an abrupt right turn, struck a guiderail and a light pole, then overturned multiple times, Westmoreland County Coroner Kenneth A. Bacha reported.

Killed was James R. Baker, 69, of Alpine Drive, Monroeville. He was pronounced dead at 3:36 p.m. at Excela Latrobe Emergency Department.

Keystone Raceway, which has featured professional and amateur car races since 1968, was hosting the weekend CARS Protection Plus 660 Bulb Nationals on its 1/4-mile drag strip at 538 Stone Jug Rd.
