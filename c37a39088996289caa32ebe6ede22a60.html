Residents hospitalized after collapse at The Grove nursing home in Washington County

A ceiling at a nursing home in Washington County reportedly collapsed Monday night.

According to Pittsburgh Post-Gazette news partner KDKA, officials said late Monday that multiple people were trapped after the collapse at The Grove at Washington.

A supervisor at The Grove who was on duty during the incident said that four residents were trapped inside a room when the ceiling collapsed, but that they were removed shortly after and have been taken to a hospital. 

The 911 call is reported to have come in around 9:19 p.m.

It is unknown at this time if there are any injuries from the incident at the Canton Township nursing home.
