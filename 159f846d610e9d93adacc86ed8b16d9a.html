Pregnant woman airlifted after crash in Westmoreland County

A pregnant woman was flown to a hospital Wednesday night after a vehicle crash in Westmoreland County.

The incident occurred around 9:28 p.m. near Sunny Lane and South Center Avenue in Hempfield, dispatchers said.

The woman was airlifted to a Pittsburgh hospital, according to Mutual Aid EMS. There were no other transports from the crash.
