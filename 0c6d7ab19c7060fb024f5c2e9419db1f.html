Foreigner, Kansas coming to S&T Music Park in August

Foreigner will bring the Juke Box Heroes 2020 tour to S&T Music Park on Aug. 2 with Kansas and Europe.

Tickets go on sale at 10 a.m. Friday at www.livenation.com.

Foreigner’s jukebox includes such hits as “Juke Box Hero,” “Cold As Ice,” “Hot Blooded,” “Feels Like The First Time,” “Urgent” and “Head Games.” The band features one original member in guitarist Mick Jones.
