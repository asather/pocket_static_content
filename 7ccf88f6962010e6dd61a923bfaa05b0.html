Worker hospitalized after industrial accident in Washington County

The federal Occupational Safety and Health Administration is investigating after an employee for a mineral-blending industrial company in Washington County was injured and flown to a hospital Friday morning.

The unidentified man was hurt while working in New Eagle at Therm-O-Rock East Inc. on Pine Street, county dispatchers said. He was taken to a hospital by a medical helicopter before noon. It was not immediately clear what his condition was.

“At this time, OSHA is aware of the incident and we are responding,” said area director Chris Robinson. “Our inspection team is conducting an open investigation.”

Further details were not immediately available.
