Police find three men shot in Mount Oliver

Police found three men shot around 6:35 p.m. Tuesday in Mount Oliver Borough, according to a release from Allegheny County police.

Mount Oliver police and paramedics were called to the 100 block of Stamm Avenue, finding a 20-year-old man shot in the head.

Two other victims fled the scene of the shooting and were found by police nearby: a 23-year-old man found on Arlington Avenue shot in the arm and side and a 19-year-old man on Locust Street shot in the neck.

All three victims were rushed to area hospitals in critical condition.

Detectives from the county homicide and general investigative units responded to the scene. They learned the three men were standing in front of a residence on Stamm Avenue when a man dressed in black clothing approached the three and shot them multiple times with a handgun.

The man fled the scene toward Brownsville Road in an older red pickup truck.

Those with information regarding this incident are asked to call the Allegheny County police tip line at 1-833-255-8477. Callers can remain anonymous.
