PODCAST: Impressions from Ke'Bryan Hayes' debut, silent trade deadline

Pirates insiders Jason Mackey and Mike Persak try to explain why the team didn't trade anyone at the deadline despite pleas from the fan base to start a complete rebuild. They did get a look at one future prospect in Ke'Bryan Hayes, and it couldn't have gone much better. We analyze his debut and how Hayes fits in their plans for the rest of this season.
