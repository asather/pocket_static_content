Pittsburgh International Airport gets $3 million for taxiway work, snow removal equipment

Pittsburgh International Airport has been awarded a $3 million federal grant to rehabilitate a taxiway and to acquire new snow removal equipment.

U.S Transportation Secretary Elaine L. Chao announced the award of the Airport Improvement Program grant Tuesday. In all, the department doled out a total of $273 million to 184 airports nationwide.

The money is in addition to the $36 million that the airport received in April from the Federal Aviation Administration to help maintain operations and replace revenue lost to steep declines in travel caused by the COVID-19 pandemic.
