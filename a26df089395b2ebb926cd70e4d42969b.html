Disturbed, Sammy Hagar added to S&T Bank Music Park season

Live Nation has added two more shows to the S&T Bank Music Park summer lineup.

Nu metal band Disturbed brings The Sickness 20th Anniversary Tour with Staind and Bad Wolves on Aug. 13.

Sammy Hagar, Whitesnake and Night Ranger team up for a hard rock bill on Aug. 19.

Tickets for both shows go on sale at 10 a.m. Friday at Livenation.com.
