Aliquippa baby's death in January ruled homicide

The death in January of a 1-year-old girl from Aliquippa has been ruled a homicide

Riana Hoskins died Jan. 8 at UPMC Children’s Hospital of Pittsburgh as a consequence of a “remote blunt impact” head injury, the Allegheny County Medical Examiner’s Office announced Tuesday morning.

The office said that the girl had been found unresponsive at an address on Johnson Street in Aliquippa on Oct. 6, 2018 and was taken to the hospital.

Authorities in Beaver County could not be reached immediately for comment Tuesday morning.
