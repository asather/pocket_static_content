Got a knack for outdoors photography?

Sean Brady’s still life of a brook trout in the snow was the 2019 winner in the Artsy Shots category of the annual Trout Photo Contest. Share your trout photos in this year’s contest run by the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette.

Submit high-resolution digital images (1MB minimum) including name, age (for minors under 18 years of age), town, county and waters fished, details of the catch, phone number (not for publication) and photographer’s name and town. Send to fishingreport@post-gazette.com by May 17. Winners in the categories Big Fish, Kidstuff and Artsy Shots will be interviewed for a feature article published May 21 online and May 24 in print.
