Police investigate fatal shooting in Upper Lawrenceville

A male was fatally shot early Monday morning at the 5100 block of Kent Way in Upper Lawrenceville.

Police arrived at the scene around 1 a.m. Monday to find a male shot multiple times.

The victim was transported to a local hospital in critical condition. He was pronounced dead shortly after.

There were no other victims or suspect descriptions.

Police are investigating.
