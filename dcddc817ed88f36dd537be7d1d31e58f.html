Man dead after fatal stabbing in Butler

Butler City police on Sunday are investigating a fatal overnight stabbing that killed one man.

Authorities said Robert Wagner, 43, was found on the 130 block of West Jefferson Street around 2:07 a.m., according to Post-Gazette news partner KDKA-TV. He had a stab wound in his chest, and was transported to Butler Memorial Hospital hospital, where he died.

Police are investigating the incident.
