Authorities investigating after 3 dump trucks catch fire in Hays

Fire officials are investigating after three dump trucks caught fire early Wednesday in a construction lot in Hays.

Emergency crews arrived at the Jadell Minniefield Construction lot after 3:30 a.m. A person living in the area called the fire department, Pittsburgh public safety officials said.

Another person who stays in a trailer on the property moved trucks that were next to the burning dump trucks to keep the fire from spreading, Pittsburgh Post-Gazette news partner KDKA-TV said.

Authorities said nothing was in the bed of the trucks at the time of the blaze. 

No injuries were reported.
