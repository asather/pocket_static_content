Seth Rogen's shot-in-Pittsburgh 'American Pickle' releases first trailer

Sure, it’s supposed to be Brooklyn, but we know better.

“An American Pickle,” the Seth Rogen feature film shot in Pittsburgh in late 2018 (yes, and in NYC) released its first trailer today. The movie comes out Aug. 6 on HBO Max.

The WarnerMax film, directed by Brandon Trost, is based on Simon Rich’s New Yorker novella. Rogen plays both Herschel Greenbaum as well as his great-grandson, Ben. After Herschel comes to America he falls into a vat of brine and emerges 100 years later, in 2020. He probably could have picked a better year…

The trailer shows a few Pittsburgh locations, including Liberty Avenue and a press conference shot in a church on the North Side.
