Pittsburgh Riverhounds season suspended through May 10

The Pittsburgh Riverhounds SC and USL Championship season will be suspended through May 10, the United Soccer League announced yesterday, upon approval from the USL Championship’s Board of Governors.

Previously, the league announced the season would be halted for a minimum of 30 days.

The time period aligns with recent guidance from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention on restricting public gatherings of 50 or more people through May 10, a news release said.

The league said it intends to reschedule all suspended matches for another date this season. Tickets to postponed Riverhounds SC home matches will be honored at each game’s respective rescheduled date. 

The safety and well-being of players, coaches, employees and fans are Riverhounds SC's first priority, the news release said. 

Fans with questions regarding ticketing, can call 412-865-GOAL(4625) or visit www.riverhounds.com.
