Former youth soccer administrator pleads guilty to sending obscene pictures to teen girl

A Washington County man who had coached recreational youth soccer in his community pleaded guilty Monday in federal court to a count of sending obscene material to a minor.

Timothy Cribbins, 46, of California, Pa., pleaded to two counts before U.S. District Judge Donetta Ambrose.

Mr. Cribbins had been indicted in 2019.

Prosecutors said that on two occasions in December 2018, he used Snapchat to send photos of his penis to a girl under age 16. At the time, he was president of the California Youth Soccer Club, although he is no longer affiliated with the group.

Judge Ambrose set sentencing for Oct. 15.
