Man shot in McKeesport

A 29-year-old man is in critical condition after being shot multiple times early Saturday morning in McKeesport.

Police responded to the 1400 block of Maple Street at 2:16 a.m., where they found the man with multiple wounds in his lower extremities and lower torso. He was airlifted to a local hospital.

Allegheny County homicide detectives say that the man was with one or more people known to him when one or more people unknown to him began firing. No one else was injured, though a vehicle parked nearby was hit by gunfire.

Police are investigating to determine possible suspects or motives. Anyone with information concerning this incident is asked to call the Allegheny County Police tip line at 1-833-255-8477; callers can remain anonymous. The department can also be reached via its social media sites.
