Gunshot victim found dead under bushes on Manchester street

Pittsburgh police are investigating the death of a man found dead Saturday morning on a Manchester street from an apparent gunshot wound. 

Police said they received a call about 6:40 a.m. about a man lying under some bushes in the 1700 block of Manhattan Street. Police found the victim unresponsive and medics who arrived shortly thereafter pronounced him dead.

Police released no other details about the incident.
