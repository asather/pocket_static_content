Ill say it again: it has been a decade since they fired Dave Wannstedt and the profram still has not recovered fully. Narduzzi finally has the program on the verge of some great things, i believe. Stability is something Pitt FB fans should want.

Matt from LA: How sincere should we consider A.B.'s comments on the Fan this morning or is this just a PR move set up by his representatives?

Matt from LA: Are the Pirates done with their non-rebuilding for this year or should we expect more salary dumps, sorry, trades before opening day?

Hail to Pitt: Paul, do you think State College media cover Pitt the way their respective teams are covered im Pittsburgh? It seems State Penn and WVU get more local attention than Duquesne snd Bobby Mo. Thanks.
Zeise: Sigh. I think it is totally irrelevant and one of the dumbest complaints among Pitt fans. This is a large media market, there are lots of fans of WVU and PSU here. We cover Pitt more than every other college program COMBINED. And it isnt close, either. A lot of PSU fans live here and they are the states team. Pitt is a regional team within the state and State College is a local yocal market and the only team in town there is PSU. They are a much smaller and more defined audience than we have here in Pgh. This is apples and oranges
