Fire burning multiple homes in Vandergrift

Multiple houses are on fire Tuesday along the 100 block of Custer Avenue in Vandergrift.

Police and fire units were responding to a call at 2:22 a.m. for a fire on the front porch of one of the homes. A second call came in 10 minutes later, reporting three houses on fire.

This story will be updated.
