One dead, one seriously injured in Lawrence County ATV crash

One person is dead and another is in the hospital after an ATV crash Tuesday night in Lawrence County.

Two quads collided in a stone quarry in Wayne Township, according to Post-Gazette news partner KDKA-TV. The crash killed one of the drivers and left the other driver seriously injured, KDKA reported.

The injured driver was flown to a Youngstown hospital.

Further information was not immediately available.
