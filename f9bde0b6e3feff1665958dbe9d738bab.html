Large brush fire burns in Shaler recreational area

A large brush fire burned in a Shaler recreational area Tuesday afternoon, according to township police.

Police identified the fire taking place in Fawcett Fields, a recreational area for soccer and fishing off McElheny Road and near Eagle Canyon Nature Trail.

The fire forced traffic to be reduced to a single alternating lane on McElheny Road between Clearview Drive and Wible Run Road.

Police also reported, on Twitter, “a few poles that have cracked and lines are down” near the trail and Little Pine Creek area. The tweet included a photo of cracked power lines.

Downed high tension wires from sheared power and telephone lines have been attributed as the cause of the brush fire and responders are on the scene, according to a tweet from Allegheny County.
