The 62nd Street Bridge re-opened Thursday evening after an overturned tractor trailer that was hauling salvage cars snarled  traffic near the Lawrenceville side during the evening rush hour.

The crash occurred around 2:30 p.m., when the truck was making a turn onto the bridge from Butler Street. 

The northbound lane of Butler Street/Route 8 was closed until roughly 6:15 p.m. while emergency crews cleared the scene, according to PennDOT.

The driver was not injured, and no other vehicles were involved, according to Pittsburgh police.
