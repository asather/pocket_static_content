Maintenance worker at Port Authority's West Mifflin garage tests positive for COVID-19

A fifth Port Authority employee, a maintenance worker at the West Mifflin garage, has tested positive for the COVID-19 virus, the agency said Friday.

The worker is the third to test positive at that garage and the second throughout the system not working as a bus operator to come down with the disease. The employee has been in self-quarantine since last working on April 10.

Another employee who works closely with the latest positive worker also is on self-quarantine with pay.

The authority has sent a crew to the garage to perform deep cleaning to sanitize the facility.
