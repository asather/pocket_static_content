Police seek suspect in McKeesport fatal shooting

Allegheny County police are investigating a fatal shooting Monday evening in McKeesport.

City of McKeesport police responded around 11:22 p.m. Monday to reports of a shooting in the 47 block of Crawford Village housing complex.

Police found a 54-year-old male deceased from a gunshot wound. The victim's name was not released.

County police is asking anyone with information about the incident to call 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477). Callers can remain anonymous.
