Robert Morris announces move to Horizon League in most sports

This is a developing story and will be updated.

Robert Morris officials announced Monday that the athletic department will join the Horizon League in most sports beginning July 1.

“This is the next great step forward for our student-athletes and a great day in the history of Robert Morris University,” school president Chris Howard said in a statement. “We are excited for the excellent competition that awaits in the Horizon League and for the opportunities it presents for the university.”

The Colonials will compete as part of the league in 10 of 15 sports: men’s and women’s basketball, men’s and women’s soccer, volleyball, women’s cross country, indoor and outdoor track & field, men’s golf and softball. Other Horizon League schools include Youngstown State ,Cleveland State and Detroit Mercy, among others.

The school also announced Monday that the football program will join the Big South Conference as an associate member starting in 2021.
