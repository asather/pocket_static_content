Police searching for missing boy last seen in Ross

Police on Wednesday were searching for a missing 11-year-old boy.

Jordan Boyd, 11, was last seen in the Perrysville area of Ross.

Police said he was wearing a black jacket and may be riding a black bicycle with a white trim. Officials said he is 5 feet, 3 inches tall with “short black hair and light skin.”

Authorities asked anyone with information on his whereabouts to contact Special Victims Unit detectives at 412-323-7141.
