Nova Chemicals names Luis Sierra as chief executive

Luis Sierra, a veteran of global energy giant BP, has been named president and chief executive of Nova Chemicals Corp. effective Aug. 1.

He succeeds Todd Karran who is retiring.

Nova, which makes plastic resins and foam materials, has about 500 employees in the Pittsburgh region at its executive center in Moon and a plant in Monaca, Beaver County.

Most of its manufacturing operations are in Canada.
