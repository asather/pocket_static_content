Motorcyclist flown to hospital after crash in Westmoreland County

A motorcyclist was flown to the hospital after a crash in Westmoreland County late Friday.

County emergency officials said the crash was reported at 11:05 p.m. and occurred at Routes 66 and 356 in Washington Township.

The motorcyclist suffered serious injuries; his condition has not been released.

No other vehicles were involved in the crash. The cause is under investigation.
