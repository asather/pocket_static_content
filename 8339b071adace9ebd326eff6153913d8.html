Man in critical condition after Hill District Z-Trip shooting

One man is in critical condition Wednesday night after being found unresponsive in a car with a gunshot wound in the Hill District.

Police responded to a 911 call in the 3900 block of Bigelow Blvd. around 7:10 p.m which stated a man had been shot, according to a release.

Police and EMS arrived to find a car with a broken rear window and an unresponsive man in the back seat. The driver told police he heard gunfire and glass breaking when he was on the 2500 block of Bedford Ave. and continued driving until he reached a safe location along Bigelow Blvd.
