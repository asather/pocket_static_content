Second resident at Concordia senior home dead after testing positive for COVID-19

A second resident at a Butler nursing home have died after testing positive for COVID-19, the facility announced Saturday.

A resident at Concordia at the Orchard died on Friday, officials from Concordia Lutheran Ministries said. Two other people at the home have also tested positive for the novel coronavirus.
