Consider all the issues before voting

I read the front-page story about a farmer from Lawrence County who says he will not vote for President Donald Trump (Aug. 15, “Volant Farmer to Speak at DNC on Why He’s Switching to Biden”).

He will not vote for Mr. Trump because he feels Mr. Trump’s economic policies vis-a-vis China have hurt his profit line. Wow. This man cares only about his own personal profit and never considers all the other issues present in this campaign.

That is the ultimate act of selfishness.
