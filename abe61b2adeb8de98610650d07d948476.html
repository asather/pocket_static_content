Police investigate death of 3-year-old girl found in Oakmont

Allegheny County police are investigating the death of a 3-year-old girl Tuesday.

Around 1:54 p.m., County 911 was alerted to an unresponsive girl on the 500 block of 10th Street in Oakmont.

First responders found the child was not breathing and initiated live-saving measures. The girl was rushed to an area hospital, where she was pronounced dead.

Homicide detectives are investigating.

Anyone with information on the incident is asked to call the county police tip line at 1-833-ALL-TIPS. Callers can remain anonymous.
