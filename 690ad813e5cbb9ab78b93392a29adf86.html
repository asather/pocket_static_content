Passenger in critical condition following Uptown crash

A vehicle passenger has been hospitalized in critical condition after a crash on the Uptown side of the Birmingham Bridge early Thursday.

The crash was reported around 2 a.m. on Fifth Avenue at Kirkpatrick Street, according to Allegheny County emergency officials.

Pittsburgh police said a vehicle carrying three people struck a utility pole. A rear-seat passenger suffered “head trauma” and was taken to the hospital in critical condition, according to police.

The driver and front-seat passenger were not injured.

Police continue to investigate what caused the crash.
