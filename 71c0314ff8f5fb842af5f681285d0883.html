Police: Man hospitalized in Hill District shooting

A man was hospitalized early Friday in a shooting that occurred in the Hill District.

Police responded around 3:40 a.m. to the 20 block of Trent Street for reports of a man shot, according to Pittsburgh police spokeswoman Cara Cruz. 

Authorities found the man in his car with a gunshot wound to the leg, police said.

He was taken to the hospital in stable condition. No arrests have been made.

Police are investigating.
