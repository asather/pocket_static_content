Two seriously injured when SUV crashes into East Huntingdon house

Two people were seriously injured when the SUV they were riding in veered off a road and crashed through a fence and into a brick house in East Huntingdon Tuesday night.

The incident happened shortly after 6 p.m. in the area of Frick Avenue and West Overton Road.

Both were taken to Excela Health Westmoreland Hospital in Greensburg.

Police are investigating the incident.
