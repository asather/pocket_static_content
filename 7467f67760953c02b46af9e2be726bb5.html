Man fatally shot in Wilkinsburg while walking with 5-year-old relative

A 23-year-old man was fatally shot Monday afternoon in Wilkinsburg as he pulled a 5-year-old relative out of the way of gunfire.

Emergency crews responded shortly after 1 p.m. to the intersection of Laketon Road and Winston Street, Allegheny County police said.

Investigators discovered that the victim had been walking on Winston Street with the 5-year-old child when a white four-door sedan approached them.

A black male wearing a brown beanie hat with short dreadlocks exited the car and shot the victim multiple times. The gunman then got back into the vehicle, which headed toward Penn Hills, according to police.

Police said the 23-year-old man was shot while pulling his relative away from the gunfire. He was taken to a hospital where he died.

The 5-year-old sustained an undisclosed injury and was assisted by two people before emergency crews arrived.

Police asked anyone with information about the incident to call them at 1-833-255-8477; callers can remain anonymous. The department can also be reached via its social media sites.
