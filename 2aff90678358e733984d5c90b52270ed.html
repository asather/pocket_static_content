Fort Ligonier Days festival canceled due to COVID-19

Organizers of the Fort Ligonier Days festival have canceled this year's event due to concerns of the COVID-19 pandemic.

The festival is held annually in October at various locations in Ligonier, Westmoreland County.

Organizers announced the decision Wednesday night.
