ATM machine stolen from Ross gas station, police said

Police are searching for suspects involved in the robbery of an ATM machine from a BP gas station in Ross early Wednesday.

Authorities responded around 1:30 a.m. to Lowries Run Road for reports that an undisclosed number of people had broken into the gas station and stole the ATM machine, according to Ross police detective Sgt. Brian Kohlhepp.

Sgt. Kohlhepp said the suspects used a stolen SUV — a  white 2011 Dodge Nitro with Ohio plate FHD2691 — in the robbery. The vehicle was reported stolen in Lake County in Ohio on July 2, Sgt. Kohlhepp said.

No other information was immediately available.
