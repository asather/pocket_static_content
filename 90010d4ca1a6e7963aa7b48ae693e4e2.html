Pittsburgh sets trick-or-treat hours with new protocols

The city of Pittsburgh announced on Monday that there will be trick-or-treating hours on Halloween this year, but also emphasized following safety protocols against spreading COVID-19.

The official trick-or-treating hours will be from 5-7 p.m. on Saturday, Oct. 31. Officials noted, though, that behaviors for those participating will change this year due to new guidelines issued by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.
