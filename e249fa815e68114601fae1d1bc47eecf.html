Cemetery the setting for Easter service in Bridgeville

It was a service for the living and the dead.

The Bethany Presbyterian Church hosted a sunrise Easter Service on Sunday in Melrose Cemetery in Bridgeville.

With tombstones serving as a backdrop, people pulled up in their cars as others — while keeping their social distance — listened from lawnchairs to hear the Rev. Bill Youmans lead the service.
