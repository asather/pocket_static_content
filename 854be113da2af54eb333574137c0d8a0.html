People on the move: 8/24/2020

Association & Organization

Jessica J. Lemmon of Cart/Horse Distilling was elected to the board of The American Craft Spirits Association.

Banking

Autumn C. Patterson joined Mars Bank as assistant vice president and manager of the Penn Banking Center.

Biotechnology

NeuBase Therapeutics Inc. appointed William Mann chief operating officer.

Engineering

McKim & Creed Inc. hired Thomas R. Rock as mid-Atlantic subsurface utility engineering manager. 

Health

Presbyterian SeniorCare hired Todd R. Boslau as chief financial officer.

Insurance

Henderson Brothers hired Julia Painter and Jessica McMahon as business associates in its commercial lines department.

Nonprofit

Gina Dessources was named to the board of Functional Literacy Ministry of Haiti.

Carnegie Science Center appointed Lisa Herrmann senior director of STEM education and Marcus Harshaw senior director of museum experiences.

Dennis Cronin joined the board of Pancreatic Cancer Action Network. Mr. Cronin is senior vice president treasury service and chief risk officer at Highmark Health.

The United Way of Washington County named officers and members to its board for fiscal year 2020-2021. Chair: Cindy Dorazio, assistant vice president/training manager, Washington Financial Bank; treasurer: Gary Kissinger, partner, Palermo/Kissinger & Associates PC; secretary: Janet McQuaid, senior counsel, Babst Calland; vice chair: Ted Gallagher, resource development assistant general counsel, NiSource Inc./Columbia Gas of Pa. Inc.; vice chair: Harlan G. Shober Jr., Community Services Community Leader/Past Washington County Commissioner; vice chair: C. Joseph Willebrand, administration president, Sta-Pure Systems LLC; past chair: Jim Lyle, senior vice president market executive – W.Va. and Ohio, Community Bank; James Cannon, regional Exterior Affairs, First Energy/West Penn Power; Danielle Cornell, director of logistics & international sales, AccuTrex Products Inc.; Craig Cowieson, project operations-project cost, Songer Services Inc; Sheila Cowieson Sr., vice president/market executive, Community Bank; Natalie Gloady, digital strategy manager, Reimagine Main Street, Observer-Reporter; Tiffani Gottschall, associate professor, Washington & Jefferson College; Tom Loadman, retired, Koppers; Drew Medva, global program manager, UPS; Pam Selker Rak, president, CommuniTech LLC; Justin Rubenstein, attorney, Steptoe & Johnson PLLC; Lori Szallar, vice president/human resource manager, Washington Financial Bank; Jeff Walentosky, president, Moody and Associates Inc.; and Ruth Anne Williams, office manager, Northwest Bank.

Wealth Management

Financial firm Baird said The Fischetti Barry Family Wealth Planning Group has joined. Formerly with Morgan Stanley, the team includes Steve Fischetti, branch manager, director and financial adviser; C. Shannon Barry, senior vice president and financial adviser; and Jill S. Frye and Alexander Fischetti, client assistants.

Business notes

Claitman Engineering Associates Inc. has acquired Lovorn Engineering Associates. The Claitman Engineering Associates staff will be located at 700 Blaw Avenue, Blawnox, as of Sept. 1.

Transfinder Corp. said Pittsburgh Public Schools has selected its Routefinder solution to create routes and improve its transportation department’s logistics.

Lockton opened an 8,000-square-foot, Class A commercial office space at One Oxford Centre.
