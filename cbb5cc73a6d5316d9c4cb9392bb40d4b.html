MLB Network skipped the Pirates during its schedule release show, and fans weren't happy

Major League Baseball released its schedule Monday for its shortened 60-game season, but you may not know that if you were a Pirates fan.

MLB Network decided not to show the Pirates’ schedule reveal during its televised event, which did not go unnoticed by Pirates fans and media.
