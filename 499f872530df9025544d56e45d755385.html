Two in critical condition after Wilkinsburg shooting

Two people were sent to the hospital in critical condition on Thursday after a shooting in Wilkinsburg.

Police responded to the shooting on the 1000 block of Princeton Boulevard around 9:52 p.m. First responders found two victims and transported them to the hospital.

Allegheny County homicide detectives are investigating.
