Person critically hurt after being hit by train in South Side

A male is in critical condition after being struck by a train Sunday in the South Side.

Pittsburgh police said the male, whose identity and age have not been released, was struck around 2:55 a.m. behind S. 13th and Breed streets.

Police said he had a wound to his head.

He was taken to the hospital by first responders.
