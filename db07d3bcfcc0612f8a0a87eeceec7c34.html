Pittsburgh Parking Authority resumes street enforcement

Beginning on Tuesday, Sept. 1 the Pittsburgh Parking Authority will resume enforcement of all residential parking permits.

Parking enforcement Downtown had resumed on May 20, when Allegheny County moved into the yellow phase, and will now be enforced in all zones, city wide.

All parking violations will now be enforced, except for the street cleaning program, which is set to resume in April 2021.
