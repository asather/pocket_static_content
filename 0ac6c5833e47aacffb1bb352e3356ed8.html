Man critically injured in Mt. Oliver shooting

A man is in critical condition after being shot multiple times Monday in Mt. Oliver.

Allegheny County police said the shooting was reported around 3:40 p.m. in the 200 block of Anthony Street and found the victim in the 100 block of Church Street. Police said he had gunshot wounds “to the lower body.”

The man, whose identity has not been released, was taken to a hospital.

Anyone with information is asked to call the County Police Tip Line 1-833-ALL-TIPS. Callers can remain anonymous.
