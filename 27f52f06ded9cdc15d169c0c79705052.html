One person shot multiple times in Uptown

A man was shot in the chest and leg early Tuesday morning in the Uptown neighborhood of Pittsburgh.

Pittsburgh Police responded to a shooting around 2:47 a.m. in the 100 block of Dinwiddie Street for a man shot multiple times.

He was transported to a local hospital in critical condition.

No arrests were made. Police continue to investigate.
