U.S. Marshals in Philly arrest suspect in Homewood double homicide

A McKeesport man who is charged in a double homicide in Homewood in November was arrested Monday morning in Philadelphia by the U.S. Marshals Service.

Darryl Barnett, 27, is charged in the shooting deaths of Kierra Eddisha Harris, 26, of McKees Rocks and Lavon Sizemore, 29, of Swissvale.

Pittsburgh police said both were fatally shot Nov. 13 at Kelly and Collier streets.

Officers found Mr. Sizemore on the ground next to a vehicle. Ms. Harris was found nearby in the street with at least one gunshot wound, police said.

Barnett is charged with two counts of homicide and two gun crimes. Court records indicate that he was charged two days after the shootings.
