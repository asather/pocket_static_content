Police search for Latrobe man allegedly found stripping in vehicle

State police on Wednesday are searching for a suspect who fled police after reportedly removing his clothes in his vehicle.

Police responded to reports of a suspicious man believed to be removing his clothes while inside his vehicle on Andrew Lane in Unity, Westmoreland County. Authorities identified the suspect as John Mekic, 34, of Latrobe.

State troopers chased Mekic as he fled the vehicle without clothing.

Officials asked anyone with details of the incident to call 911.
