Tanger Outlets to close through March 27 due to COVID-19 concerns

Folks who do venture out during the next couple of weeks will have a few less places to shop.

Tanger Outlets announced Monday it will close its outlets in Pittsburgh, Lancaster and Hershey through, at least, March 27.

Tanger said it will continue to “monitor developments and communicate changes as they occur.”

It said changes to center hours will be posted on tangeroutlets.com.
