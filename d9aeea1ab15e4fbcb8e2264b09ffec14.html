Celine Dion postpones Pittsburgh concert due to cold

Celine Dion is postponing her tour stop Friday in Pittsburgh because of a cold.

PPG Paints Arena officials announced the cancellation Tuesday night. 

Her “Courage World Tour” show has been rescheduled to Nov. 18, 2020. 

“On Monday night, a day after completing a six-show run in the New York area, Celine began feeling the symptoms of a common cold. The symptoms persisted into Tuesday, and her doctors instructed her to rest for the next 5-7 days. After testing her, the doctors concluded that her virus was not related to COVID-19,” a news release said.

Ms. Dion also canceled her Wednesday show in Washington D.C. Her tour is expected to resume March 24 in Denver.

Ticket holders of the postponed show will receive more information.
