McKeesport Sister of late City Council member will finish term

The sister of a late McKeesport City Council member will serve the remainder of her term.

Jill Lape was appointed to fill the council seat once occupied by her sister, Jamie Brewster-Filotei, 46, who died of cancer March 12.

Council voted to appoint Ms. Lape at its June 3 meeting.

Ms. Lape is a teacher at Twin Rivers Intermediate School in the McKeesport Area School District. Her term expires at the end of 2021.

In other matters, council approved the application for Coronavirus Emergency Supplemental Funding through the U.S. Department of Justice and office of Justice Programs.

If McKeesport is approved for the funding it would be used for assistance in preventing, preparing for and responding to the coronavirus.

The government website says funding would include but is not limited to: overtime, equipment such as law enforcement and personal protective equipment, hiring, and purchasing supplies such as sanitizer, masks and gloves.
