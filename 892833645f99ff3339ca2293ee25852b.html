Man shot in McKeesport

A man was hospitalized Thursday night after being shot in McKeesport, authorities said.

McKeesport police confirmed the man was shot on the 1700 block of Soles Street. Authorities said there are currently no suspects in custody.

Authorities said they could give no further comment.

Allegheny County dispatch said the shooting was reported at 8:25 p.m.
