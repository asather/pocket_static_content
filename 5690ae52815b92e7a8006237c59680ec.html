Allegiant to launch nonstop flights to Memphis, Nashville and Norfolk in May

Allegiant Airlines is launching three new seasonal routes from Pittsburgh International Airport.

Twice-weekly nonstop flights to Memphis, Tenn., and Norfolk, Va., will start May 21. A twice-weekly flight to Nashville, Tenn., will begin May 22.

Memphis and Norfolk currently are not served from Pittsburgh International.

Southwest Airlines flies two times a day to Nashville.

Flight dates and times are available on the Allegiant website.
