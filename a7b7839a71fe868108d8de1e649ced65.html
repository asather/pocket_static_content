2 hurt in fall from roof in East Liberty

Two men were hospitalized Thursday morning in critical condition after falling three stories from the roof of a home in East Liberty, according to Pittsburgh police.

Both adults — one a resident of the home and the other a friend — were doing repairs on the roof in the 600 block of Mellon Street when they fell, Pittsburgh police spokesman Maurice Matthews said.

Emergency officials responded around 11:15 a.m. to the accident.

"Once they arrive at the hospital the doctors will be able to establish the severity their injuries," Mr. Matthews said.

One of the unidentified men may have suffered a back injury, he added.
