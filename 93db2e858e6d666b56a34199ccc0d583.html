State police searching for escaped inmate in Pittsburgh

State police are searching for a woman who signed out of a Pittsburgh halfway house on Wednesday and never returned.

Amber Ellifritz was being held in Pittsburgh Community Corrections Center, a medium security state prison in Shadyside, when she signed out and never came back, according to a tweet sent out by state police Troop B late Thursday evening.

Ellifritz is currently serving time for access device fraud, a crime that involves obtaining or trying to obtain another person’s property, money or services without their permission, by using an access device that is counterfeit, unauthorized, or revoked. 

Police have issued an escape warrant.

If you have any information on Ellifritz’s whereabouts, police ask that you contact them at 412-299-1607.
