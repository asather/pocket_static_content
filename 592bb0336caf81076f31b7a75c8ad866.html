2 found dead inside Upper St. Clair home after police receive threatening call

Two bodies — a man and woman — were found inside a home Monday in Upper St. Clair, and the township’s police chief called it a “domestic-related homicide.”

Allegheny County police said they received a call from the home around 10:10 a.m. from a man who claimed he had just killed his wife and intended to kill himself.

When SWAT team member entered the home on Thousand Oaks Drive, they found the man, 67, and his 54-year-old wife dead.

County police said “the couple was recently estranged, and the woman returned to the residence for personal effects when she was shot and killed.”

Police never reported hearing gunfire and believe the man took his own life after placing the phone call.

No other information was immediately available.
