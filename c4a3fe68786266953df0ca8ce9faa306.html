Tractor-trailer overturns on Streets Run Road

A portion of Streets Run Road in West Mifflin is closed Tuesday due to a truck crash, PennDOT said.

The closure is between Delwar and Prospect roads.

KDKA-TV, the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette's news partner, said a tractor-trailer carrying steel overturned near a set of railroad tracks just before noon and began leaking diesel fuel.

The crash happened in the 1600 block of Streets Run Road, which is the border of Baldwin Borough and West Mifflin.

Rail traffic has been temporarily suspended, and no one was injured, KDKA said.
