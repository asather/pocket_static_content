The amphitheater in Burgettstown, which some Pittsburghers still call Star Lake, has a new name for the 2020 season.

It is changing from KeyBank Pavilion to the S&T Bank Music Park.

This is now the fifth name for the venue, which started in 1990 as the Coca-Cola Star Lake Amphitheater, and then became the Post-Gazette Pavilion, First Niagara Pavilion and KeyBank Pavilion.
