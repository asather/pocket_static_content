Bridge inspections to affect traffic on I-376, Boulevard of the Allies

Drivers can expect traffic restrictions Friday on I-376 and the Boulevard of the Allies near the Birmingham Bridge while PennDOT conducts bridge inspections.

Traffic will be reduced to single lanes and shoulders will be restricted in the both directions on the Boulevard of the Allies/Route 885, I-376/Parkway East and ramps in the area of the Birmingham Bridge. 

Roadwork will occur on weekdays from 9 a.m.to 3 p.m. and over the weekend from 6 a.m. to noon until Jan. 16.

Crews from the Pittsburgh-based JMT Engineering, the New York City-based WSP and the Clinton, Pa.-based Sofis Rigging Company will conduct the inspection work.
