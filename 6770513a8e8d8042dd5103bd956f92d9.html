Live Nation offers $69 Ticket to Rock

Live Nation is offering four metal shows for $69 in the 2020 Ticket to Rock.

The four S&T Bank Music Park shows are Breaking Benjamin, Disturbed, Korn/Faith No More and Megadeth/Lamb of God.

The Ticket to Rock goes on sale at noon Tuesday at Livenation.com.

Here are the dates:

June 28: Megadeth/Lamb of God/Trivium/In Flames

July 29: Breaking Benjamin

Aug. 13: Disturbed/Staind/Bad Wolves

Sept. 5: Korn/Faith No More
