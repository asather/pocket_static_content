Man in critical condition after crashing into wall on Forbes Avenue

A man was in critical condition after a Saturday morning crash on Forbes Avenue in Uptown.

Pittsburgh police said they arrived at the scene of the single-vehicle collision around 8:11 a.m.

The man had crashed into a wall at Forbes Avenue after exiting the Liberty Bridge. Firefighters had to remove him from the car, and he was taken to the hospital in critical condition, according to police. 

No one else was in the car.

Police continue to investigate.
