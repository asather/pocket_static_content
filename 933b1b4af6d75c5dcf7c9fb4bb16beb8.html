Person walks into hospital with gunshot wound, later dies

Police are investigating after a person died after walking into a hospital with a gunshot wound.

Post-Gazette news partner KDKA reported officials responded around 7 p.m. Wednesday to a ShotSpotter alert for the 500 block of Norton Street in Mount Washington.

Pittsburgh Police on the scene learned a vehicle was hit by a bullet, KDKA said.

Officials learned later that a person walked into a nearby hospital with a gunshot wound.

The victim died after arriving, police said.
