Janet Jackson and Megadeth/Lamb of God are coming -- not together

Two more, very different concerts have been added to the summer lineup:

Janet Jackson will bring the Black Diamond World Tour to the PPG Paints Arena on July 10, supporting her forthcoming album, “Black Diamond.” Tickets go on sale at noon Feb. 13 at Livenation.com.

Megadeth will team up with the much heavier Lamb of God, plus Trivium and In Flames. on June 28 at S&T Bank Music Park. Tickets go on sale at 11 a.m. Friday at Livenation.com. Citi cardmembers will have access to purchase presale tickets & VIP packages beginning Tuesday at 10 a.m. through Thursday at 10 p.m. at www.citientertainment.com.
