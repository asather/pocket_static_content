TRAFFIC: Tractor-trailer crash shuts down I-70 EB in South Strabane

A tractor-trailer crash blocked the eastbound lanes of Interstate 70 in Washington County Friday morning.

No injuries were reported and no other vehicles were involved in the crash, state police said.

The crash was reported shortly before 6 a.m. east of the I-79 interchange in South Strabane.

The cause of the crash is under investigation.

Traffic was being detoured onto I-79 south then taking the next exit to return to the highway.
