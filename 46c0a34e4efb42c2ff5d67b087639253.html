Brentwood 'gun battle' under investigation

Police are investigating a “gun battle” that occurred Thursday in Brentwood.

Allegheny County police said there were no reports of injuries, but there was evidence of a battle found after first responders arrived around 11:17 a.m. to the 3100 block of Clermont Avenue, an area behind Concordia Lutheran Church.

Police said a tan-colored sedan, which was occupied by two males, was seen fleeing the scene

Anyone with information concerning this incident is asked to call the Allegheny County Police Tipline at 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477). Callers can remain anonymous.
