Police investigating Edgewood robbery

A robber broke through a window late Friday and fled an Edgewood business with stolen cash after a clerk electronically locked the door. 

City police responded at 11:40 p.m. to the 300 block of Craft Street, where a store clerk reported that a bearded male wearing a Steeler jacket attempted to reach inside the cash register after it was opened.

The man jumped over the counter, assaulted the clerk and grabbed cash. But before he could flee the business, the clerk locked the door, prompting the man to break a window and escape in an unknown direction.

Police are investigating.
