Allegheny County COVID-19 cases up to 876 with 3 new deaths

 

There are 19 new cases of COVID-19 in Allegheny County and three new deaths, according to the latest data released Monday.

The new cases push the county totals to 876 and 21, up from Sunday’s figures of 857 and 19.

Of the deaths, all were over the age of 65, the county said.

Countywide, since the first case was confirmed on March 11, there have been 134 hospitalizations due to COVID-19. The county said it has administered 10,056 tests.

In Pennsylvania, the number of positive cases reported Monday rose to 24,199 from 22,833 on Sunday, a difference of 1,366. There are 524 deaths blamed on COVID-19 in the state, up from 507.
