Woman, man killed in Washington County crash

A driver and her passenger were killed early Wednesday when the driver lost control of her vehicle in Washington County.

Pennsylvania State Police said Kacey Coen Shuba, 20, and Garrett E. Delvecchio, 21, both of Washington, Pa., died when Shuba lost control of her car and struck a tree near Paxton Farm Road in Chartiers Township.

Police said the crash occurred some time between 1 a.m. and 5 a.m. They were both pronounced dead at the scene around 6 a.m.

The crash remains under investigation.
