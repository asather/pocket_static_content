1 killed, 2 injured after car crashes into convenience store

A woman was killed and two others were injured when the car they were traveling in crashed into a CoGo’s convenience store early Sunday in Middlesex, according to KDKA-TV, the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette’s news partner.

Witnesses told police that the car drove from Dwellington Road across Route 8, went airborne, landed in the parking lot and crashed into the store, which was closed at the time.

One of the passengers was killed. The two other women, including the driver, were trapped and then taken to hospitals — one in serious condition and the other in critical.

The incident happened around 1 a.m.

 

Police have not provided a cause for the accident.
