Man injured in East HIlls shooting

A man was transported to an area hospital after he was shot in the 8400 block of Frankstown Road, according to Post-Gazette news partner KDKA.

According to police, officers were called to Frankstown Road just after midnight after they were alerted to a ShotSpotter reporting over 30 shots fired.

Officers found a man several blocks away at the intersection of Bennett and Oakwood streets. He was taken to the hospital in critical condition.

Crime scene units located multiple shell casings in the 8400 block of Frankstown Road.

Anyone with information to call police at 412-323-7800 while they continue to investigate.

This story will be updated.
