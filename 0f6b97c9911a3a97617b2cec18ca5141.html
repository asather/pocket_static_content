Police seeking man accused of throwing bricks during June 1 protests

A task force investigating recent protests is searching for a man accused of throwing bricks at police during demonstrations in East Liberty last month.

The Damage Assessment and Accountability Task Force, made up of made up of local and federal authorities, is asking for the public’s help in identifying a man they say threw concrete at police officers during a June 1 protest near Centre and Negley avenues.

In images released to the media, the unidentified man is wearing a camouflage baseball cap, a “Curators of Hip Hop” T-shirt and Nike Air Max shoes, according to Pittsburgh police spokeswoman Cara Cruz.

Anyone with information is asked to email the task force at DAAT@Pittsburghpa.gov or call 412-323-7800 and ask for the task force.
