Police: Missing Pittsburgh man last seen in the South Hills

Pittsburgh police are searching for a missing city man who was last seen in the South Hills.

Police said Jordan Coles, 29, was last seen on Sunday.

He is described as 5 feet 10 inches tall with black and blond hair and brown eyes.

Anyone with information on his whereabouts is asked to call the Special Victims Unit at (412) 323-7141.
