Good fishing despite travel restrictions

Allegheny County’s Deer Creek gave up a 25-inch, 8-pound brown trout caught on spinning gear with a gel-treated jig and released by James Swearingen of Windgap. This year the state Fish and Boat Commission stocked more big breeders that were retired from the hatchery system.
