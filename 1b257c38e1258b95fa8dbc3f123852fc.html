Last chance to enter Trout Photo Contest

Annie Betler of Harrisville, Butler County, pulled an 18-inch rainbow trout out of the private pond at Grove City Sportsmen’s Club.

Her entry, photographed by Sam Uminski of Fox Chapel, made it in a few days before the deadline in this year’s Post Gazette Trout Photo Contest. High-resolution jpgs (1 MB minimum) should be emailed to fishingreport@post-gazette.com by Sunday. Include the angler’s name, age (for minors), town, species, size, body of water, details of the catch and phone number (not for publication), as well as the photographer’s name and town. Winners in the Big Fish, Kidstuff and Artsy Shots categories will be interviewed for web and print publication May 24.
