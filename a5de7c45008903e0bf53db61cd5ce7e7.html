Police: 5-year-old hospitalized in East Hills hit-and-run

Police on Sunday said they are investigating a hit-and-run after a 5-year-old boy was struck by a vehicle that fled the scene. 

In a release, authorities said the boy was hit by a grey sedan on the 2100 block of Park Hill Drive in the East Hills neighborhood.

When police and EMS arrived around 5 p.m, the boy was “conscious, alert and complaining of leg pain,” the release reads.

Police said the boy was transported to Children’s Hospital and is in stable condition.
