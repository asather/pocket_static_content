Mathias Barnwell, 15-year-old tight end, commits to Penn State

Mathias Barnwell had plenty of time needing to decide where he would play college football, but the 15-year-old is getting it out of the way early.

The Spotsylvania, Va., native announced via Twitter on Monday that he’s committing to Penn State. The 6-foot-5 tight end is first recruit committed to Penn State’s class of 2023. He held 15 offers and picked the Nittany Lions over schools including Pitt, USC, Virginia Tech, Nebraska and Tennessee.

Barnwell attended the Michigan game at Beaver Stadium in 2019. A few days later, Penn State offered him a scholarship.

Barnwell’s impact on his Spotsylvania program was immediate. He had 10 catches for 154 yards as a freshman season and also converted six two-point conversions.
