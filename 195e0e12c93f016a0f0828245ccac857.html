Woman and daughter hurt in hit-and-run crash in Fineview

A woman and her 4-year-old daughter, both pedestrians, were injured Saturday night in a hit-and-run crash in Fineview.

The incident, which is under investigation by the Pittsburgh police, occurred at about 11:45 p.m.

Officers arrived at the intersection of Belleau Drive and Belleau Street found the two alert and conscious. Emergency services transported them to a hospital in stable condition with undisclosed injuries.

No vehicle description was available.
