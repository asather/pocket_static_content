One person hospitalized after bus collides with vehicle in Carrick

One person has been hospitalized Thursday night after a Port Authority bus crashed into a vehicle in Carrick.

County dispatchers said they received a report of a collision at Brownsville Road and Biscayne Drive around 10:10 p.m.

One person was transported to the hospital, but it dispatchers were unsure if it was someone from the bus or a from the other vehicle.

Police and EMS cleared the scene by 10:30 p.m.
