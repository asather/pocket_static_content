Students briefly evacuated from Quaker Valley Middle School

Students were evacuated briefly from Quaker Valley Middle School in Sewickley Tuesday morning after a strange odor was reported by staff in the building.

District officials said the odor was detected near the nurse’s office around 9 a.m. 

The fire department arrived on the scene, and school maintenance staff and technicians from the natural gas company investigated the source and determined nothing hazardous was in the building. School officials said the odor was “not linked to the presence of gas in the building.”

Students were relocated to St. Andrew’s Church in Sewickley, and were allowed to return back to the middle school around 10:30 a.m.
