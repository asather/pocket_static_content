Water main break closes part of Mifflin Road in Lincoln Place

 

Crews were called out to the scene of a water main break along Mifflin Road in Lincoln Place, according to Pittsburgh Post-Gazette news partner KDKA.

Crews were called just before 1 a.m. to block off the road between Mohrbach Street and Buttermilk Hollow Road. The break may have damaged part of the road, KDKA said.

The water also had to be shut off.

Officials did not disclose how long the section of the road will be closed.
