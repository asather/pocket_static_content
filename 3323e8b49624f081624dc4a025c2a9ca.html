17-year-old in critical condition after shooting in Duquesne

Allegheny County Police homicide detectives on Friday are investigating a shooting in Duquesne that left a 17-year-old male in critical condition.

Emergency dispatchers were notified around 6:08 p.m. of a shooting in the 600 block of Duquesne Boulevard. First responders arrived and found a crashed vehicle with four passengers inside.

The 17-year-old male victim in the vehicle had a gunshot wound and was transported to a local hospital in critical condition.

County police requested anyone with information on the incident to call the their tip line at 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477). Callers can remain anonymous.
