Spring Run Road reopens in Crescent

Spring Run Road less than a half mile east of Florence Drive in Crescent is reopened to traffic.

A landslide on Feb. 25 lead to closure of the eastbound lane and the entire road was closed May 11 so the county Department of Public Works could construct a rock embankment, improve drainage, rebuild the roadway and replace a guide rail.
