Teen shot in Spring Hill neighborhood

A teenager was taken to a hospital after a shooting in Spring Hill on Friday morning.

Pittsburgh Police and EMS responded to a 9-1-1 call of a male shot on the 100 block of Rhine Place at around 7:20 a.m.

Officers found the 14-year-old victim, who was conscious and alert, with a gunshot wound to his leg. The victim said he was shot in a stairwell, public safety officials said.

Medics then transported the teenager to a hospital in stable condition.

Officials say that there were no other victims or suspect descriptions, but that the investigation is ongoing.
