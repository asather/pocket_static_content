Four injured in North Sewickley Township crash

Beaver County dispatch reported four people were transported to hospitals following a vehicle crash in North Sewickley Township. 

The accident occurred at 5:51 p.m. at the intersection of Wises Grove and Dogwood Drive, according to Beaver County dispatch two of the injured were transported to UPMC Presbyterian Hospital in Oakland, one was taken to Allegheny General Hospital on the North Side and the other was taken to a local hospital, according to Beaver County dispatch. 

The extent of the injuries are unknown at this time.
