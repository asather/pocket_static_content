Fatal house fire claims two in Duquesne

A man and a woman are dead after an early morning house fire in Duquesne.

First responders received the call shorty after midnight to the home on the 1100 block of Karl Avenue. 

Heavy, black smoke could be seen coming from the back of the 2 1/2 story brick structure, according to Pittsburgh Post-Gazette news partner KDKA.

Allegheny County Police and multiple fire companies are on the scene.

The fire marshal is investigating.
