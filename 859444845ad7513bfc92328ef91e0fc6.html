John Cusack will hit Munhall on 'Say Anything' tour

John Cusack will appear at the Carnegie of Homestead Music Hall on April 23 holding a boombox high above his head.

OK, we can’t guarantee that he’ll actually do that, but he might while appearing along with his 1989 classic “Say Anything.”

Following a screening of the teen romantic comedy, directed by Cameron Crowe, the Hollywood star and Twitter pundit will share stories about his career and the breakout role as Lloyd Dobler and also do an audience Q&A.

Tickets are $49.75, $59.75 and $74.75 and go on sale at 10 a.m. Friday. Gold Circle is $150 (all Gold Circle seating includes a post-show photo op with John Cusack, limited quantity). Info at druskyentertainment.com.
