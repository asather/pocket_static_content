Police: Man hospitalized after Homewood shooting

Police are investigating after a man was shot Monday night in Homewood.

Authorities said they responded around 11:50 p.m. to the 7000 block of Frankstown Avenue for reports of a shooting and a possible gunshot victim. Police found a man lying on the ground in a parking lot near a business, according to Pittsburgh police spokeswoman Cara Cruz.

The man was conscious, police said, and suffering a gunshot wound to his lower right leg. He was taken to a hospital in stable condition.

Further information was not immediately available.
