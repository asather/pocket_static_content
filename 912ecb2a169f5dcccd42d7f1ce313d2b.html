Woman critical after being hit by vehicle in Downtown

A woman is in critical condition Thursday after she was struck by a vehicle in Downtown.

The crash happened around 1:50 p.m. at Forbes Avenue and Grant Street.

The woman was taken to a hospital. Police said the driver remained at the scene.

During the investigation, authorities closed Forbes Avenue between Grant Street and Scrip Way, according to a release.
