Code Orange will go 'Under the Skin' with livestream on July 30

Code Orange, who pioneered the empty-venue livestream when COVID-19 shut things down in mid-March, will be back at it again on July 30 with a new livestream called “Under the Skin.”

The Pittsburgh hardcore / metal band, known for its crushing live shows, will do a more stripped-down set from The Theatre Factory in Trafford, broadcast via the Code Orange Twitch channel.

They describe it as “conceptually akin to classic MTV Unplugged performances, but with nightmarish digital twists and turns.”

Code Orange, currently promoting the acclaimed new album “Underneath,” teased the stream with an animated video piece Monday night following their bi-weekly Twitch livestream series, “YOU AND YOU ALONE.” Exclusive merch along with a new limited-edition vinyl pressing of “Underneath” will be available during the livestream event via the band’s webstore.
