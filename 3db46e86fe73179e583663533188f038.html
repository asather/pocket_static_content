City drug bust nets an arrest, heroin worth $3,300 and a stolen handgun

Allegheny County Police recovered heroin, cash, a stolen handgun and made one arrest in a drug raid in Hazelwood early Friday morning.

Narcotics detectives served a search warrant at 4713 Monongahela St. in the morning as part of an ongoing investigation into heroin trafficking in the county, an Allegheny County Police news release said.

Inside the house, officers seized 550 stamp bags of heroin and fentanyl, a stolen 9 mm pistol, a small amount of marijuana and cash totaling $7,221. The drugs have a retail value of $3,300, police said in the statement.

Police arrested Derrick Phillips, no age or residence provided, during the raid. Phillips, a felon, was charged with felony possession with intent to deliver heroin/fentanyl, receiving stolen property, person not to possess a firearm, and possession. He is in the Allegheny County Jail pending a preliminary hearing.
