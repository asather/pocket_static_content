WATCH: Steelers’ JuJu Smith-Schuster surprises family with new house

On Saturday, Steelers running back James Conner surprised his mother by buying her a new house. About a month earlier, Conner had gotten his father a brand-new truck.

Current and form Pittsburgh athletes have been showing their families love in the form of expensive gifts quite a lot lately, from Pirates pitcher Chris Archer getting his parents two cars for Christmas to former Pitt running back Qadree Ollison purchasing his dad a house for Father’s Day.

Enter Steelers receiver JuJu Smith-Schuster, who himself is no stranger to publicly expressing his appreciation for his family in the form of lavish presents.

In September, Smith-Schuster bought a truck for his stepfather, Lawrence Schuster, a particularly thoughtful gift because he grew up in a one-car family and Lawrence would take the bus to work for the sake of his family’s comfort.

On Monday, he took care of his whole family by getting them a new house. As is usually the case with these videos, expect tears both from those involved and yourself.

Smith-Schuster has one older sister and five younger siblings, many of whom it seems will be living in this house based on the video.

The many sides of Smith-Schuster’s personality have been on display the last few days, from this effort to provide for his family to him putting on a giant fireworks show in the South Side on the Fourth of July.

Whatever you think of him as a person and football player, one thing’s for sure: Smith-Schuster’s content game has always been and continues to be strong.
