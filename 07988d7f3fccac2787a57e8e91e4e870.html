TRAFFIC: Lanes restricted on Noblestown Road in Elliott and West End

Drivers are advised to avoid Noblestown Road between the Elliott and West End neighborhoods.

Traffic on Noblestown Rd. between Weaver Street and Wabash Street is restricted to one alternating lane due to a slide that occurred early Sunday morning, city officials. Drivers should avoid the road due to significant traffic delays, the city said. 

The city has ordered an analysis of the hillside.
