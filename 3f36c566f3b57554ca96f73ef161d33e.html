'Hamilton' postponed again in Pittsburgh

Pittsburgh Cultural Trust has announced the PNC Broadway in Pittsburgh show “Hamilton” has been rescheduled to Feb. 22 - March 13, 2022, at the Benedum Center.

The show was originally scheduled to be in town Jan. 5 - Feb. 7, 2021.

Season ticket holders and varying levels of trust members will have first access to tickets to this performance and will be contacted when more information is available, the trust said on its website.

Tickets will go on sale to the general public at a later date in 2021.

Season tickets will be rescheduled for the same package day and performance time during the week of February 22-27, 2022, the trust said.

Since March 13 and through the end of 2020, almost all Pittsburgh Cultural Trust events have been either postponed, rescheduled or canceled due to threat of COVID-19.

Once the status of an event has been determined, the trust said it will contact ticket buyers with the new information via email.
