Six male students, including two adults and four juveniles, at Summit Academy in Summit, Butler County, were arrested and charged Thursday with assaulting another juvenile male student, state police reported.

The victim in the attack at the private, residential school for at-risk young men, ages 14-19 in grades 9-12, is a 16-year-old from Sacramento, Calif. He was taken to Armstrong Center for Medicine & Health with minor injuries, police reported.

The juveniles charged as his assailants were two 16-year-olds and two 17-year-olds, all from Philadelphia. The adults who were charged include a 19-year-old from Philadelphia and an 18-year-old, whose hometown was not listed by state police on a news release.

The adults were taken to the Butler County Jail. The juveniles will be processed through juvenile probation, police said.
