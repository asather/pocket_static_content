Body found along walking trail in Swisshelm Park

A group of dog walkers discovered the body of a man Wednesday along a trail in Swisshelm Park, according to Pittsburgh police.

The body was found around noon about 400 yards from the end of Goodman Street.

Police said foul play is not suspected.

No other details were immediately available.
