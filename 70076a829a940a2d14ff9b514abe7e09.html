Man critically wounded in Perry South

A man was critically wounded when he was shot multiple times early Saturday in Perry South, Pittsburgh police reported.

The victim, who police did not identify, was shot around 1:40 a.m. in the 300 block of West Burgess Street, police reported. He was taken to a local hospital. Police were alerted by a ShotSpotter notification, they said.

Violent Crime Unit detectives are investigating.
