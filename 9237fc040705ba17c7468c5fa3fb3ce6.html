McKeesport City Council approves street improvements, lighting project for Boys and Girls Club

McKeesport City Council has approved a $230,674 street improvement program that includes the reconstruction of several city streets.

The approval came at a May 5 council meeting at which council voted to award the street improvement program to El Grande Industries of Donora.

Some of the streets set to be reconstructed under the program are: Bailie Avenue from Union to Cornell, Library Avenue from Coursin to Union, Union Avenue from Library to Bailie, and Jenny Lind Street from Union to Converse.

Additionally at the meeting, council approved a contract with Kucich Construction Inc., of McKeesport, in an amount not to exceed $74,600 for improvements to the LaRosa Boys and Girls Club.

The project includes the installation of new exterior LED lighting for the entrance of the club on Ravine Street as well as rehabilitation of the main clubhouse restrooms and exterior lighting.
