It’s easy to find regional fly tying events and activities

There are more trout streams than social fly tying opportunities in southwestern Pennsylvania, but they’re not difficult to find. Demos are often free; classes, clinics and multi-meeting courses usually charge a fee.

See a partial listing below.

Can’t wait for spring trout fishing? Among many good books on fly tying for steelhead trout, check out the updated fourth edition of “Steelhead Guide: Fly Fishing Techniques and Strategies for Lake Erie Steelhead” (Great Lakes Publishing, $29.95) by Pittsburgh author John Nagy.

Allegheny Outdoor, Sport and Travel Show (www.sportandtravel.com), Feb. 14-16, Monroeville Convention Center, 209 Mall Plaza Blvd. Daily introductory fly tying instruction with members of Penn’s Woods West Trout Unlimited.

Days on the Water (www.daysonthewater.com), fly tying clinic runs 9 a.m. Saturdays Feb. 1-April 4. Community College of Allegheny County Boyce Campus, 595 Beatty Road, Monroeville. 412-788-7546.

Fly Tyers Reunion 6 p.m. April 27, Seven Springs Mountain Resort, Sporting Clays Lodge, 777 Waterwheel Drive, Champion, Westmoreland County. Annual tying demo featuring top regional fly tiers. 800-452-2223 ext. 7899.

International Angler, 5275 Steubenville Pike, Robinson. Weekly fly tying classes. Josh Miller Jan. 25, Jake Villwock Feb. 1, Bruce Cox Feb. 8, Shane Hawryliak Feb. 15, Tim Camisa Feb. 22, George Daniel Feb. 29. 412-788-8088.

Orvis Pittsburgh, Galleria Mall, 1500 Washington Road, Mt. Lebanon. 9 a.m. Saturdays free Fly Tying 101 classes through March. 412-343-1612.

Penn’s Woods West Trout Unlimited (pwwtu.org), 7 p.m. second Mondays, Christ Church at Grove Farm, 249 Duff Road, Sewickley. Occasional fly tying demos, meetings free and open to the public. Bar Flies free informal tying meetings 7 p.m. fourth Mondays, Perrytowne Draft House, 1002 Perry Highway, McKnight. Cabin Fever fly fishing expo benefiting PWWTU includes fly tying demos, March 1, Doubletree by Hilton Pittsburgh, 910 Sheraton Drive, Mars.

Project Healing Waters, 7 p.m. fourth Wednesdays, Upper St. Clair Municipal Building, 1770 McLaughlin Run Road. Membership required, 412-877-7864.

Upper St. Clair Fly Fishing Club, 7 p.m. fourth Wednesdays, Upper St. Clair Municipal Building, 1770 McLaughlin Run Road. Free, open to the public. 412-352-9421.
