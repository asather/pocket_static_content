County police investigating fatal shooting in West Mifflin

Allegheny County Police are investigating the shooting of a 20-year-old man Saturday night in West Mifflin as a homicide.

Police said county 911 was notified at 10:20 p.m of a shooting in the 100 block of Palmer Lane. First responders found the victim with gunshot wounds in his chest.

Homicide detectives are now investigating.

Authorities ask anyone with information on this incident to call the county police tip line at 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477).
