Boil water advisory lifted for Aliquippa, Hopewell Township

A boil water advisory in Beaver County was lifted Sunday afternoon.

The advisory, put into effect Feb. 20, affected several areas in Aliquippa and Hopewell, including Upper Brodhead Road, Sheffield Heights, upper and lower Sheffield Terrace and upper Woodlawn Park.
