Woman's body found in McKees Rocks home under 'suspicious' circumstances

Police are investigating the death of a woman under suspicious circumstances in McKees Rocks.

The body of the unidentified woman was found Monday morning inside a home in the 90 block of Helen Street.

Allegheny County homicide detectives are investigating.

Police said 911 was notified of the woman’s body around 9:46 a.m.. “First responders found suspicious circumstances,” county police said in a news release, without elaborating.

Anyone with information can call county police at 1-833-ALL-TIPS.
