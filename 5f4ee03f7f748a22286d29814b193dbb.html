Blink 182 will headline Four Chord Festival in July

Four Chord Music Festival producer Rishi Bahl got the band he’d been wanting ever since he started the pop-punk celebration.

Blink-182 will headline Four Chord Music Festival 7, which moves to Wild Things Park in Washington, Pa., on July 11.

Also on the festival will be The Used, State Champs, Four Year Strong, The Ataris, Hit the Lights, Sleep On It, Eternal Boy, Patent Pending, Keep Flying, Fortune Cove and Look Out Loretta. Gates will open at noon with music starting at 1:30 p.m.

Tickets are $61 and go on sale at 10 a.m. Friday at fourchordmusicfestival.com or at showclix.com. Limited VIP packages will be available for $160. A limited early bird for GA & VIP will begin at the time of announce: $55 for GA, $150 for VIP.

The VIP Packages include early admission to the festival, an exclusive pizza party, VIP T-Shirt, festival poster, access to VIP lounge, VIP parking and special acoustic performances from Four Chord Music Festival artists.

Four Chord almost got Blink-182 last summer, but the band’s tour plans changed when they hooked up with Lil Wayne. Four Chord then booked The Offspring, which had to cancel at the last minute. The event went anyway with the other bands at Highmark Stadium at Station Square.

Wild Things Park is at 1 Washington Federal Way, Washington, PA 15301.

It is presented by 105.9 The X, Four Chord Music & Drusky Entertainment.
