Woman found dead in residence during Beechview fire

A woman was found dead at a residence in Beechview as authorities responded to a fire at the home Tuesday morning, Pittsburgh police said.

The blaze was reported at a two-story home just after 7:30 a.m. in the 1600 block of Dagmar Avenue. Fire officials arrived at the scene and worked to put out the fire. The woman was found dead on the second floor after firefighters extinguished flames upon entering.

A man who lives at the home told authorities he arrived home from work and smelled smoke, but could not make it far into the house due to flames, police said. He was able to save his dog.

City fire officials are investigating the cause of the blaze.
