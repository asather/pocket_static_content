Central Catholic's Rocco Salvitti takes first-round lead at Tri-State Open

Rocco Salvitti, a sophomore-to-be at Central Catholic High School, shot a 5-under 67 to take the first-round lead in the 36-hole Tri-State Open at Williams Country Club in Weirton, W.Va.

Salvitti, who finished sixth in the WPIAL Class AAA championship and seventh in the PIAA Championship, has a one-shot lead on professional Ty Roush of Riverside GC in Mason, W.Va., heading into the final round on Tuesday.

Salvitti is trying to become the third amateur in the past five years to win the Tri-State Open.
