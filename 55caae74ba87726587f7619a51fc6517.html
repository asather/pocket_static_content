Police investigating Downtown assault

Pittsburgh Police are investigating an assault involving two men Sunday afternoon in Downtown.

Pittsburgh Police and EMS responded to 911 call around 2:30 p.m. of a fight on the 300 block of Sixth Avenue.

When Zone 2 officers arrived, they found a conscious male on the sidewalk.

The victim told officers he was assaulted by a male after a confrontation. He had a cut on his head and complained of a leg injury. He was transported to a local hospital in stable condition.

The male suspect was last seen headed towards Smithfield Street.
