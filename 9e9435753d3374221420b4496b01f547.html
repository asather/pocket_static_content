Police: Infant shot in Indiana County

A 4-month-old baby was shot Saturday evening in Indiana County.

State police responded to reports of a shooting at an apartment on College Lodge Road in White Township, according to Pennsylvania State Police in Indiana, Pa. Inside the apartment, police discovered that the infant boy suffered a single gunshot wound in the upper body. 

The child’s parents were at the apartment during the incident and immediately alerted a neighbor, who called 911. The child was initially taken to Indiana Regional Medical Center before being transported to UPMC Children’s Hospital of Pittsburgh.

The boy was last reported in stable condition, police said.

No arrests have been made and state police continue to investigate.
