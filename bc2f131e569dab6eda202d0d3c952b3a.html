Woman, juvenile dead after truck and buggy crash in Venango County

A woman and a juvenile died Sunday afternoon after a log truck collided with an Amish buggy in Venango County, according to officials.

Around 3:05 p.m., a logging truck traveling eastbound on Route 208 in Irwin Township struck the buggy from behind, according to state police. The woman and the juvenile male were pronounced dead at the scene.

The buggy was occupied by a family of five, police said. A man was flown to a hospital in Youngstown, Ohio, and a juvenile girl was flown to Children’s Hospital in Pittsburgh. Another juvenile male was treated and released from Grove City Medical Center.

“The operator of the log truck is cooperating with investigators and the vehicle was secured,” officers from State Police Troop E wrote in the release. 

Emergency Medical Services, fire officials and police officers responded to the scene in the area of 2414 Clintonville Road, dispatchers said. The road was closed for several hours while investigators were on scene.
