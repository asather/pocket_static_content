Allegheny County reports another rise in COVID-19 cases

The Allegheny County Health Department said it has confirmed a case of COVID-19 and has a report of another presumptive positive case, according to an announcement Wednesday.

The number of confirmed cases in the county is eight ,and there are four presumptive positive cases, bring the county’s total to 12, the ACHD said.

The agency said contact tracing “is just underway on these cases. As a result, no further information is available at this time.”

The new figure raises the southwestern Pennsylvania’s total to 15, with two cases in Washington County and another in Beaver County. Montgomery County in Eastern Pennsylvania has the state’s highest total with 32.
