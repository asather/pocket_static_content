State police investigate shooting in Aliquippa

State police Wednesday night are investigating a shooting in Beaver County.

The shooting happened at the Valley Terrace apartments on Superior Avenue in Aliquippa, dispatchers said. Information on injuries or victims was not immediately available.

State troopers from Beaver County said they received a call for the shooting around 6:28 p.m. Troopers were still at the scene as of 9:45 p.m.
