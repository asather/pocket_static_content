TRAFFIC ALERT: Crash that caused heavy delays for Parkway East traffic cleared

Motorists going eastbound/outbound on the Parkway East experienced heavy delays Sunday due to a traffic accident at the Squirrel Hill Tunnel.

Itâ€™s unclear how many vehicles were involved or if there are any injuries. The crash occurred on the Swissvale end of the outbound tunnel.

Footage from PennDOT surveillance cameras showed that, initially, both the inbound and outbound lanes of the tunnel were shut down, causing delays in both directions.

The crash occurred after 2 p.m. The inbound lane was reopened after 2:30 p.m. The outbound side was finally opened around 3:25 p.m.

At one point, outbound traffic was backed up to the Birmingham Bridge.
