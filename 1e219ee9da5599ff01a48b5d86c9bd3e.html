Man dies after crash in Washington County

A Charleroi man died Thursday night after crashing into a building in Washington County.

The Washington County coroner identified the man as Charles Matava, 69. He was driving a vehicle that lost control and hit a building on the 700 block of Washington Avenue in Charleroi.

The collision happened just before 9 p.m. There was no information available on what caused the crash, and the cause and manner of death are still pending

Mr. Matava was pronounced dead around 10:26 p.m. at the Monongahela Valley Hospital Emergency Room.

The Charleroi Regional Police Department is investigating the incident.
