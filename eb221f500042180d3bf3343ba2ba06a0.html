Police investigate body found inside South Oakland residence

A body was found inside a residence in South Oakland early Friday morning.

Pittsburgh Police responded to a call around 1 a.m. Friday to the single-digit block of Halket Street in South Oakland.

When officers arrived, they found the body of a deceased adult male in an upstairs bedroom believed to have been there for some time.

The cause, manner and time of death will be determined by the Allegheny County Medical Examiner's Office.

Violent Crime Unit detectives are investigating.
