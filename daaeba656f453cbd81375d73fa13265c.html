Girls basketball highlights: Washington shocks top-seeded Seton LaSalle

Washington might not qualify for the WPIAL Class 3A girls playoffs, but the Little Prexies certainly played a spoiler role to perfection Monday night.

Senior point guard Randi Thomas scored a game-high 21 points and Kyla Woods chipped in 15 as Washington (7-13, 5-8) dumped No. 1 Seton LaSalle (14-6, 11-2), 54-50, in overtime to move within a game of Brownsville (11-8, 6-7) for the fourth and final playoff spot in Section 2.
