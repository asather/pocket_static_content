Man shot in Mount Oliver

A man was shot in the leg in Mount Oliver Monday morning.

Allegheny County Police said officers responded to a shooting around 7:30 a.m. in the 200 block of Locust Street, where they found a 22-year-old man with a gunshot wound. He was taken to a Pittsburgh hospital in stable condition.

Police continue to investigate; no arrests have been made.
