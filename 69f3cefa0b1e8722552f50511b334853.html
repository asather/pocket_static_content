Police search for missing New Castle woman

Police are searching for a New Castle woman who went missing before the holildays.

New Castle police said Sabrina Salamon, 39, was last seen on Dec. 20 in New Castle.

Salamon is described as 5-foot-4 with brown hair and green eyes.

Police ask that anyone with on her whereabouts to call New Castle police at 724-656-3586 or tips can be left on the police tip line at www.newcastlepd.com.
