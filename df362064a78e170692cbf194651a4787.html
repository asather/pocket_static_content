WPIAL Class 5A roundup: Upper St. Clair keeps streak alive against Bethel Park

A few hours after Upper St. Clair standout wide receiver David Pantelis committed to Yale, his Panthers took Bethel Park to school.

Pantelis hauled in six passes for 50 yards and the Panthers running game provided more big plays as Upper St. Clair picked up its sixth win in a row against the visiting Black Hawks, 34-7, in the Allegheny Six Conference.

Ethan Hiester carried the ball nine times for 92 yards and scored on a 15-yard run. Jaden Keating and Ethan Dahlem had two rushing touchdowns apiece.
