Police: Woman found dead inside Homewood residence

Police are investigating after a woman was found dead early Thursday at a residence in Homewood, according to Pittsburgh police spokesman Maurice Matthews.

Authorities responded just before 5:30 a.m. to the 7000 block of Forest Way for a welfare check.

A man, whose identity has not been released, was high on drugs, police said, and told authorities he was afraid of his surroundings. He was taken to a local hospital for evaluation.

While at the hospital, the man called police and asked that they return to the residence. When police returned to the home, they found a deceased woman.
