WPIAL qualifiers headed to PIAA wrestling tournament in Hershey

The PIAA wrestling championships begin Thursday at Giant Center in Hershey. The WPIAL will be represented by 85 wrestlers — 56 in Class 3A, 29 in Class 2A. Below is a list of the qualifiers, with region place, grade and record. Only regional champs are seeded.
