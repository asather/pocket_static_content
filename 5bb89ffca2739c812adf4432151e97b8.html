Pedestrian seriously injured after struck by vehicle on McKnight Road

A pedestrian was seriously injured when he was struck by a vehicle on McKnight Road in Ross Monday night.

Ross police said that shortly before 9:30  p.m., the pedestrian attempted to cross McKnight by jumping over the middle barrier and walking into the northbound lanes. The victim saw an oncoming vehicle and then turned back to the barrier. The vehicle swerved in an attempt to avoid hitting the pedestrian, but the front of the car hit him, according to police.

Police said the victim suffered head, leg and internal injuries. He was still being treated in the trauma unit at Allegheny General Hospital Tuesday morning.

The pedestrian was not walking in a crosswalk when the accident occurred, according to police.

The driver of the vehicle remained at the scene.
