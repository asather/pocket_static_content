Police investigating fatal shooting in Lincoln-Lemington

A man was found dead on a sidewalk late Tuesday evening after a shooting in the Lincoln-Lemington neighborhood of Pittsburgh.

Pittsburgh Police responded to a ShotSpotter alert for multiple rounds fired on the 6400 block of Clifford Street around 11:40 p.m. Tuesday.

When Zone 5 officers arrived, they discovered a man down on the sidewalk. Medics pronounced the victim dead at the scene.

There were no other victims or suspect descriptions.

The Mobile Crime Unit processed the scene; the investigation is ongoing.
