Small aircraft made emergency landing near Rostraver Airport

An ultralight plane was forced to make an emergency landing Sunday evening near the Rostraver Airport, Pittsburgh Post-Gazette news partner KDKA reported.

According to the Rostraver Central Fire Department, the aircraft was forced to land about a half-mile north of the airport, into Allegheny County.

No injuries were reported. Emergency crews cleaned up a minor fuel leak.
