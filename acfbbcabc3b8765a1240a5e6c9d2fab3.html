Police: Pedestrian hit, shots fired in East Pittsburgh

This story was updated at 10:45 p.m. on July 11, 2020.

State police are investigating after a driver struck a pedestrian and someone fired gunshots into a vehicle Saturday in East Pittsburgh.

Authorities were dispatched around 5:17 p.m. to the 500 block of Bessemer Avenue for reports of a hit pedestrian. The juvenile pedestrian was taken to a hospital in serious, but stable, condition.

An altercation occurred shortly after the crash and someone fired into the rear of the vehicle, police said. The shooting caused no injuries and a suspect is in custody.

Police did not give more information on the suspect.
