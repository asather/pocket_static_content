Four Chord Music Festival with Blink-182 moved to July 2021

The Four Chord Music Festival, originally scheduled for July 10 at Wild Things Park in Washington, Pa., has been moved to July 17, 2021.

The pop-punk fest will still be headlined by Blink-182, joined by The Used, State Champs, Mayday Parade, Four Year Strong, Hit the Lights, Sleep On It, Eternal Boy, Patent Pending, Keep Flying, Fortune Cove, Look Out Loretta and more.

Tickets are $61. Limited VIP packages will be available for $160 and include early admission, pizza party, VIP T-Shirt, festival poster,

lanyard and laminate, access to VIP Lounge, VIP Parking and special acoustic performances from Four Chord Music Festival artists.

A limited early bird for GA & VIP will begin at the time of announce for $55 for GA, $150 for VIP. Go to FourChordMusicFestival.com or at ShowClix.com.

All tickets previously purchased tickets for 2020 will be honored for Four Chord Music Festival 7 in 2021.

Gates will open at noon, with the bands starting at 1:30 p.m.
