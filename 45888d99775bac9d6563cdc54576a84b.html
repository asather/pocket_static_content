Body of missing New Castle woman found

The body of a New Castle woman who went missing before Christmas was found Thursday.

New Castle Police announced that a volunteer search party found the body of Sabrina Salamon just after 4 p.m. Her body was located on Long Avenue in New Castle. 

Salamon was reported missing on Dec. 20. 

Police continue to investigate as they await autopsy results.
