12th Port Authority of Allegheny County employee tests positive for COVID-19

A twelfth Port Authority of Allegheny County employee has tested positive for COVID-19.

The Port Authority reported that the employee works in maintenance at the West Mifflin garage. The employee's last day of work was April 21 and has since been in self-quarantine.

This is the tenth case at the West Mifflin garage and the eighth case involving a maintenance employee at this location.

The company said its West Mifflin garage has been deep cleaned and sanitized twice in the last two weeks, most recently on Friday.

In response to this cluster of cases, Port Authority said it began taking employeesâ€™ temperatures Sunday before starting their shift. Any employee with a temperature above 100.4 degrees Fahrenheit will be sent home, with pay, until cleared to return to work by their medical provider, the company said.

The Port Authority has more than 2,600 employees, including 1,300 operators and 800 mechanics. As of Tuesday, the company has 97 employees awaiting COVID-19 testing. Fifty-two employees have returned to work since testing negative.
