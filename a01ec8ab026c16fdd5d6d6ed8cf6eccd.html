City Theatre announces Drive-In Arts Festival at Hazelwood Green

City Theatre will present The Drive-In Arts Festival, 12 nights of music, theater, comedy and dance at the Hazelwood Green, running Sept. 10 to 27.

The space is a large parking lot with a covered stage and large screen simulcasting a live projection of the show. Audio will be transmitted through the car FM radio. Vehicles will be spaced out in a staggered configuration to allow for physical distancing between guests.

Restrooms will be available but there will be no concessions. Guests can bring their own food, but alcohol and smoking are not permitted. Attendees will be asked to wear masks and social distance while away from their cars.

Doors open at 7 p.m. and performances begin at 8 p.m. Late-comers will not be admitted. Most performances will end by 9:45 p.m.

Tickets are per person and are $20 for subscribers and $25 for non-subscribers. Children under the age of 7 do not need a ticket. Attendees can have up to four people per car. Tickets must be purchased in advance, before 4 p.m. the day of the performance. Go to citytheatre.org or call 412-431-2489.

Subscribers can transfer their money into tickets by calling the City Theatre box office Monday through Friday from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m.

Here is the lineup:

Sept. 10: The Buckle Downs/The Living Street presented by 91.3FM WYEP and Pittsburgh Cultural Trust

Sept. 11: A Night of Epic Comedy presented by Drinking Partners

Sept. 12: 1Hood Media (hip-hop)

Sept. 13: An Evening of Dance/Classical Music featuring Texture Dance Company, a Classical Music Ensemble presented by Pittsburgh Symphony Orchestra, and James Gilmer, Pearlann Porter and slowdanger presented by the Pittsburgh Cultural Trust

Sept. 16: Classical Music Ensemble presented by Pittsburgh Symphony Orchestra

Sept. 17: Dwayne Dolphin Trio and Tony DePaolis Quartet presented by MCG Jazz

Sept. 18: Rapper-singer Mars Jackson presented by the August Wilson African American Cultural Center

Sept. 19: R&B singer INEZ presented by WYEP

Sept. 20: Magician Lee Terbosic presented by Liberty Magic

Sept. 24: Rock band Meeting of Important People presented by Pittsburgh Cultural Trust and WYEP

Sept. 25: Hip Hop and Spoken Word presented by 1Hood Media

Sept. 26: An Evening of Jazz and Poetry featuring the Yoko Suzuki Trio and Thomas Wendt Quintet presented by MCG Jazz and City of Asylum

Sept. 27: Social Justice Disco presented by Pittsburgh Cultural Trust
