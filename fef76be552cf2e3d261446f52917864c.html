Wrong-way driver collides with police car on Parkway West

A vehicle driving the wrong way on Westbound Interstate 376 on Friday hit a police car, state police said.

There are no reports of any injuries yet.

The collision happened around 10:15 p.m., according to PennDOT alerts.

The crash area has been cleared and the westbound lanes of the Parkway West have reopened.

This story is developing. It will be updated.
