Police investigate Northview Heights shooting; man in critical condition

Pittsburgh Police responded to a ShotSpotter alert around 12:10 a.m. Monday of multiple shots fired on the 400 block of Mount Pleasant Road.

When Zone 1 officers arrived, they found an adult male with multiple gunshot wounds.

Medics transported the man to a local hospital in critical condition.

Mount Pleasant Road is closed while detectives process the scene for evidence.
