Oakland Indian restaurant hit with consumer alert

All India on North Craig Street in Oakland was hit was a consumer alert by the Allegheny County Health Department for several food safety violations. 

The alert, posted on March 3, noted water leaking from the ceiling in food service and food preparation areas, as well as repeat violations concerning improper hot holding temperatures, improper cold holding temperatures, and lack of a certified food protection manager.

A message left at the restaurant was not immediately returned.
