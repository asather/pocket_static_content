One person shot during armed robbery near IUP

Indiana police said two people were injured following an armed robbery near the Indiana University of Pennsylvania.

Indiana Borough police responded to an armed robbery at 7:56 p.m in the 800 block of Wayne Ave, according to a release from police.

As police responded to that call, a second call came in reporting gunshots heard in the same area.

When police arrived, they found two injured people, one of whom was shot. Both victims have received medical attention, police said, but there is no update on their conditions.

The release from authorities described the suspects as “three males attempting to obstruct their identities.” An alert from IUP earlier in the evening said the suspects were three black men in ski masks. They are still at-large.

Police said they are processing two separate crime scenes, and believe this “was not a random incident,” though they “do not believe there is a current risk to community safety.”

When police first responded, the university released an advisory telling students to stay locked indoors. The shooting location is two blocks from IUP’s main campus.

The statement from police said the investigation is ongoing and “all residents are encouraged to remain vigilant and report suspicious activity.”
