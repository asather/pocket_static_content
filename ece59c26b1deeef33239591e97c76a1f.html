Steelers, Pirates, Penguins offer specialized player Valentine's Day cards

Love is in the air ... and at Heinz Field, at PNC Park and at PPG Paints Arena.

Pittsburgh’s sports teams are spreading the love with catchy Valentine’s Day cards that hold heartfelt messages from the city’s finest athletes.

Express your appreciation for your loved one and tell them “I’d give up a first round pick for you,” with a card featuring Steelers safety Minkah Fitzpatrick.

Or, try “Girl, you make me Ka-swooon,” with the help of Penguins forward Dominik Kahun.

And if all else fails, there is always Josh Bell. “I can’t BELLieve you’re mine,” reads the card from the Pirates first baseman.

Each team is offering on their websites downloadable cards for fans to print and share.
