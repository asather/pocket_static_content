Labor Day 2020 closings

All city of Pittsburgh, Allegheny County, commonwealth of Pennsylvania and federal offices and courts will be closed Monday for the Labor Day holiday.

• Post offices will be closed, and regular mail will be neither picked up nor delivered.

• Banks will be closed. State liquor stores normally open on Mondays will be open until 5 p.m.

• Garbage pickup will be delayed one day.

• The Port Authority of Allegheny County will operate holiday bus, T and incline service; the customer service phone line (412-442-2000 or for TTY, 412-231-7007) will be available from 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. The Downtown Service Center will be closed.
