U.S. Steel adds Tracy Atkinson to board of directors

Pittsburgh-based U.S. Steel said Tracy Atkinson has joined its board of directors.

Ms. Atkinson, a certified public accountant, retired in March from State Street Corp. after working 12 years for the financial services firm in jobs including executive vice president, chief compliance officer, treasurer and chief administration officer.

She previously held leadership positions at MFS Investment Management and was a partner at PricewaterhouseCoopers.

Ms. Atkinson also serves on the board of Raytheon Technologies.

With her appointment, announced Monday, U.S. Steel has three women on its 14-member board. 

Ms. Atkinson’s election to U.S. Steel’s board bumps the total percentage of female corporate directors at Pittsburgh companies to 26.4%. A Post-Gazette story published Sunday showed the percentage at 26.3%.
