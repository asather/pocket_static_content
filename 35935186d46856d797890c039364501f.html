Steelers sign punter Dustin Colquitt, release veteran Jordan Berry

The Steelers will have a new punter for the first time since the 2014 season.

Dustin Colquitt, a 15-year veteran, signed with the Steelers on Monday after a tryout. Jordan Berry, who had served as the punter since 2015, was released.

Colquitt spent 15 seasons in Kansas City and was the punter in the Super Bowl for the Chiefs earlier this year. He played for the Chiefs from 2005 until he was released in the spring.
