HOV lanes closed to traffic in Pittsburgh and Ross

PennDOT District 11 announced the closure of the High Occupancy Vehicle (HOV) lanes in Ross and the City of Pittsburgh.

PennDOT said the lanes would be closed indefinitely for public safety purposes.
