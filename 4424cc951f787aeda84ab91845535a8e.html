11-year-old girl goes missing in West Mifflin

West Mifflin Emergency Services are looking for a missing girl Tuesday night.

Haley Hanson, 11, went missing in the Homeville Road area.

Anyone who has seen Haley is asked to call the West Mifflin Police Department at (412) 461-3125 or call 911.
