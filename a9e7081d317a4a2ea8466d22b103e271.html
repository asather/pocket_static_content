'A Beautiful Day In The Neighborhood' to be released on DVD next month

The film “A Beautiful Day In The Neighborhood” is scheduled to hit DVD and digital platforms next month.

The DVD and Blu-ray release date is set for Feb. 18. The movie will be available earlier through Amazon Video and iTunes Feb. 4.

“A Beautiful Day” opened in Pittsburgh theaters on Nov. 22.

The movie, which stars Tom Hanks as the beloved TV personality Fred Rogers as well as Matthew Rhys and Chris Cooper, was filmed throughout the Pittsburgh area, including Oakland, Allegheny Cemetery, Mt. Lebanon, Allison Park and Squirrel Hill.

Based on journalist Tom Junod’s longtime friendship with the “Mister Rogers’ Neighborhood” host, the film touches on how Junod befriends the man who influenced generations of children and how their relationship turns into a life-altering experience.

An Academy Award and Golden Globe winner, Hanks was nominated for another Globe in the best supporting actor category for his portrayal of Mr. Rogers, who died in February 2003.
