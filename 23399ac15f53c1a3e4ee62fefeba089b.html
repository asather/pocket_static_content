Pittsburgh Police searching for missing girl, 11

Pittsburgh Police on Monday are searching for a missing 11-year-old girl last seen in Beltzhoover area of Pittsburgh.

Daitarra Rippy is a light-skinned Black female who is 5 foot, 4 inches tall and weighs 100 pounds. She has long black hair which is usually worn in a ponytail.

She was last seen in the Beltzhoover area around 9:30 p.m. Sunday wearing a white shirt and black shorts.

Anyone with information is asked to call 911.
