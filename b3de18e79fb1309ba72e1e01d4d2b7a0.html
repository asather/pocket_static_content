New Castle police charge man with homicide after shooting

A New Castle man on Sunday was charged with homicide after another man was shot to death on Thursday.

Juan Aviles Suarez, 33, is wanted in connection to the shooting that resulted in the death of Eiram Alfonso Santiago-Hernandez, 26. 

Authorities say Mr. Suarez shot Mr. Santiago-Hernandez around 7:48 pm Thursday on Pin Oak Drive in the Oak Leaf Gardens Housing Complex.

Mr. Santiago-Hernandez was transported to UPMC Jameson, where he was pronounced dead, police said.

Officials asked anyone with knowledge of Mr. Suarez's whereabouts to contact the New Castle police at 724-656-9300.
