'Our Lady of the Parkway' receives historic designation

A shrine to the Blessed Mother that overlooks the Parkway East from South Oakland will receive historic designation.

A cliff-side statue of the Virgin Mary placed there by community members in 1956 just off Wakefield Street is commonly referred to as “Our Lady of the Parkway.”

The shrine, where a plaque and other components have been added over the years, has been maintained by volunteers, according to historic nomination documents.

City Council voted 7-1 Tuesday to approve historic status for the shrine.

Councilman Ricky Burgess voted against. Council President Theresa Kail-Smith was absent.

Mayor Bill Peduto supports the historic designation.
