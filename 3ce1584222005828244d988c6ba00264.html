City to distribute meals to seniors already registered with food program

A program used by the city to distribute meals to seniors in need will be providing food for those already registered for normal “congregate meal service” beginning Friday.

The city, through the Allegheny County Area Agency on Aging program, will distribute pre-packaged meals, which will likely be frozen, to current food distribution providers.

The meals will be available for take-out only and will be served between 11 a.m. to 1 p;.m. on Monday, Wednesday and Friday at the following City Healthy Active Living (Senior) Centers:

● Glen Hazel, 945 Rosselle Court

● Sheraden, 720 Sherwood Avenue

● South Side, 12th Street and 1 Bedford Square

● Lawrenceville, 4600 Butler Street

● Homewood, 7321 Frankstown Avenue

● Hazelwood, 5344 Second Avenue

According to the city, approximately 100 meals a day will be distributed through the program.

Seniors who are not registered for the program but are in need of food or other resources should contact the Allegheny County Area on Aging’s SeniorLine at 1-800-344-4319.
