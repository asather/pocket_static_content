Lucky No. 7: Ben Roethlisberger welcomes seven new puppies to family

The number seven seems to run in the Roethlisberger family. 

Ben Roethlisberger’s German Shepherd named Kota recently gave birth to seven puppies, according to an announcement on the Steelers quarterback’s Twitter account. 

BigBen7.com, the official website for Roethlisberger, posted two photos on Sunday of Kota and her new babies. 

Kota was one of two dogs in the Roethlisberger household along with their Vizsla named Remy.
