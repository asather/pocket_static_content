Pirates add Carson Fulmer, Kyle Crick for Saturday’s game

MILWAUKEE — The Pirates on Saturday added Carson Fulmer to the active roster and reinstated Kyle Crick from the injured list.

They claimed Fulmer off waivers from the Detroit Tigers on Monday. He had made seven appearances, allowing five earned runs over 6 ⅔ innings, before he was designated for assignment June 20.

Crick went on the 10-day injured list with a right lat muscle strain July 28. He last appeared in a game July 27.

To make room on the active roster, the Pirates optioned right-handed reliever Nick Mears and first baseman Will Craig to Altoona.
