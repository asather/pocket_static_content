Pittsburgh police officer memorial vandalized

A police memorial on Pittsburgh’s North Shore was vandalized Wednesday night.

The base of the Law Enforcement Officers Memorial near Heinz Field was spray-painted and appears to say “Deciet.”

The defacement comes after protests in Minneapolis following the death of a black man in police custody. 

More information was not immediately available.

A World War I memorial was vandalized in Lawrenceville earlier this week.
