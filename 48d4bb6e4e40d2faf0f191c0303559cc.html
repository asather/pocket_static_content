Police searching for missing 14-year-old girl last seen in Mount Oliver

Police are looking for a missing 14-year-old girl last seen Sunday in Mount Oliver.

London Rutherford, who stands about 5’5 and is roughly 130 pounds, was reported missing by her mother Monday, according to the Mount Oliver Borough Police Department.

Police chief Matt Juzwick said the teen got into argument with her mother on Sunday. Her mom noticed she was missing Monday morning and reported it to police, he said.

“Rutherford’s family believes she might be with a friend in Pittsburgh’s Carrick neighborhood,” Mr. Juzwick said.

Anyone with information is asked to call the Mount Oliver Borough Police Department at 412-431-2254.
