At least 1 killed in Fayette County tractor-trailer crash

At least one person was killed in a crash involving an SUV and tractor-trailer truck in Fayette County Tuesday morning.

The crash occurred around 8 a.m. on Route 40/National Pike in Henry Clay Township, according to county emergency officials. The county coroner has been called to the scene.

Pennsylvania State Police are investigating.

Further details were not immediately available.
