Male injured in Sheraden shooting

A male was found shot Sunday morning in Sheraden.

Police were called around 1:35 a.m. to the 3100 block of Chartiers Avenue, where they found the male shot in the arm.

He was taken to the hospital in stable condition.

He told police that he was shot at by a black SUV that took off in the direction of Windgap.

No arrests have been made, police said.
