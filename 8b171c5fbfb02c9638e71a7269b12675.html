Death of man found in Etna alley under investigation

Allegheny County homicide detectives are investigating after a man was found unresponsive Sunday morning in an Etna alley and later pronounced dead.

The 52-year-old was found lying in the alley of South Spring Street. He was taken to a local hospital where he was pronounced dead, police said.

Anyone with information is asked to call the county police tip line at 1-833-ALL-TIPS. Callers can remain anonymous.
