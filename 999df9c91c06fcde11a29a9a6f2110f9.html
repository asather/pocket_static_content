Westmoreland County coroner identifies man who died in motorcycle accident

The Westmoreland County coroner Kenneth A. Bacha has identified a man who died in an off-road motorcycle accident on Sunday. The coroner said the cause of death was multiple blunt force trauma and ruled his death as an accident.

The incident occurred at 1:21 p.m. Sunday at 165 Sewickley Creed Road in Mount Pleasant Township. 

The coroner identified 19-year-old Kenneth W. Miller, Jr., of Salem Township, as the operator and lone occupant of a Suzuki RM-Z450. The coroner states he was traveling on a motocross track and “attempted to clear a table top jump, came up short, and was separated from the motorcycle.”

Emergency medical personnel transported Mr. Miller to Excela Frick Emergency Department where he was pronounced dead by a hospital physician. Mr. Miller had been wearing a helmet and proper protective gear, according to the coroner.
