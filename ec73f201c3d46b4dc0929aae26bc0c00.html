Pirates, other MLB clubs donating $1 million each to ballpark employees

Each Major League Baseball team will donate $1 million to ballpark employees losing wages because of the COVID-19 pandemic that has postponed the 2020 season, commissioner Rob Manfred announced on Tuesday.

That obviously includes the Pirates and PNC Park, as the total given will be $30 million.

In a statement, Manfred also said individual clubs will be announcing more details soon, as they look to offer support in local communities.
