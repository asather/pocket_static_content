Man arrested from Larimer rooftop

A man was arrested from the roof of a Larimer home Friday night after Pittsburgh police were called to the home for a domestic incident.

Police responded to a report of a male involved in a domestic dispute on the 600 block of Lenora Street around 9 p.m. Friday. When police arrived and tried to speak to him, the man refused to leave the house. Police called SWAT to the scene and evacuated neighboring homes.

The man climbed out of a window and on to the roof of the two-story home. SWAT was able to access the roof around 10:30 p.m. and arrested him on an outstanding warrant for an unrelated domestic incident.
