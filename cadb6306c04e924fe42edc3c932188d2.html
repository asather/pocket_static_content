City police officer not seriously injured after motorcycle struck road 'hazard'

Pittsburgh police and EMS responded to a single vehicle collision at the Fort Pitt ramp Saturday afternoon after a police officer’s motorcycle went down after they “struck some type of hazard on the road.” 

According to police, the officer was not seriously injured, but was transported to the hospital for evaluation.

Around 3 p.m., police say they arrived at the scene at Commonwealth Place at the Fort Pitt ramp to assist the officer. 

Police say they will be investigating the cause of the hazard.
