Two hospitalized in Shadyside crash

Two people were transported to the hospital Thursday night following a crash in Shadyside, dispatchers said.

The collision was reported 9:39 p.m at the intersection of Fifth Avenue and College Street.

Dispatchers could not confirm how many vehicles were involved or the conditions of those injured.
