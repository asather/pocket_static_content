New Castle police seek two homicide suspects in missing person case

New Castle police on Sunday filed homicide and conspiracy charges against two people in connection to a missing person case.

Amari Wise, 19, was reported missing earlier this month. Officials have charged Connor Henry, 20, and Todd Henry, 47, in connection with Mr. Wise’s disappearance.

The younger Mr. Henry was charged with homicide and tampering with evidence. He was arrested in Ocean City, Md., police said Sunday evening. The elder Mr. Henry was charged with criminal conspiracy to commit homicide, obstruction of justice and tampering with evidence. Both are from New Castle, according to court documents.

More information on the charges was not immediately available. Police said they would release more information on the case “in the coming days.”
