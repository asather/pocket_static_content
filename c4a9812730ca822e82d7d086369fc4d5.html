Five-year-old girl hit by car in Sheraden

A five-year-old girl was taken to the hospital on Monday afternoon after she was hit by a car in Sheraden. 

Pittsburgh Police, EMS and Fire responded to calls of a child struck by a vehicle on the 600 block of Hillsboro Street at around 2:30 p.m.

According to Public Safety officials, the girl was struck on a roadway after exiting a vehicle. Medics transported the girl to a hospital in critical condition. 

The adult male driver of the vehicle that hit the girl stayed on the scene and cooperated with police, officials said. 

The Crash Investigation Unit is investigating.
