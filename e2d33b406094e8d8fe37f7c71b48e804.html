Suspects sought in North Side robbery, assault

Police are searching for two suspects believed to be involved with a robbery and assault of a woman on the North Side.

The pair robbed and assaulted a 59-year-old woman in the 200 block of Isabella Street at 5:45 a.m. Saturday.

The male suspect was wearing all black clothing with distinctly copper-colored shoes. The female suspect was wearing a Puma sweatshirt during the robbery, but then changed into a Baltimore Ravens sweatshirt.

Anyone with information on the identity of the suspects in the photo is asked to contact Pittsburgh Police Zone 1 Detectives Lang and Pascucci at 412-323-7201.
