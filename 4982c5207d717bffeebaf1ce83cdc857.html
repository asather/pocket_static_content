Pirates to use PNG Field in Altoona as alternate training facility

The Pirates announced on Thursday that they will use Peoples Natural Gas Field in Altoona as an alternate facility for spring training 2.0.

PNG Field is the home of the Class AA Altoona Curve, which has been a Pirates affiliate since 1999.

Altoona will be used by minor league and rehabbing players who do not train at PNC Park, where the Pirates will hold the bulk of their workouts.

In the release, the Pirates announced that workout dates, a roster and media information will be announced in the coming days.

"Being chosen as the alternate training facility really reinforces the strength of that relationship. Being 90 miles from PNC Park, Altoona is a great location to allow the Pirates to get a player to Pittsburgh, or wherever they need to get to, quickly and safely."
