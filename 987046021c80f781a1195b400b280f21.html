At least 1 injured in two-vehicle crash in Jefferson Hills area

At least one person was injured Friday in a two-vehicle crash on the Jefferson Hills-South Park Township border.

Allegheny County emergency officials said the crash occurred in the 900 block Cochran Mill Road shortly after 11 a.m.

The street was closed in both directions.

No further information was immediately available.
