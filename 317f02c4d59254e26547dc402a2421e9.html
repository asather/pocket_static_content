Body found in Armstrong Township lake

Police on Wednesday are investigating after a body was found in a lake in Kiski Township.

Officials from the Kiski Township Police Department responded Wednesday afternoon to reports of suspicious activity at the Apollo Spring Church Sportsman Club in Armstrong County.

A dive team searched the lake, found a body and returned it to shore, according to the Armstrong County Water Task Force. Authorities believe the incident is related to a missing persons report.

Multiple area fire departments and EMS, and the Pennsylvania Fish and Boat Commission, also responded.

The individual’s name has not yet been released.

Kiski police, the Armstrong County District Attorney’s Office, the Armstrong Coroner’s Office and the Fish and Boat Commission are investigating.
