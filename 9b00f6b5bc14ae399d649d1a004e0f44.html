2020 WPIAL basketball playoffs feature five defending champs as No. 1 seeds

The WPIAL boys and girls basketball playoffs begin Friday and five champions from last year are No. 1 seeds this season.

The WPIAL released pairings and seedings at a meeting Tuesday night at the DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel in Green Tree.

The No. 1 seeds for boys are Butler (Class 6A), Penn Hills (5A), Highlands (4A), Lincoln Park (3A), Our Lady of the Sacred Heart (2A) and Vincentian (1A). Lincoln Park and OLSH won championships a year ago.

The No. 1 seeds for girls are Bethel Park (Class 6A), Chartiers Valley (5A), North Catholic (4A), Beaver (3A), Serra (2A) and Rochester (1A). Chartiers Valley, North Catholic and Rochester are defending champions.

The first round of the playoffs will last through Feb. 19. All 12 WPIAL championships will be played over three days (Feb. 27-29) at Petersen Events Center on the campus of the University of Pittsburgh.
