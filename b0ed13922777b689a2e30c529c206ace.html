One person is in custody and authorities are searching for another suspect over the border in West Virginia after a police chase in Beaver County early Thursday.

The chase started around 3:30 a.m. when police tried to stop a vehicle along Route 168 in Hookstown, according to Pennsylvania State Police.

The chase ended a few miles away in Georgetown, Beaver County and police took one man into custody, Pittsburgh Post-Gazette news partner KDKA-TV reported. A second person who was in the car, ran off and police were searching for him in the area of Chester, W.Va., west of Georgetown.

A SWAT team, police helicopter and police K-9 units were called in to assist.

No further information was immediately available.
