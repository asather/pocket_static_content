2 killed in head-on crash in Fayette County

Authorities said speed is believed to have been a factor in a head-on crash that killed two people in Fayette County Tuesday.

Pennsylvania State Police said the crash occurred around 2:15 p.m. on Route 201 in Dunbar.

Police said a 58-year-old man and a 24-year-old man were killed. Their identities have not been released.

Police continue to investigate.
