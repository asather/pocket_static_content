Suspect flees after shooting at police in New Kensington

Shots were fired at police officers in New Kensington on Tuesday night, according to Post-Gazette news partner KDKA-TV.

Multiple police departments were on the scene in the Kenneth Avenue and Constitution Boulevard area of the city. A branch of Westmoreland Community College is nearby.

Officials told KDKA that, while on patrol, they approached an individual they believed to be wanted in a separate incident. The person then shot at the officers, fled and is “unaccounted for,” according to police. No one was hurt. The incident occurred at the intersection of Kenneth Avenue and Walnut Street.

They are uncertain as to the identity of the suspect and are closing streets in the area for investigation. A car on Kenneth Avenue appeared to have broken windows that may have been shot out.

Local residents are told to stay indoors as the suspect is considered armed and dangerous.
