Joe Musgrove to start Wednesday against Cubs

Joe Musgrove will start Wednesday against the Cubs at PNC Park, manager Derek Shelton announced after Tuesday’s loss.

Musgrove — the Pirates’ opening day starter — has not pitched since Aug. 4 because of right triceps inflammation and right ankle discomfort. This will be Musgrove’s fourth start of the season.

Musgrove is 0-3 this season with a 6.75 ERA. He has struck out 17 in 14⅔ innings, although control has been an issue at times. The right-hander has 11 walks in those three starts.

Derek Holland was supposed to start Wednesday’s game. Shelton said the Pirates were still deciding whether Holland would work out of the bullpen or have his start pushed back.
