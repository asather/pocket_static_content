Missing man's body recovered from Youghiogheny River in Rostraver

The body of a man who was reported missing Sunday in Westmoreland County was found in the Youghiogheny River Monday morning.

The man, whose identity has not been released, was pulled from the river around 10:45 a.m., nearly three hours after emergency officials resumed their search Monday.

The Rostraver Township Police Department and several fire departments assisted in the search, according to Westmoreland dispatch officials. 

A state police helicopter was also used.

Further information was not immediately available.
