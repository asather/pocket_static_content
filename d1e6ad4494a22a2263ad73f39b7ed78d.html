Fire in Bloomfield apartment building on Liberty Avenue

Firefighters are working on a 2-alarm fire on Liberty Avenue in Bloomfield.

The fire started around 4 a.m. Tuesday at 4512 Liberty Ave.

Firefighters arriving on the scene reported smoke coming from the first floor, where a pizza business is located.

Reports said everyone was evacuated from the apartment buildings above.
