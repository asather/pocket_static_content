Pittsburgh Symphony will welcome Ben Folds in June

The always entertaining Ben Folds will return to Heinz Hall on June 3 for another evening with the Pittsburgh Symphony Orchestra.

Tickets are $35 to $125 and go on sale at 10 a.m. Friday, March 6 at pittsburghsymphony.org or 412-392-4900. It will begin at 7:30 p.m.

The pop singer-songwriter-pianist and classical composer has done four appearances with the Pittsburgh Symphony, dating back to a sold-out concert in 2010.

Special VIP Experiences are available, including a “Meet & Greet” with premium seating and “Ask Me Anything…About Music”; for VIP Experience details, visit www.pittsburghsymphony.org.
