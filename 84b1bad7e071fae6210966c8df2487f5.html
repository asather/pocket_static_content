Water main break causes outage in Blawnox

A water main break in Blawnox caused an outage on Sunday.

Authorities reported the shortage was near Maple Way and Cable Avenue. A water buffalo has been requested.

Officials were unsure of when service would be restored.

More information was not immediately available.
