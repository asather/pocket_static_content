Reba McEntire moves tour, and PPG Paints Arena date, to 2021

Country star Reba McEntire has rescheduled her tour for summer 2021 and will play her PPG Paints Arena show next year on Aug. 8.

It was originally set for March 26 and then moved to July 16.

Previously purchased tickets will be honored for all dates. If fans are unable to attend the new dates they will receive an email explaining how to request a refund.

“I can’t wait to see everybody when we know it’s safe to gather again,” McEntire said. “We’ve worked so hard on this new show and I’ll be ready to go as soon as we get the green light!”
