1 killed, 1 injured in stabbings at Lincoln-Lemington

Two men were stabbed, one fatally, on Saturday night in Lincoln-Lemington.

Pittsburgh police said that a man went into a corner store at Lincoln Avenue and Loire Way around 9:50 p.m. and stabbed and employee in the face. The victim was taken to the hospital in critical condition.

A short time later, police said, the suspect left the store and walked up Lincoln Avenue. Near Wilsie Street, the suspect stabbed another man multiple times, killing him at the scene.

Police did not provide a motive for either stabbing and no arrests have been made. Neither victim has been identified.

Anyone with information is asked to call Violent Crime Unit detectives at (412) 323-7161.
