Woman struck, killed by train in Tarentum

A woman on Friday night was hit and killed by a train in Tarentum, officials said.

Authorities have not yet identified the woman.

The collision occurred at Ross Street and East 4th Avenue near the Tarentum Bridge, dispatchers said.

The woman was reportedly walking on the tracks and was hit by a Norfolk Southern train heading eastbound to Cresson in Cambria County, according to Norfolk Southern spokesman Jeff DeGraff.

The call came in around 8:00 p.m., dispatchers said.

The train crew was uninjured.
