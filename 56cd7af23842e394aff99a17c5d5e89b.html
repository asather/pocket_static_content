Police: Man, 24, shot in chest crashes car into McKees Rocks police station

A man who was shot in the chest crashed his vehicle into the McKees Rocks police station Tuesday night.

Local officers found a 24-year-old man inside the vehicle, a Ford Taurus that crashed into the front of station on Bell Avenue around 11:33 p.m., Allegheny County police said Wednesday.

He was taken to a Pittsburgh hospital in critical condition.

County homicide is investigating. No arrests have been made.

Police are asking anyone with information about the shooting to call the County Police Tip Line 1-833-ALL-TIPS (255-8477); callers can remain anonymous.

This story will be updated.
