COVID-19: Pittsburgh Post-Gazette Coverage

The coronavirus, named for its crown-like spikes, is new. It can cause symptoms from the common cold to severe respiratory problems. It can infect people and animals and is considered moderately contagious. Experts are not sure how it is spread, but hypothesize it is through the air.

The virus first appeared in Wuhan, China, and now has been found in at least 25 other countries, although the vast majority of cases are in China.

Health experts encourage people to wash their hands often and to stay home if they are sick.
