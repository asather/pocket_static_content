Magnitude 3.7 earthquake reported Wednesday in Los Angeles

LOS ANGELES â€” A magnitude 3.7 earthquake was reported Wednesday morning at 12:03 a.m. Pacific time in Los Angeles, according to the U.S. Geological Survey.

The earthquake occurred less than a mile from View Park-Windsor Hills, less than a mile from Inglewood.

In the past 10 days, there have been two earthquakes of magnitude 3.0 or greater centered nearby.  A magnitude 3.5 earthquake was reported at 5:50 a.m. Saturday about 26 miles from Ridgecrest, Calif., according to the U.S. Geological Survey. 

A second earthquake of magnitude 3.3 occurred about 15 minutes later at the same location, the USGS said.
