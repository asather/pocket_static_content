2 killed in Rostraver house fire

This story was updated at 2:35 p.m. July 12. 

An elderly couple were killed Sunday when a fire tore through their Rostraver home.

The two-alarm fire was reported around 2 a.m. at 808 Collinsburg Rd., and is under investigation by the state fire marshal.

The Westmoreland County Coroner’s office identified the victims as Marie Oblak, 81, and Lloyd Oblak, 83.

A dog also was killed in the blaze.

The victims’ son is a volunteer with the fire company, said Frank Backstrom, president of the Collinsburg Fire Company.

Fire Chief Chris Hoffman said there was no chance of rescuing the victims.

According to the coroner’s report, neighbors contacted 911 and the fire was fully involved when local fire companies responded.

The victims, who were pronounced dead at the scene, were found inside the split-level home, said Mr. Backstrom. 

The cause of the fire is unknown.

Mr. Backstrom said it was the first fatal fire in the small community “in a long time.” 

McCauley Funeral Home, West Newton, is handling arrangements for the couple.
