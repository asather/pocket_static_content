Firefighters respond to 1-alarm fire at Bravo restaurant in Ross

Updated at 10:26 p.m., Sept. 20, 2020: 

Firefighters battled a 1-Alarm fire at the Bravo Italian Kitchen off of McKnight Road in Ross.

First responders arrived at the scene of the restaurant located at 4976 McKnight Road around 8 p.m. Sunday, according to Allegheny County dispatch. The restaurant was evacuated and the fire is under control, according to dispatch. There are no reported injuries.

According to Post-Gazette news partner KDKA-TV, the intersection of McKnight Road and Seibert Road is closed temporarily as crews continue to keep the fire under control.

By 10:30 p.m. Sunday, dispatch said crews have left, except for a fire investigator who remained on scene.
