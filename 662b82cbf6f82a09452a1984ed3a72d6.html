Man injured in Swissvale shooting

A man was injured during a shooting late Tuesday in Swissvale.

At approximately 10:53 p.m. Tuesday, Allegheny County 911 was notified of a shooting in the 2200 block of South Braddock Avenue.

First responders found an adult male victim suffering from a gunshot wound to the upper body. He was transported to a local hospital and is listed in stable condition.

Allegheny County Police Department's Homicide Unit and Swissvale Police are investigating.

Anyone with information concerning this incident is asked to call the County Police Tip Line 1-833-ALL-TIPS. Callers can remain anonymous.
