Penguins-Capitals: Game time, TV information and matchup notes

The Penguins will be facing the Capitals on Sunday in Washington, D.C. Here’s everything you need to know about this impending matchup:

Who: Penguins at Washington Capitals.

When, where: Noon Sunday, Capital One Arena.

Look, listen: WPXI, WXDX-FM (105.9), PittsburghPenguins.com.

Noteworthy: Projected Penguins starting goalie Matt Murray stopped 29 of 32 shots in a Feb. 2 win in Washington. ... Capitals winger Alex Ovechkin scored his 700th career goal Saturday. ... Evgeni Malkin scored twice in Saturday’s 5-2 loss to the Buffalo Sabres and with 20 goals is now three behind team leader Bryan Rust. ... The Capitals’ power play does not rank within the league’s top 10 but their penalty kill is in the top five at 83.7%. ... Jared McCann, who couldn’t score on any of his three shots Saturday, has now gone 14 games without a goal. ... Washington’s John Carlson leads all NHL defensemen with 71 points.

Did you know? The Penguins have won 48.4% of their faceoffs this season. Only five teams, including the Capitals, have fared worse.
