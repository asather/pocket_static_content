Police investigate after North Side gunshot victim admitted to hospital

Pittsburgh police on Wednesday are investigating after a male was admitted to a local hospital with a gunshot wound.

The victim appeared at the hospital shortly after police received a ShotSpotter alert around 9:15 p.m. on the 1400 block of Federal Street.

The male had a gunshot wound to the leg and was admitted to the hospital in stable condition.
