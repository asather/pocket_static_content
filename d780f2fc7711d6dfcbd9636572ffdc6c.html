Man shot in leg in North Braddock

A man was treated at a local hospital after a shooting Sunday evening in North Braddock.

Allegheny County 911 was notified at 11:56 p.m. Sunday of a possible shooting in the 1100 block of Franklin Avenue in North Braddock.

A 29-year-old male was treated at an area hospital for a single gun shot wound to the leg. The patient was listed in stable condition.

Allegheny County Police determined that the shooting occurred sometime earlier in the night.

Anyone with information concerning this incident is asked to call the County Police Tip Line 1-833-ALL-TIPS. Callers can remain anonymous.
