T collides with vehicle in Dormont

One person was taken to a hospital following a collision Tuesday in Dormont between a vehicle and a Port Authority light rail car.

The crash occurred at Hillsdale Avenue between Belrose and Voelkel avenues, according to Allegheny County.

A Port Authority spokesperson told Pittsburgh Post-Gazette news partner KDKA-TV that a person from the private vehicle was transported to St. Clair Hospital. There were six people on board the light-rail car.

Red Line service — both inbound and outbound — has since resumed, according to the Port Authority, which said riders may experience delays of up to 30 minutes.
