Jimmy Buffett coming to S&T Music Park in July

Jimmy Buffett & The Coral Reefer Band will bring the Slack Tide Tour to S&T Music Park (formerly KeyBank Pavilion) on July 16.

Tickets go on sale at 10 a.m. Friday at Livenation.com.
