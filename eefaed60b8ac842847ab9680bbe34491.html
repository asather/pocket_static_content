Police: Missing 20-year-old woman last seen January in Etna

Police are searching for a missing 20-year-old woman who was last seen January in Etna.

Kaylene Oehling wears brown framed glasses and goes by the name “Kaylene Young,” the Etna Police Department shared in a Facebook post Thursday. Police said she stands about 5’2, weighs 110 pounds, has dark brown hair and brown eyes. It is unclear what Ms. Oehling was last seen wearing.

She was last seen Jan. 9 in the borough, but last made contact with her mom in a phone call around 1:36 a.m. the following day, police said. She has not been heard from since.

She also stopped using her social media after that time, “which is not like her,” police shared. She left without her medication. 

Anyone with information on her whereabouts is asked to contact the Etna Police Department at 412-781-6271.
