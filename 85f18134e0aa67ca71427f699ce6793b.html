Tour of historic black churches Feb. 1

Tickets for a guided bus tour of historic sites in black history on Feb. 1 are on sale now through Jan. 30. Doors Open Pittsburgh is sponsoring the tour, “Houses of Freedom: Black History Tour,” from 9:45 a.m. to 1:45 p.m.

The tour will take participants inside some of the most significant black churches in Pittsburgh: Macedonia Church of Pittsburgh, St. Benedict the Moor and Brown Chapel AME Church.

For tickets, which are $25, online only, visit www.DoorsOpenPgh.org.
