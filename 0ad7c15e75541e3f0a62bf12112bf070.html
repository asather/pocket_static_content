Upper St. Clair School Board appoints school director

The Upper St. Clair School Board last week unanimously appointed Jennifer Schnore as school director.

Ms. Schnore replaces Dante Plutko, who was required to resign from the board in January after being elected to the Upper St. Clair Township Board of Commissioners in November.

She will serve out the remainder of Mr. Plutko’s term, which expires in December 2021.

Ms. Schnore has lived in Upper St. Clair since 2003 and is involved with the Eisenhower Elementary and Boyce Middle School parent teacher organizations and the Upper St. Clair parent teacher council.

She has a law degree from the University of Pittsburgh School of Law as well as a bachelor’s degree from Clemson University.
