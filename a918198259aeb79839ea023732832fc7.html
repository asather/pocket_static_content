Fourth of July closings

The following government and related services and offices normally operating on Saturday will be closed in recognition of Independence Day: Banks, post offices (including pick-up and delivery of regular mail) and Pennsylvania Department of Transportation driver license and photo-ID centers.

State liquor stores normally open on Saturdays are operating until 4 p.m.

The Port Authority of Allegheny County is running holiday bus, T and incline service, and the customer service line (412-442-2000, or the TTY number, 412-231-7007) is operating from 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
