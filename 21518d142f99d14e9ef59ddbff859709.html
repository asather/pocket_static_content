Allegheny County reports 17 new cases of COVID-19 and 0 new deaths

There were no new deaths and 17 new positive cases of COVID-19 reported Sunday in Allegheny County, the county health department announced.

Allegheny County has a total of 178 deaths — 166 are confirmed and 12 are probable. 

With the new cases of the respiratory disease, the county has had 2,175 positive cases since its first case was announced on March 14. Of them, 2,038 are confirmed and 137 are probable.

The number of hospitalizations rose by one for a total of 364 past or present hospitalizations. Of them, at least 141 required intensive care and 70 needed a mechanical ventilator.

 

On Sunday, the state reported 464 new positive cases of COVID-19, bringing its total to 81,730. The reported four deaths on Sunday bringing its number of fataliites to 6,423.
