Motley Crue, Def Leppard Stadium Tour rescheduled for summer 2021

The Stadium Tour, featuring Motley Crue, Def Leppard, Poison and Joan Jett and the Blackhearts, has been rescheduled for 2021 with a July 22 date at PNC Park.

The concert was originally set for Aug. 16 but was postponed due to the pandemic.

It would have been a Saturday night. It is now a Thursday show.

All tickets will be honored for the new dates. Ticketholders who cannot attend the 2021 show can visit livenation.com/refund.
