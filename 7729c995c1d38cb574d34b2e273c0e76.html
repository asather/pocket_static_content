2 dead, child critical after crash in Chateau

Two adults were killed and a child is in critical condition after a single-vehicle crash Saturday afternoon in Pittsburgh’s Chateau neighborhood.

All three were entrapped in the vehicle, which caught fire at the scene on Beaver Avenue near Island Avenue.

A man was found dead on arrival, according to police officials. A woman died after she was extricated and transported to a nearby hospital. The child was extricated and taken to UPMC Children’s Hospital of Pittsburgh.

Neither victim has yet been identified.

A dog was also killed in the crash. Two police officers received scratches while extricating the victims.

Beaver Avenue between Island Avenue and Branchport Street was closed while the Crash Investigation Unit processed the scene.

Police said witness accounts suggest speed was a factor in what caused the crash. The collision happened around 4:20 p.m.
