Pittsburgh concerts: What's on, what's postponed

The coronavirus threat wiped out most of the concerts in late March, and it’s beginning to spread further into the spring.

Already, the most anticipated show of the summer — The Rolling Stones at Heinz Field — has been postponed as the band looks to reschedule its No Filter Tour.

Stage AE has postponed all shows through the end of April, which, for now, would have the North Shore venue reopening for the sold-out Girl Talk concert on May 1.

The latest postponement is the June 2 concert by The 1975 at the Petersen Events Center.

A few shows, such as Reba McEntire and Nathaniel Rateliff, have already landed on new dates.

Some of the concerts in this section have been moved to new dates or have pending dates. This list will be updated:
