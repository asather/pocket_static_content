Police search for 2-year-old boy last seen June 1

Pittsburgh police are searching for a 2-year-old boy last seen June 1.

Moor Bey was last seen when he was dropped off at his mother’s house, police said. He is described as 2 feet, 6 inches tall, weighing 35 pounds with short, black hair.

Moor was left with his mother, 30-year-old Timmesha Gaines-Bey. Officials said Gaines-Bey’s most recent addresses were in Beechview and McKees Rocks. She may have traveled to areas of Florida such as Tampa or Fort Lauderdale, police said.

Anyone with information on Moor Bey’s and Timmesha Gaines-Bey’s location is asked to call the special victims unit at at 412-323-7141.
