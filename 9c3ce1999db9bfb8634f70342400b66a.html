Police search for missing teen last seen in Uniontown

State Police are searching for a missing 17-year-old last seen in Uniontown on Monday. 

Police say that Maurese “Reese” Richardson was last seen at a Walmart in Uniontown headed in an unknown direction. 

Richardson is described as five feet seven inches tall and about 165 pounds. 

Police say that he may be with another male named Patrick.
