Sewickley Bridge to close for 24 days for preservation work

The Sewickley Bridge, which connects Moon with Sewickley Borough over the Ohio River, will close for nearly a month for preservation work beginning in July.

The Pennsylvania Department of Transportation on Tuesday said the bridge will close on July 17 for 24 consecutive days — through Aug. 10 — while workers with Swank Construction renovate the span’s deck overlay and replace the bearing and deck joints.

With the schedule, PennDOT said it hopes to “minimize traffic impact, improve safety and take advantage of lower traffic volumes.”

Single-lane alternating traffic will be in effect on the bridge from 8 a.m. to 3 p.m. each day during the $3.69 million project. The overall project is expected to be completed in November, PennDOT said.
