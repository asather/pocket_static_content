Authorities locate body of boy who fell into Ohio River

Authorities on Wednesday located the body of a 10-year-old boy who fell into the Ohio River in Beaver County on Monday.

Post-Gazette news partner KDKA-TV reports the boy was found after state police from Philadelphia used special equipment to help complete the search.

The identity of the child has not yet been released.

Police and fire authorities said the boy fell into the river just north of the Ambridge-Aliquippa Bridge. He was with a group of children biking along the river and fell into a drop-off near a sandbar while trying to retrieve a lost shoe.

Emergency crews used a dog, a remote-controlled vehicle similar to a submarine and dive teams to find the boy. Authorities identified the body based on a clothing description and photographs provided to them.

“Our heartfelt condolences to the family,” fire Chief Robert Gottschalk said.
