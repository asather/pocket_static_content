Sources: Dave Turgeon to be named Class AA Altoona manager

Dave Turgeon, the Pirates’ coordinator of instruction since 2015, is expected to be named the new manager for the team’s Class AA affiliate in Altoona, sources told the Post-Gazette.

Turgeon began his coaching career in the Indians system as manager of the Burlington Indians in 2000 before moving on to the college ranks for stops at Boston College, UConn, Duke and Virginia Tech. He joined the Pirates’ organization in 2010. 

Turgeon would replace Michael Ryan as skipper for the Curve. Ryan and the club parted ways in September after a 69-71 season.
