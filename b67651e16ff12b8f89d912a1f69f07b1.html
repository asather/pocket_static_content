Woman, 94, injured when hit by Access vehicle in Oakland

An elderly woman was injured Wednesday when she was hit by an Access bus in Oakland.

Pittsburgh police said the 94-year-old woman was injured in the leg when she was struck around 9:40 a.m. in the intersection of Fifth Avenue and North Dithridge Street.

The woman was taken to UPMC Presbyterian in serious condition.

The driver remained on scene.
