Girl on bike struck by vehicle in Sheraden

Police are investigating after a young bicyclist was hit by a vehicle Wednesday night in Sheraden.

Emergency officials responded around 7:35 p.m. to the intersection of Ashlyn Street and Zephyr Avenue for reports of a crash. Authorities found a girl had been struck by a car while riding her bike through the intersection, police said. The age of the girl was not released.

The driver stayed at the scene. 

The girl was alert and talking to medics, police said, and was taken to a local hospital in stable condition.
