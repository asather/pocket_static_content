Police find possible human remains at illegal dump site in Plum

A Plum police officer patrolling an area on Sunday notorious for illegal dumping and off-road vehicle use found a garbage bag that contained “what appeared to be human remains,” according to Allegheny County police.

In a statement, police said the officer was in the 200 block Repp Road around 10:20 a.m. and was drawn to the trash bag because it “appeared suspicious.” 

The remains have been sent to the Allegheny County Medical Examiner’s Office for further examination.

County homicide detectives are handling the investigation.
