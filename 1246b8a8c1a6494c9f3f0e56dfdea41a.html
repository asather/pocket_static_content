Hidden camera found in West Penn Hospital employee restroom

The discovery of a hidden camera in an employee restroom at West Penn Hospital in Bloomfield prompted an “immediate investigation” by hospital officials.

“The culprit in this criminal act has been identified and will be prosecuted accordingly,” said a written statement by an Allegheny Health Network spokesperson Thursday.

The suspect’s name is unknown at this time.

Allegheny Health Network officials did not specify when the camera was found, only that it had been “recently” discovered.

The health care company said it is working with law enforcement to retrieve any photographs or video recorded by the camera and “will be notifying identifiable individuals who have been victimized by the crime,” the statement said.
