Man shot in Arlington

A man was shot Saturday afternoon in Arlington.

Police were notified via Shotspotter of multiple rounds fired in the 2900 block of Arlington Ave just before 1 p.m.

Shortly afterward, a man with a gunshot wound to the shoulder came into the Mt. Oliver police station. He was then taken to a hospital by private transportation.

The investigation is continuing.
