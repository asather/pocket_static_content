WPIAL 4A-3A roundup: Elizabeth Forward prevails in two overtimes

Vernon Settles threw a 10-yard touchdown pass to Chase Whatton in the second overtime to lead Elizabeth Forward to a 20-14 victory Friday night against South Allegheny in an Interstate Conference game played at Elizabeth Forwardâ€™s Warrior Stadium.

The Warriors (4-0, 3-0), ranked No. 2 in Class 3A, scored twice in the first half to take a 14-0 lead on a 9-yard run by DaVontay Brownfiel and a 19-yard pass from Evan Lewis to Zach Boyd.

The Gladiators (2-3, 2-3) evened the game with two fourth-quarter touchdowns. The first came on a 16-yard pass from Jett Jones to Ethan Kirkwood. The other came on a 61-yard hook-and-lateral play from Eric Wehrer to Jones to Antonio Epps.

More Class 3A

North Catholic 28, South Park 2: Joe Prentice threw for two touchdowns and ran for two more as the No. 3 ranked Trojans (5-0) defeated the visiting Eagles (3-1) in a non-conference game. Prentice threw for 221 yards and connected with Nicholas Maher and Liam Straub on touchdown passes. He also scored twice on one-yard runs.

Southmoreland 24, Yough 0: Zach Cernuto connected with Jason Bloom and Isaac Trout on touchdown passes to lead the visiting Scotties (2-2, 1-2) to an Interstate Conference shutout of the Cougars (1-3, 0-3).

Avonworth 48, Ambridge 6: Ian Syam rushed for 267 yards on 26 carries and scored four touchdowns as the host Antelopes (3-2, 2-1) dominated the Bridgers (0-5, 0-3) in the Northwestern Six Conference.

Class 4A

Thomas Jefferson 58, Laurel Highlands 0: Conner Murga rushed for 140 yards and 5 touchdowns as the top-ranked Jaguars (3-0, 3-0) dominated the visiting Mustangs (1-3, 1-2) in the Big Eight Conference.

McKeesport 12, West Mifflin 7: Milton Campos connected on field goals of 31 and 32 yards to lead the Tigers (4-1, 3-1) to a Big Eight Conference win over the visiting Titans (1-4, 1-4). The Tigers also scored on a 48-yard run by Caleb Reist.

Mars 48, Knoch 13: Teddy Ruffner rushed for six touchdowns as the Planets (4-1, 4-1) pounded the host Knights (0-5, 0-5) in the Greater Allegheny Conference. Ruffner has 17 touchdowns in five games.

Trinity 41, Washington 0: The Hillers (3-2) claimed city bragging rights in their shutout of the Little Prexies (3-2) in this non-conference game. Connor Roberts threw for 158 yards and 3 touchdowns. Micah Finley ran for two scores and caught one.
