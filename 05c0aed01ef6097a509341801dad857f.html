Dave Matthews Band coming to S&T Bank Music Park in July

The Dave Matthews Band, recent nominees for the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, will bring their summer tour to S&T Bank Music Park on July 3.

Tickets go on sale at 10 a.m. Feb. 21 at Livenation.com, but an online ticket presale for members of the DMB Warehouse Fan Association will begin on Thursday, Jan. 23, at 10 a.m. at www.warehouse.davematthewsband.com.

Citi cardmembers will have access to purchase presale tickets beginning Tuesday, Feb. 18, at 10 a.m. through Thursday, Feb. 20 at 10 p.m. at www.citientertainment.com.

Dave Matthews Band is committing to help plant one million trees as a collective effort with The Dreaming Tree Wines and DocuSign. The band invites concertgoers to join them in this mission by contributing an optional donation of $2 per ticket to plant a tree with The Nature Conservancy’s Plant a Billion Trees campaign – a major forest restoration effort with a goal of planting a billion trees around the world by 2025. For further details, visit plantabillion.org/DMB.
