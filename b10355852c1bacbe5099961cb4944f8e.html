Suspect charged with abuse of McKees Rocks corpse reported to have been victim’s boyfriend

A man charged with abuse of a corpse following the discovery of a woman’s dismembered body in a refrigerator in a McKees Rocks apartment complex was the victim’s boyfriend, according to Post-Gazette news partner KDKA-TV.

Daryl Jones, 40, has been put behind bars at the Allegheny County jail, following the discovery of the remains of Kristy Jefferson, 38, of McKees Rocks by residents of the apartment building.

The victim was identified by the Allegheny County Medical Examiner’s Office. Ms. Jefferson was initially reported missing April 29, Allegheny County police said.

The cause and manner of Ms. Jefferson’s death are still under investigation.

Neighbors reported Mr. Jones and Ms. Jefferson fought often, and Mr. Jones had a violent past, according to a criminal complaint filed by police.

Building residents found the partially dismembered body Monday morning after a strong smell began emanating from an unplugged refrigerator in the second-floor hallway. Police were called to the scene.

Mr. Jones was arraigned early Tuesday and is being held without bond. His preliminary hearing has been scheduled for July.

County homicide detectives are investigating and are asking anyone with information to call 1-833-ALL-TIPS.
