1 dead, 1 critical after shooting in Brighton Heights

Two men were shot — one fatally — early Sunday in Brighton Heights, according to Pittsburgh police.

Police responded to Shotspotter alerts at 1:45 a.m. in the 1200 block of Woods Run Avenue.

At the scene, first responders found a male shot with a gunshot wound to the chest. He died after being taken to a hospital.

Police said another male gunshot victim arrived at a hospital by private vehicle. He is in critical condition.

No other information about the case was immediately available.
