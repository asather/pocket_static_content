Rochester man gets decade in prison for recording sex with 14-year-old girl

A Beaver County man is headed to federal prison for a decade for possessing videos of himself having sex with a teenage girl.

U.S. District Judge Joy Flowers Conti imposed that term on Elijah Winchester, 23, of Rochester.

Winchester had previously pleaded guilty to possession of material depicting the sexual exploitation of a child related to explicit videos found on his phone in 2018.

He was 21 at the time, and she was 14.

The case began in the spring of 2018 when police in Beaver acquired evidence that Winchester was having sex with the girl. They questioned him and he denied it.

But when officers received more evidence, they got a search warrant for his phone.

On it they found 14 sex videos involving the girl. The faces of Winchester and the victim were visible in some and in others the girl was identified by her navel piercing.

All of the videos were created on May 16 and 17 in 2018.

In addition to the 10-year prison term, Winchester will be on probation for seven years.
