KeKe Kitchen, Bella's Smokin' BBQ closed by health department

The Allegheny County Health Department has ordered two area eateries to close because they were operating without health permits.

They include KeKe Kitchen in the Garfield section of the city, and Bella’s Smokin’ BBQ, a mobile food truck operating on William Penn Highway in Monroeville.

Bella’s also was cited for several food safety violations, including holding food at unsafe temperatures and an insufficient potable water supply.

Inspection reports for permitted food facilities in the county are available at webapps.achd.net/Restaurant/.
