Two men injured in Stowe shooting

Two men who were shot in Stowe Friday afternoon are in stable condition.

Police were notified around 4:10 p.m. of the shooting in the 1200 block of Stowe Avenue. First responders found the two victims with gunshot wounds to the “lower extremities,” police said. They were transported to area hospitals.

County police are investigating the incident.
