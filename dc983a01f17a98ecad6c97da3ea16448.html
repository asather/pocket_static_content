Tuned In: WTAE returns to DirecTV

WTAE returned to AT&T’s DirecTV and AT&T Now Sunday after Channel 4 parent company Hearst reached a multi-year agreement for retransmisson consent with AT&T. Terms of the agreement were not disclosed.

WTAE had been off DirecTV since Friday in a rerun of a retransmission dispute from 2017.

WPXI and Verizon’s FiOS TV resolved a retransmission spat Saturday night.

The only unresolved situation from a week of disruptions to Pittsburgh cable/satellite viewers is Comcast’s decision Tuesday to drop WPXI’s PCNC channel.
