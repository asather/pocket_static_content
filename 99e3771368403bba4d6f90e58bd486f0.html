High school basketball roundup: Chartiers Valley narrowly survives scares versus Mt. Lebanon

Chartiers Valley has a chance to come away with the No. 1 seed in the WPIAL Class 5A playoffs when the pairings are announced on Tuesday.

On Monday night, the Colts made their final case for the top spot.

Brayden Reynolds scored a game-high 22 points and Jared Goldstrom added 12 as Section 2 champion Chartiers Valley (17-4) overcame a 13-point halftime deficit to eke out a 56-51 road victory over Class 6A No. 3 Mt. Lebanon (17-5).
