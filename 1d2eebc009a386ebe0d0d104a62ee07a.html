Allegheny County officials to provide update on primary day preparations

Allegheny County election officials will provide an update on Facebook Live today on the preparations underway for the June 2 primary.

Officials said Tuesday that staffers in the elections division have been working around the clock to relieve a backlog of thousands of mail-in ballot applications from voters.

Election officials will hold a Facebook Live discussion from 11:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. today to outline how their preparations are going.

Jerry Tyskiewicz, director of administrative services for the county, and Elections Division Manager David Voye will lead the discussion and afterwards will take questions from the media and the public.

County officials said that as of Tuesday about 80,000 ballots were still waiting to be sent out to voters who applied for one either online or through regular mail.
