Mayor Peduto announces the passing of his mother, Eva

Mayor Bill Peduto announced the death of his mother, Eva Maria, who died Wednesday morning.

Mr. Peduto took to Twitter later that evening to inform the public about his loss.

“This morning I lost the most inspirational person of my life, my mom, Eva Maria (Zarroli) Peduto,” he wrote.

Ms. Peduto was 92, and for years lived in Scott, where Mr. Peduto grew up. The mayor has said in the past that his mother was born and raised in Carnegie.

Mr. Peduto’s father died in 1998, according to his Twitter feed.
