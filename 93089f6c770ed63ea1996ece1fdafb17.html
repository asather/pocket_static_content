Police charge man in connection to robbery in Dormont

Allegheny County police on Tuesday announced they have arrested and charged a man in connection to a robbery at a Dollar Bank in Dormont. 

Police identified 51-year-old Angelo Koupas of Carnegie as the actor. Police say he is charged with robbery, theft, receiving stolen property and terroristic threats.

On Friday, Dormont police were called to the Dollar Bank on the 3000 block of West Liberty Avenue for reports of a robbery. Dormont police requested assistance from county police.

When detectives arrived at the scene, witnesses told detectives the actor produced a note demanding cash and stated he had a bomb, according to police. The teller then handed the man an undetermined amount of cash, and the actor fled the scene.
