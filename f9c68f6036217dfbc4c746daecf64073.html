Pittsburgh film 'Rehabilitation of the Hill' available on demand

The Film Lab by the Numbers from Steeltown Entertainment Project on Vimeo.

“Rehabilitation of the Hill,” a feature film shot in Pittsburgh in the spring of 2017, has just been released on Vimeo.

Written and directed by Demetrius Wren, the film tells the story of two friends coping with redevelopment in the Hill District.

Wren, who worked with the Steeltown Film Lab, taught filmmaking classes at the University of Pittsburgh. His students were involved in the production. Steeltown also conducted skilled trade workshops in an effort to create more diversity when TV and film projects come to Pittsburgh.

“Rehabilitation of the Hill” stars Harry Lennix, Annika Marks, Christina Wren and Rita Gregory. It is available to rent on Vimeo for $2.99 or purchase for $5.99.
