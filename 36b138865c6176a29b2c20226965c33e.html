One dead in Wilkinsburg shooting

This story was updated at 11:10 p.m. on June 7

One person died Sunday in a shooting in Wilkinsburg.

Emergency dispatchers were notified around 4:36 p.m. of the shooting on the 1600 block of Laketon Road. First responders found a deceased male where the street intersects with Maple Way.

The medical examiner is working to identify the victim.

Officials said he was near a set of steps with two other people when a man approached from behind and fired multiple times. The victim was hit in the head and torso.

There is no known motive for the shooting, and police are trying to identify suspects.

Allegheny County homicide detectives are investigating the incident. Police asked anyone with information to call their anonymous tip line at 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477).
