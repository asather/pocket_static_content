Woman hospitalized after gunshot wound in Spring Hill

Pittsburgh police are investigating the shooting of a woman in Spring Hill just before dawn Saturday.

Police said they received a call reporting a female shot in the 100 block of Rhine Place just after 5:15 a.m. They found the victim with a gunshot wound in the leg and she was taken by emergency medical services to to the hospital in stable condition.
