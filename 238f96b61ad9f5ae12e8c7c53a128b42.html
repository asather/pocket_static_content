Two men arrested after drug investigation

Police arrested two men in Lawrenceville on Thursday in connection to a drug investigation.

William Davis, 62, of Lawrenceville, and 41-year-old Anthony Glover, of East McKeesport, were taken into custody after detectives observed alleged drug activity at an apartment in the 400 block of 44th Street in Lawrenceville. 

Police obtained a warrant and entered the Lawrenceville residence on Thursday and found crack cocaine, raw heroin and drug packaging materials.

Authorities also got a warrant to search Mr. Glover’s residence in the 500 block of Larimer Street and found a loaded Glock .45 pistol.

Police found a locked door in the home leading to Mr. Glover’s “man cave,” a police affidavit says. Investigators unlocked the “man cave” and located a black digital scale and a plastic bag containing .45 caliber ammunition, according to the affidavit. 

Both men face charges of possession of a controlled substance, intent to deliver a controlled substance, possession of drug paraphernalia and criminal conspiracy. Mr. Glover also faces gun charges.

A preliminary hearing is set for July 6.
