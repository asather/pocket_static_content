Police: East Pittsburgh man arrested after authorities find 5,000 stamp bags of heroin

Police arrested an East Pittsburgh man after authorities found about 5,000 stamp bags containing heroin and other items at a Wilkins home.

Authorities said the drug bust at the house on Cline Avenue was the culmination of a six-month investigation, Post-Gazette news partner KDKA-TV reported.

Richard Broadus, 48, was arrested and charged after police found the heroin as well as several large bags of marijuana and other drugs at the residence.

A preliminary hearing is set for Sept. 24.
