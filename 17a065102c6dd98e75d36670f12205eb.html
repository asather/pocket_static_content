Beaver Valley interchange to close on Pa. Turnpike weekdays for 3 weeks

The Pennsylvania Turnpike will close the Beaver Valley interchange on weekdays for three weeks beginning Monday.

The closure is needed so crews can reconstruct the shoulders and resurface the ramps to and from the interchange, the turnpike said.

Work will begin on Monday and stop Friday evenings to allow traffic to use the interchange over the weekend. The work depends on the weather and crews may work into the weekend, if necessary.

The project is scheduled to be finished by Oct. 9.

Motorists traveling on the turnpike who want to exit at Beaver Valley to be diverted to the New Castle interchange and then detoured to Route 18.

The turnpike also recommends motorists who want to enter the turnpike at Beaver Valley to use New Castle by following routes 18 and 351.
