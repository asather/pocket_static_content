Police charge West Mifflin teen with non-fatal shooting in Duquesne

Police on Friday arrested a 17-year-old from West Mifflin for shooting a 19-year-old multiple times on Feb. 16 in Duquesne.

Terril Leverett is charged with attempted homicide, aggravated assault and gun offenses.

He was arrested at his house by Allegheny County police and West Mifflin police.

The victim was shot around 12:31 p.m. in the 600 block of Grant Avenue. He took himself to the hospital, police said.

At the time, police said the victim approached two other men on Grant Avenue when one of them pulled out a gun and fired.

Anyone with information can call police at 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477).
