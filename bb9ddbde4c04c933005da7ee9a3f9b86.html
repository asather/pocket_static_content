Motorcyclist killed in Blairsville identified

The driver of a motorcycle involved in a fatal one-vehicle accident Friday morning in Blairsville, Indiana County, has been identified as 22-year-old Christian Shugars, the Allegheny County Medical Examiner’s office reported.

Mr. Shugars, of Blairsville, died in a Pittsburgh hospital a little more than hour following the 10:03 a.m. accident on Pizza Barn Road.

The cause of death was determined to be blunt force trauma to the head and trunk. The manner of death is an accident, the medical examiner’s office said.
