Man killed in Knoxville shooting identified

The identity of a man who was killed early Wednesday in a shooting in Pittsburgh’s Knoxville neighborhood has been released by the Allegheny County Medical Examiner’s Office.

Clinton Jamar Miller, 40, of Pittsburgh, died at UPMC Mercy before 3 a.m. from a gunshot wound to his stomach, the medical examiner’s office reported Thursday.

Police responded just before 1 a.m. to reports of a shooting that morning in the 80 block of Knox Avenue, authorities said.

No arrests have been made and police are investigating.
