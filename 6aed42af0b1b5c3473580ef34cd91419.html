Pittsburgh holds 1st meeting for Community Task Force on Police Reform

The city of Pittsburgh hosted its first Community Task Force on Police Reform meeting on Monday evening.

The task force, which was announced on Wednesday by Mayor Bill Peduto, livestreamed the meeting on the city’s official Twitter page as it began at 5:30 p.m.

The mayor’s office said during its announcement that the group will be tasked with reviewing current police practices, police-community relations and the state of safety in communities.
