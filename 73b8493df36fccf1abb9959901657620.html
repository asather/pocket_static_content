Two found dead inside Hazelwood home

This story will be updated. 

Pittsburgh police are investigating the deaths of a man and woman whose bodies were found Wednesday morning in a Hazelwood home.

A family member who was doing a welfare check called 911 after discovering the bodies around 10 a.m. at the home on the 300 block of Flowers Avenue.

Authorities have not released the identity of either person, nor did they release information about their relationship.
