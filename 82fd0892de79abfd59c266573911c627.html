Joe Rogan coming to PPG Paints Arena in May

Comedian and podcast sensation Joe Rogan will bring the Sacred Clown Tour to PPG Paints Arena on May 16.

Tickets go on sale at 10 a.m. Friday at JoeRogan.com.

Rogan, who has been doing stand-up for 25 years, is the host of “The Joe Rogan Experience,” the most popular podcast online.
