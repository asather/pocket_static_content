3 people flown to Pittsburgh hospitals after Westmoreland County crash

Three people were flown to Pittsburgh hospitals after a crash in Westmoreland County Tuesday.

The injured included two men and one woman who were involved in a two-car crash on state Route 380 in Washington Township, according to Post-Gazette news partner KDKA-TV.

Their conditions were unknown.
