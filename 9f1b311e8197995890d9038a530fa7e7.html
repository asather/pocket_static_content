Allegheny County sheriff sales postponed

The Allegheny County sheriff sales have been postponed due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

April’s sale has been postponed until June 1, according to an April 4 post on the sheriff’s Facebook page.

Additionally, the May sale has been postponed by a court order until July 6.
