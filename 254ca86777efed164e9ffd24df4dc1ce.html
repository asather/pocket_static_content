Take a shot in our Trout Photo Contest

Share your fishing story, and a photo to prove it’s true, in the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette Trout Photo Contest.

Submit high-resolution digital images (1MB minimum) including name, age (for minors under 18 years of age), town, county and waters fished, details of the catch, phone number (not for publication) and photographer’s name and town. Email to fishingreport@post-gazette.com by  May 17. Trout photo submissions will be posted at post-gazette.com. Winning anglers in the categories Big Fish, Kidstuff and Artsy Shots will be interviewed for a feature article published May 21 online and May 24 in print.
