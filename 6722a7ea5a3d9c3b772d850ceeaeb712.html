Washington County woman dies after being run over by tractor

A Washington County woman died Sunday after she was run over by a tractor in Buffalo Township.

The state police identified the woman as Elizabeth McCord, 60, of Buffalo Township.

Ms. McCord was hit by a tractor at the intersection of Jolly School Road and Mounts Road just after 3 p.m., police said.

She was pronounced dead at Washington Hospital at 4:10 p.m.

State police are investigating the incident.
