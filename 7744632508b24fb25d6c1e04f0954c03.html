Elderly man dies in house fire in Washington County

An elderly man died in a house fire in Washington County Tuesday morning.

The county coroner’s office said Gary B. Bigler, 85, was pronounced dead at the scene on McElree Road in North Franklin. The fire was reported around 5:45 a.m.

The Pennsylvania State Police fire marshal’s office is investigating the cause of the blaze.
