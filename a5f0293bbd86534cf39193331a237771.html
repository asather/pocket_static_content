Biden will visit Shanksville on Sept. 11

Like he did twice as vice president, Joe Biden will travel to Shanksville this Sept. 11 to commemorate the anniversary of the 2001 terrorist attacks.

His presidential campaign announced Wednesday that he'd make the trip alongside his wife, Jill, to Shanksville -- a few miles south of the Flight 93 National Memorial.
