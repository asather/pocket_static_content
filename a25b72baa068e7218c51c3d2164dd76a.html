Where to find answers on COVID-19

One of the most comprehensive lists of local COVID-19 resources can be found at The Pittsburgh Foundation website. Here are some that focus on answering questions related to physical and mental health, testing, volunteering and meeting needs during a pandemic.

1Hood Media and Gateway Medical Society’s “Ask a Black Doctor”: Gateway on Facebook or www.facebook.com/1HoodMedia

Pittsburgh Cares: www.pittsburghcares.org/covid19

Be Well Pittsburgh: bewellpgh.org/2020/05/19/covid-19-resources

Take Action Mon Valley: www.tamv.org

The Wellness Collective: www.facebook.com/thepittsburghwellnesscollective and Allegheny County Resources & Support Amid COVID-19 Facebook group 

Urban League of Greater Pittsburgh: http://ulpgh.org/

Center for Disease Control list of community and faith-based organizations: www.cdc.gov/coronavirus

Testing sites in Western Pennsylvania (appointments required): www.health.pa.gov

PA Dept. of Health with translations in French, Spanish, Chinese and Nepali: www.health.pa

Allegheny Health Network: www.ahn.org

UPMC: www.upmc.com

The Hospital Health System of Pennsylvania: www.haponline.org

412 Food Rescue: 412foodrescue.org

Greater Pittsburgh Community Food Bank: www.pittsburghfoodbank.org

Feed the Hood Pittsburgh: www.facebook.com/feedthehoodpittsburgh

compiled by Sharon Eberson, Post-Gazette
