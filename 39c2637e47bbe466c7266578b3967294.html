Pedestrian in serious condition after being trapped under vehicle in Strip

A woman was taken to a hospital in serious condition after a driver struck her with a car in the Strip District.

Police, fire and EMS responded around 4 p.m. to the incident at the intersection of 17th Street and Liberty Avenue. The woman was trapped under the vehicle, but alert and talking to medics.

First responders placed airbags under the vehicle to lift it, and the woman was extricated after about 10 minutes, police said. She was transported by medic 14 to a local hospital.

Police are investigating the incident, and said the driver stayed on scene and was cooperative.
