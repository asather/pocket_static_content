Gun found on kindergarten student at North Side elementary school

A kindergarten student at Allegheny K-5 in the North Side brought a gun to school Friday morning, a Pittsburgh Public Schools spokeswoman said.

District spokeswoman Ebony Pugh said a school staff member found the gun while assisting at the building’s metal detector. 

A report was submitted to Childline, the state’s child abuse hotline, according to Ms. Pugh. Families will receive notification about the incident though the district’s parent notification system, she said.

The incident remains under investigation.
