WATCH: NTSB releases footage of turnpike crash investigation

The National Transportation Safety Board has released footage of its investigators surveying the damage from Sunday’s deadly crash on the Pennsylvania Turnpike.

The crash, which occurred near mile marker 86 in Mount Pleasant, Westmoreland County, killed five people and injured dozens more.

The team of NTSB investigators will spend about a week in the area while conducting its report.

On Sunday, a passenger bus traveling westbound from New York and headed for Ohio lost control and crashed, causing a chain-reaction that involved three tractor-trailers and a Mercedes car.
