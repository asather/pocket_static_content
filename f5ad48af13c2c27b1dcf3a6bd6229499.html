Pedestrian injured in hit and run in Homewood

A 47-year-old man was injured in a hit-and-run accident Thursday night in Homewood, according to Pittsburgh police spokesman Chris Togneri.

Police responded to the 7300 block of Frankstown Avenue around 8:35 p.m. and found that a man had been hit by a car, and the driver had left the scene.

The unidentified man was taken to a local hospital with severe lacerations to his face and injuries to his arms and shoulder, police said. He is in stable condition.

Authorities later towed a vehicle suspected to have been involved in the accident after finding it on Dearborn Street in Garfield.

Charges have yet to be filed.
