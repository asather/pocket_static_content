Fire destroys Monroeville business

Monroeville firefighters remained on the scene of a fire that reportedly destroyed the Overlook Lounge on Saturday afternoon.

The fire was reported shortly before noon at the bar-restaurant located at 1131 James St.

Post-Gazette news partner KDKA-TV reported the business was destroyed. There was no word on injuries.
