Primanti Bros. offering discount sandwiches if you shred your ex's photo on Valentine's Day

Valentine’s Day may bring up a lot of emotions for those of us who have no one to share it with, but Primanti Bros. is here to help ease the pain.

The sandwich chain is offering a deal to patrons who bring in a photo of their ex-lover, and toss it into an industrial mobile shredding truck.

The ‘Shred your Ex’ special happening Friday comes with a coupon for half-off any classic Primanti Bros. sandwich.

In a tweet posted Wednesday, Primanti’s encouraged visitors to ditch their “better half.”
