Glen Hazel assisted living center reports 29 residents, staff test positive for COVID-19

Twenty-nine residents and staff have tested positive for COVID-19 at Kane Community Living Center in Glen Hazel, it confirmed on Tuesday.

Eighteen residents and 11 workers at the assisted living center have contracted the virus as of April 6.

A staff member had previously tested positive at the facility on March 25. Two days later, on March 27, two residents tested positive.
