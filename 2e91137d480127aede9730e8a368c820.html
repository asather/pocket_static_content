Man hospitalized after Uptown shooting

Pittsburgh Police responded Saturday afternoon to a shooting in Uptown that left one man hospitalized.

The shooting occurred around 12:15 on the 100 block of Seneca Street. Shortly afterward a male victim came to a local hospital with a gunshot wound to the back, according to police, who said his condition was unknown.

Police searched a residence near the shooting and took a woman to police headquarters for questioning.

The investigation is ongoing.
