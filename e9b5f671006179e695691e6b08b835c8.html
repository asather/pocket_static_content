Authorities ID victim of fatal shooting in Garfield

Authorities have identified a Pittsburgh man who was fatally shot in the city’s Garfield neighborhood on Monday.

Trai Wilson, 36, died Tuesday after sustaining multiple gunshot wounds in a shooting in the area of Dearborn Street, according to city police and the Allegheny County medical examiner’s office.

 

Police said officers responded to the 5100 block of Dearborn around 11 p.m. after receiving a ShotSpotter alert of multiple rounds fired in the area.

Shortly after, Mr. Wilson arrived at Allegheny General Hospital in a private vehicle and was in critical condition. He died the next day.

No arrests have been made.

Detectives with the city’s Major Crimes Unit continue to investigate.
