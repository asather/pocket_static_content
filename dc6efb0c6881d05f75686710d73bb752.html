Police investigating drive-by shooting in Braddock Hills

A family of five traveling in Braddock Hills Friday afternoon came under fire from another vehicle during a drive-by shooting.

A 1-year-old boy in the backseat suffered “superficial” injuries from broken glass, according to Allegheny County Police. The child’s two brothers, ages 5 and 6, were also in the back seat and were not injured.

The children’s mother and father, both age 27, were also uninjured when their car was shot multiple times just before 4:17 p.m. at the intersection of Yost Boulevard and Brinton Road.

Both remain unidentified. 

The woman was driving a white Kia Optima on Brinton Road when unknown individuals began shooting at the car, according to county police.

Police said they believe the adult male in the passenger’s seat was the intended target. 

No arrests have been made.

Yost Boulevard was temporarily shut down between Brinton Road and Ardmore Boulevard, according to emergency dispatchers.

Anyone with information is asked to call the county police tip line 1-833-ALL-TIPS. Callers can remain anonymous.
