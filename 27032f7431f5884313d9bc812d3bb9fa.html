Maura Tierney, Alex Neustaedter among new additions to Showtime's 'Rust,' which will shoot in Pittsburgh

Emmy nominee and Golden Globe winner Maura Tierney (“The Affair”) is joining the cast of the Showtime series, “Rust,” which begins shooting in Pittsburgh at the end of the month.

Tierney plays Grace Poe, whose relationship with the police chief of a small Rust Belt town in Western Pennsylvania complicates his involvement with a murder investigation. Her son (Alex Neustaedter, “Colony”) is the accused. It was previously announced that Jeff Daniels (“The Newsroom”) will play Del Harris, the chief.

Also just announced as series regulars are Emmy and Tony nominee Bill Camp (“The Outsider”), Tony winner David Alvarez (the upcoming film “West Side Story”) and Julia Mayorga.

Showtime will produce 10 episodes of the family drama.

“Rust” is based on author Philipp Meyer’s 2009 novel “American Rust,” set in the fictional Mon Valley town of Buell, Fayette County.
