Greensburg police looking for missing woman and her son

Greensburg police are searching for a missing woman and her one-year-old son.

Aziyza King, 37, and her son, Zayn, were last seen March 6 at 10:00 a.m. leaving their Grove Street home in Greensburg. She told a neighbor that she was stopping at the Dollar General on South Main Street, but police said surveillance video shows she never entered the store.

Ms. King was last seen wearing a black jacket and black pants. She has family in the Harrisburg area, but has not been in contact with them recently, police said. She does not own a vehicle.

There is no photo available of her son.
