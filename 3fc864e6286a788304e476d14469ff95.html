Pirates broadcaster Bob Walk recovering from COVID-19, hopes to return by Opening Day

Pirates broadcaster Bob Walk tested positive for the coronavirus and is recovering, his partner, Greg Brown, said on Saturday’s Pirates telecast.

Walk testing positive was later confirmed by the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette.

“A couple of rough days, but he says he’s feeling great,” said Brown, who was calling play-by-play on AT&T SportsNet at the time. “You know Walkie, he’s a warrior.”

Brown said the Pirates hope to have Walk back in the broadcast booth for opening day Friday, July 24 against the Cardinals at Busch Stadium.

If the rules for broadcasters are anything like they are for players, Walk would theoretically have to test negative twice before being allowed back.
