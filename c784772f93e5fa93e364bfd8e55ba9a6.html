Black Crowes tour is postponed until 2021

The Black Crowes reunion tour has been pushed back to 2021, the band posted on its Instagram.

The Robinson brothers, reuniting for the 30th Anniversary Shake Your Money Maker Tour, were scheduled to play S&T Bank Music Park on Aug. 1.

They ask that you hold on to your tickets for a future date.
