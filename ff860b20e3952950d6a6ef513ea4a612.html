State high school basketball rankings: Fox Chapel moves to No. 5

Fox Chapel is the only undefeated boys basketball team in the WPIAL, and the perfect start has earned the Foxes statewide attention.

The newest state boys basketball rankings were released Monday and Fox Chapel is all the way up to the No. 5 spot in Class 6A. The Foxes were No. 7 last week, but moved up two spots. The rankings are compiled weekly by Mike Bullock and the Sunbury Daily Item.

Fox Chapel is 12-0 and has won some games recently despite missing two of its top players. Arnold Vento and Sam Brown have been out with ankle injuries. Sophomore Eli Yofan has stepped up and led the Foxes to decisive wins. Yofan averages 15 points a game.

Fox Chapel’s next game is Tuesday night at Connellsville.

Elsewhere in the rankings, Vincentian is the only WPIAL team to hold a No. 1 spot. The Royals are atop the Class 1A rankings and one of the biggest games Tuesday night in the WPIAL has Vincentian playing host to Cornell, No. 4 in Class 4A.
