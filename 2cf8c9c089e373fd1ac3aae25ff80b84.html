Code Orange will release new unplugged album on Friday

Code Orange will release “Under the Skin,” a new digital live album, on Friday.

The Pittsburgh hardcore / metal band recorded the stripped-down album in MTV Unplugged fashion earlier this summer during a Twitch livetream with enhanced audio.

It includes a captivating cover of the Alice in Chains song “Down In A Hole,” sung by guitarist Reba Meyers, that can be found on the band’s YouTube channel with performance footage shot and edited by Sunny Singh of hate5six.

Code Orange has also launched limited edition Under the Skin” merch including a DVD of the performance available for pre-order now at https://store.codeorangetoth.com.

In the second week of March, when the pandemic shut things down, the Grammy-nominated Pittsburgh, now fronted by drummer Jami Morgan, launched a new livestream era by taking to Twitch with an empty-venue performance at the Roxian in McKees Rocks. It coincided with the release of the band’s new fourth album “Underneath.”
