Police investigate non-fatal stabbing of woman, 22, in Hill District

A 22-year-old woman was stabbed in the abdomen early Saturday in the Hill District.

Pittsburgh police found the woman around 4 a.m. in the 2400 block of Bedford Avenue. 

She was conscious and alert, police said, and was taken to the hospital in stable condition.

Police are investigating.
