Firefighters respond to early morning blaze in Beaver County

Emergency officials responded to a fire early Friday morning that erupted at a home in Beaver County.

The Hopewell Police Department and the Hopewell Volunteer Fire Department responded just before 3 a.m. to a home in the 1500 block of Wimpole Street in Hopewell Township. Officers learned residents had safely made it out of the house by the time they arrived, police said.

No injuries were reported.

The home was severely damaged, police said, but the extent is unknown.

A state fire marshal is investigating the cause of the fire. 

Further information was not immediately available.
