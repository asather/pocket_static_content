Man killed in McKees Rocks after gunman fires into parked vehicle

A 24-year man who was sitting in a parked vehicle was shot and killed late Saturday in McKees Rocks.

Allegheny County police said the man — identified by the medical examiner’s office as Kwame Washington of Pittsburgh — was inside the vehicle with a woman at 11:42 p.m. in the 300 block of Shingiss Street when an unknown male assailant approached the passenger side of their Chevrolet Equinox and fired into the vehicle.

Mr. Washington, who was sitting in the front passenger seat, was shot in the torso and pronounced dead at the scene.

Police said there are no suspects and motive is unclear.

Anyone with information concerning this incident is asked to call the Allegheny County Police tipline at 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477). Callers can remain anonymous.
