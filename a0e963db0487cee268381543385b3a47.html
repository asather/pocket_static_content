UPDATE: State police say missing Armstrong County teen has been located

A 13-year-old girl who was reported missing from her Armstrong County home has been located.

Pennsylvania State Police announced Tuesday on Twitter that they were seeking the teen, Melissa Erin Dunkle, who had been last seen Monday at 4 p.m. at her home in Parker.

Police tweeted late Tuesday morning that she had been located.

They provided no further information.
