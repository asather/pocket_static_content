Motorcyclist killed in Beechview crash

A motorcyclist was killed in a crash in Pittsburgh’s Beechview neighborhood Thursday morning.

The crash was reported around 6:40 a.m. on Crane Avenue near Brashear High School.

No other vehicles were involved. The victim’s identity has not been released.

The police collision unit is investigating the cause.
