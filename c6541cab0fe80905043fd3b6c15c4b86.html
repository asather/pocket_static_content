Girl, 15, wounded in Aliquippa shooting

A 15-year-old girl was flown to a hospital after being shot overnight in Aliquippa.

Aliquippa police responded at 1:20 a.m. Thursday to the Linmar Terrace public housing complex, where they found the teen with a gunshot wound.

The girl was flown to UPMC Presbyterian. Her condition has not been released.

State police were called to the scene for assistance and are investigating.
