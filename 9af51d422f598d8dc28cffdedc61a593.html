Clairton man arrested for distributing drugs

Allegheny County police arrested a Clairton man Thursday for his involvement in distributing heroin that may be laced with fentanyl and rock crack cocaine in the Mon Valley area.

When Damian Graves, 48, was taken into custody at 7:25 p.m. county narcotics detectives found 140 stamp bags of heroin/fentanyl and 16 rocks of crack cocaine in his possession. Additionally, detectives found 90 more stamp bags of both drugs hidden inside of a plastic clothes lint roller tube.

He is in the Allegheny County Jail. A preliminary hearing is scheduled for Jan. 22.
