Shiverr Shack on McKnight Road closed for health code violations

The Shiverr Shack mobile unit operating at 4529 McKnight Road was shut down by the Allegheny County Health Department for numerous health code violations.

Some of the problems included failure to wash and sanitize food contact surfaces and lack of an adequate water supply, hot water and hand washing facilities.

“No means of sanitizing shaved ice machine or equipment used in shaved ice making process,” either in the mobile unit or in the building used as a commissary, the inspection report said.

The unit also was operating without a permit, the report said.

The operator of the facility could not be reached.

Inspection reports for food facilities in the county are available at webapps.achd.net/Restaurant/.
