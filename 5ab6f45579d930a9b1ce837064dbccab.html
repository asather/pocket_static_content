Sewickley package delivery service cutting 91 jobs

A Los Angeles-based package delivery service is permanently furloughing 408 workers, including 91 in the Pittsburgh area, according to the state Department of Labor and Industry.

Greenwich Logistics LLC, doing business as 1-800Courier, said the Sewickley terminal jobs would be eliminated March 20. The four other cities affected by the furloughs are in California. Company representatives were not immediately available for comment Monday.
