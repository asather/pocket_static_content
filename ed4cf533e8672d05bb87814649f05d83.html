Kennedy's Ashley Marina will not be singing in the next round of 'America's Got Talent'

In reality television, it’s possible to be loved, yet lost.

Kennedy Township’s Ashley Marina, 12, will not be advancing to the live shows on NBC’s “America’s Got Talent.” The singer/songwriter who came back to impress even Simon Cowell earlier this year was part of Tuesday night’s Judge Cuts episode.

Talking to the panel via streamed interview on a gigantic screen, Ashley was given the bad news by Howie Mandel.

“I want to tell you that this decision was not unanimous,” he said. “And this is really hard for me to say but for now, it’s a ‘no.’”

Heidi Klum immediately chimed in: “Don’t give up because you’re great, OK?’” Sofia Vergara, the fourth judge, said she agreed.

Earlier in the show when “AGT” raced through video clips from 100 auditions, Vergara, who is married to Mt. Lebanon native Joe Manganiello, saw Ashley and remarked “I want to clap again for her.”
