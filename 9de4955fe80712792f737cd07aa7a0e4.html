Authorities searching for man who fell into Ohio River while fishing

Ohio Township police said they are searching for a 23-year-old man who fell into the Ohio River on Saturday night while fishing with friends.

Police on Sunday posted a message onto its Facebook page that says the man was fishing from a dock on Neville Island when he fell in and "was unable to be pulled out of the water," police said.
