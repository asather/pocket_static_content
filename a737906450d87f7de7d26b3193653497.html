Officials respond to 4-alarm fire in the Strip District

Fire and EMS responded Wednesday evening to a four-alarm fire in Pittsburghâ€™s Strip District.

The fire was reported on the 1700 block of Penn Avenue near 18th Street, dispatchers said. The call came in at 8:08 p.m.

Fire Chief Darryl Jones declared the fire under control around 9:20 p.m.

One firefighter was transported by EMS to Mercy Hospital due to a shoulder injury.

Heavy smoke and flames spilled from the third floor of the Yinzers in the Burgh sportswear store, eventually breaking through the roof. Officials said the building immediately adjacent to the store may also have been exposed to the fire.

The cause of the fire is under investigation by the Fire Investigation Unit.
