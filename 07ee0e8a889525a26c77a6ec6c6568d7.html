Foreigner cancels Juke Box Heroes 2020 Tour

The Juke Box Heroes 2020 Tour, a concert featuring the bands Foreigner, Kansas and Europe, which was scheduled for Aug. 2 at S&T Bank Music Park in Burgettstown, has been canceled.

"We were very much looking forward to seeing you all at what promised to be a memorable evening of classic rock hits with Kansas and Europe this summer," Foreigner said on the band's website Tuesday. "We are grateful that so many of you had purchased tickets and we were looking forward to the tour as much as you were. We will get through this together and can’t wait to see you all again as soon as safely possible."

The band’s site said LiveNation would be emailing ticket holders refund options.

Visit www.livenationentertainment.com/ticketrefund for more information.
