Homicide detectives investigate after man found stabbed in McKeesport

McKeesport police first responded around 6:05 p.m. to reports of a possible homicide at an apartment on the 500 block of Sinclair Street. When they discovered the dead man, they called the Allegheny County Police for assistance.
