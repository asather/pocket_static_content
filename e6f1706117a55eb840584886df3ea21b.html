Westmoreland County woman killed by bullet fired from outside her home

A Derry Township woman was killed Monday morning by a bullet apparently fired from outside her home, according to State Police. 

Authorities say that Tracy Marie Squib, 52, called 911 at around 4 a.m. in the bedroom of her Pandora Road home to report a wound in her chest. The wound was determined to be from a gunshot, one of multiple fired at her home from the outside as she slept, and she died shortly after being transported to a hospital.

Her death is being investigated as a homicide. 

“We’re very saddened by what took place here.” State Trooper Steve Limani said at a press conference outside Ms. Squib’s home. “Right now we’re trying very hard to put the pieces together of what transpired here at this residence. It’s very early in our investigation.”

Trooper Limani said that three other people were sleeping inside the home when the incident occurred. 

He also encouraged anyone with information, particularly others who live on Pandora Road, to report it to police at 724-832-3288.
