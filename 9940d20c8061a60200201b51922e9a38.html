Postponed Pirates-Reds games shift to doubleheaders in September

Details were announced Friday for the Piratesâ€™ two games in Cincinnati this past Saturday and Sunday that were postponed after a Reds player tested positive for the coronavirus.

They will be made up as part of two doubleheaders in September.

The first will be played on Friday, Sept. 4 at PNC Park beginning at 4:05 p.m. The Pirates will be the home team for Game 1, which will be the originally scheduled game. The Reds will be the home team for Game 2, which will be a makeup of Saturday, Aug. 15.

The second doubleheader will be played on Monday, Sept. 14 at Great American Ball Park beginning at 4:10 p.m. The Reds will be the home team in both games, with the first being the originally scheduled game and the second serving as the makeup for Sunday, Aug. 16.
