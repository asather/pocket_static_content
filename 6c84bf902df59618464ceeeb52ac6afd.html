Upper Lawrenceville fire leaves several homes damaged

Emergency crews responded to a 2-alarm fire in Upper Lawrenceville on Monday evening.

When firefighters arrived at the 5200 block of Wickliff Street at around 5:30 p.m., they saw flames coming from two separate structures. They were able to extinguish the fire safely in just over an hour.

No injuries were reported, though seven homes were damaged from the blaze.
