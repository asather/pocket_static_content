Suspect arrested in connection with November bank robbery in N. Versailles

A North Versailles man was arrested Tuesday on charges that he robbed a bank in the township last fall, according to authorities.

The Allegheny County Sheriff’s Office said D’el Mackey, 38, had been wanted on a federal warrant since Nov. 13 in connection with the robbery of Huntington Bank in the Great Valley Shopping Center near Walmart. 

Police said Mr. Mackey passed a note to the bank teller asking for money — and they obliged — before he fled. He was identified as wearing a yellow rain jacket, a black hat, white gloves, black pants and carrying a backpack in surveillance video. An arrest warrant was issued for Mr. Mackey less than seven hours after the incident, police said.

Authorities said they arrested him Tuesday at a friend’s house in the 500 block of Third Street in Clairton after learning where he was living, police said. He was arrested without incident and taken to FBI headquarters on the South Side to be interviewed.
