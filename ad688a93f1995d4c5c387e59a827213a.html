Teen surrenders to police in slaying of 16-year-old in Uniontown

A 17-year-old has surrendered to authorities in connection with the shooting death of another teen in Uniontown last week.

Jadan Shaw, of Uniontown, is charged with criminal homicide and weapons violations in the Wednesday night slaying of a 16-year-old boy in the victim’s home on Lenox Street.

Police issued a warrant for Jadan’s arrest late last week and he turned himself in to police Friday night, Uniontown investigators said.

Jadan is being held without bond in the Fayette County Prison. A preliminary hearing is scheduled for Aug. 11, according to court records.
