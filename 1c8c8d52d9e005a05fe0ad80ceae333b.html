Man claiming to have bomb robs Dormont bank

A man claiming to have a bomb is sought by police after robbing a bank Friday in Dormont.

Allegheny County police on Monday released surveillance footage captured from inside the Dollar Bank on West Liberty Avenue.

The suspect, who was wearing a brown wig and floppy hat produced, robbed the bank around 6:40 p.m. when he produced a note that demanded cash and said he had a bomb.

The suspect is a white man, approximately 5-foot-8 with a thin build.

He left the bank with an undetermined amount of cash and fled in the direction of Texas Avenue.

Anyone with information is asked to call the Allegheny County police tipline at 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477). Callers can remain anonymous.
