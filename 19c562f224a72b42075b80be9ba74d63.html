Woman fatally hit by vehicle in Crafton shopping center

An elderly woman was struck by a vehicle and killed Thursday at a shopping center in Crafton.

First responders arrived at the Giant Eagle in the Crafton Ingram shopping center after 11 a.m. and found the victim dead at the scene, Allegheny County police said.

Homicide detectives and collision re-constructionists are investigating.

Anyone with information can call the County Police Tip Line at 1-833-ALL-TIPS. Callers can remain anonymous.
