McKnight Road reopened after fatal crash in which a car hit a stopped Port Authority bus

A motorist was killed on McKnight Road in McCandless after a car hit a stopped Port Authority bus at 3:40 p.m. Wednesday.

Port Authority spokesman Adam Brandolph said an O12 McKnight Flyer was stopped at Arcadia Drive when the front of the car hit the rear of the bus. The driver of the car died.

The driver was identified Wednesday night by the medical examiner as Anthony John Diulus, 50 of Pittsburgh.

About 12 passengers were on the bus when the accident occurred. One passenger was treated by medics at the scene.

The bus driver will go through routine drug and alcohol testing, Mr. Brandolph said.

Traffic on McKnight Road was closed in both directions immediately after the crash. McCandless police confirmed the road has since reopened.
