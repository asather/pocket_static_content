Man stabbed during fight in Carrick

One person was stabbed overnight during a fight in Pittsburgh’s Carrick neighborhood.

City police said officers responded shortly before 1 a.m. Sunday to the 1600 block of Nobles Lane after receiving reports of a stabbing.

When they arrived on the scene, they found two men had been fighting. One suffered a cut and another man had dislocated his shoulder. The man who was stabbed was not seriously injured, according to police.

Police consulted with the Allegheny County district attorney’s office and determined that no charges would be filed.
