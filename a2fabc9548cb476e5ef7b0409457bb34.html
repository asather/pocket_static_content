Ringgold school district in Washington County closed after undisclosed threat

The Ringgold School District in Washington County is closed Friday due to an undisclosed threat at the Ringgold Middle School, officials said in a statement. The missed day will be considered a Flexible Instruction Day, also due in part to Friday morning’s snowy weather.

In a statement posted on Twitter Thursday evening, the Monongahela school district said they received warning about a threat to Ringgold Middle School from a youth violence prevention program run by the state Attorney General’s office.

Authorities are investigating the Safe2Say tip in coordination with school district police. The notification raised “safety concerns” for the district’s students, officials said.

“We want to encourage students to feel comfortable reporting any information they may have that compromises their safety and the safety of others,” the district said in a statement. “Please contact your child’s school administrator with any questions.”

Students are asked to record their attendance Friday via the district website or the Ringgold phone app.
