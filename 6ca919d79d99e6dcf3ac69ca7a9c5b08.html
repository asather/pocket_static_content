Pittsburgh Public Schools adds distribution locations for students' meals

Pittsburgh Public Schools on Thursday announced several new student-meal distribution locations in partnership with the city and Housing Authority of Pittsburgh.

The locations are:

● Allegheny Dwellings Community Room, 1710 Belleau Drive

● East Hills Community Center, 2291 Wilner Drive

● Glen Hazel Community Center, 895 Johnston Ave.

● Homewood North Community Center,

● Northview Heights Recreation Center, 525 Mount Pleasant Road

● St. Paul AME Church (Beltzhoover), 400 Orchard Place

Meals will be distributed to students at these locations from 11 a.m. to 1 p.m.

Other previously announced sites for meal pickup include:

● Paulson Recreation Center, 1201 Paulson Ave.

● Warrington Recreation Center, 329 E. Warrington Ave.

● Salvation Army Homewood, 8020 Frankstown Ave.

● Salvation Army Westside, 1821 Broadhead Fording Road

● Tree of Life Church, 1036 Brookline Blvd.

● Emanuel United Methodist Church (for Kids Meal Network), 825 Lorenz Ave.

As of this week, many schools also remain open for meal distribution.
