'Odd Squad' picks up another Daytime Emmy award

“Odd Squad,” the charmingly nerdy live-action kids show from Pittsburgh’s Fred Rogers Productions, picked up a Daytime Emmy Sunday.

The PBS show won in the category of outstanding sound edition for a live-action program. One of this season’s episodes was shot around the Pittsburgh area last summer.

The 47th Daytime Emmys were presented in three different virtual shows from Los Angeles. Sunday’s was for children’s, lifestyle and animation categories.
