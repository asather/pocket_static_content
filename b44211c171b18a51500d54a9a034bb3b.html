Police searching for suspect after fatal shooting in Westmoreland County

A person was shot and killed in Westmoreland County Thursday, and police are searching for a suspect.

The man, who is unidentified, was fatally shot in the city of Arnold, Post-Gazette news partner KDKA-TV reported.

Police responded to the incident in the 200 block of 17th Street about 4:30 p.m., according to dispatchers.

Court records show that police have issued an arrest warrant for Mario Matthew Gatti, 30, of Arnold, on charges of criminal homicide, burglary and a firearms violation, among others.

He is believed to be in the area of Arnold, according to the KDKA report.
