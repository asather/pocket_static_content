Car strikes tree in Fayette County, killing two

Two people died in a single-vehicle crash in Fayette County early Saturday morning when their car lost control and struck a tree.

The accident occurred around 2 a.m. in Redstone.

The Fayette County coroner identified the driver as Miranda Huseman, 27, and the front-seat passenger as Daniel A. Guthrie, 32. Both were from the Fayette County area, Coroner Phillip E. Reilly said.

Ms. Huseman was driving a Honda Fit along Filbert-Orient Road when she lost control due to a “significant speed factor,” according to Dr. Reilly.

The car scraped against a wall that runs alongside the road and then hit a tree, causing “massive destruction” and ejecting Ms. Huseman.

Mr. Guthrie was partially entrapped.

Neither victim wore a seatbelt, Dr. Reilly said.

Mr. Guthrie was pronounced dead at the scene at 3:15 a.m.; Ms. Huseman was pronounced there 15 minutes later.

Dr. Reilly did not know the relationship between the victims.

Redstone police were investigating. No one from the department could be reached for comment.
