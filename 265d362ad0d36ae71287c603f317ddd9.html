Police: 55-year-old Indiana County woman missing

State police are searching for a 55-year-old Indiana County woman who was reported missing by a family member early Friday.

Lori Ann Lane, of West Lebanon, is about 5 feet tall with blue eyes and brown hair, according to a report from state police spokesman Clifford A. Greenfield. Ms. Lane was reportedly wearing a white fleece jacket and black leggings. Family members told police she was last seen Thursday between 8 p.m. and 9 p.m. at her home along Vellesig Road in Young Township.

Police said Ms. Lane is believed to be driving a silver 2003 Honda Pilot with license plate KVY7890. She may have been traveling to Saltsburg or Apollo, authorities said.

Anyone with information is asked to call 911.
