One wounded following two shootings in Swissvale

Allegheny County police are investigating a possible connection between two shootings in Swissvale, one on Wednesday and another late Tuesday, that left one person wounded.

 

The first shooting was reported around 11 p.m. Tuesday at the intersection of Washington Avenue and Center Street. Police said officers arriving at the scene recovered ballistic evidence but did not find any victims.

Then around 12:15 p.m. Wednesday, emergency officials were notified that a male gunshot victim had entered a local hospital emergency room. The man, who was in stable condition, told police that he had been shot in the 2600 block of South Braddock Avenue.

Police ask that anyone with information about the shootings call the county police Tipline at 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477); callers can remain anonymous.
