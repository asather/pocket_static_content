Police looking for two men charged in Oakland assault

Police on Wednesday charged two Pittsburgh men with aggravated assault after a man was found unconscious in Oakland on Saturday.

William Murphy, 27, and Kevin Allison, 30, were charged with assaulting a man on the 300 block of Atwood Street around 3 a.m. on March 14.

When police arrived on-scene at the assault, they found a man unconscious on the sidewalk. Witnesses said he had been jumped by a group of men, according to police.

The man was transported to the hospital in serious condition.

Police ask anyone with information on their whereabouts to call 412-422-6520.
