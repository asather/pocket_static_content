Greater Pittsburgh Community Food Bank, Veterans Leadership Program to hold food distributions Monday

Two area nonprofits will hold food distributions Monday.

The Greater Pittsburgh Community Food Bank will serve up to 1,000 vehicles during its distribution from noon to 3 p.m. at its Duquesne location. The food bank is asking people not to arrive before 11 a.m. at the intersection of Rt. 837 (South Duquesne Boulevard) and South Linden Street. 

Each vehicle will receive two boxes of food. People are asked to stay in their vehicles and to unlock their trunk or backseat door so volunteers can place the food into their car.

Veterans Leadership Program will distribute free food for veterans and military families in need Monday at its parking lot, 2934 Smallman St., in the Strip District.

Drive-thru and walk-up pickup begins at noon.

Vehicles should enter Mulberry Way on 26th Street. Prepared food kits of fresh produce and shelf-stable goods will be placed into your vehicle. For more information: 412-481-8200.
