Steelers offensive lineman Matt Feiler beats the clock, signs RFA tender

Steelers offensive lineman Matt Feiler and cornerback Mike Hilton are going to sign their restricted free agent tenders Thursday, a move most RFAs will be making before the end of the week.

Under the new collective bargaining agreement, teams have the right to reduce the offer beginning next week. Feiler and Hilton will make $3.25 million on the second-round tender the Steelers offered.

Feiler is expected to start at right tackle or left guard for the Steelers this season. He spent most of the past two years as the starting right tackle.

Hilton, who is entering his fourth season, is the starting slot corner and has four career interceptions.
