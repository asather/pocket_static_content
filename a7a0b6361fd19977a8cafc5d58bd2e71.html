Vice President Pence visiting Wilkinsburg, Butler County today

This story was updated at 11:45 a.m. on June 12, 2020.

Vice President Mike Pence is visiting Wilkinsburg and Butler County today to speak with religious leaders and community members.

Mr. Pence was first visiting the Covenant Church of Pittsburgh in Wilkinsburg for a “listening session” with faith and community leaders.

Then he was scheduled to visit Oberg Industries in Buffalo Township, where he was to give a talk following a round-table discussion on the economy and the nation’s recovery from the coronavirus pandemic, according to America First Policies.

The group is hosting the policy event as part of a nationwide tour in support of the Trump re-election campaign.

The vice president landed this morning at the 171st Air Refueling Wing in Pittsburgh.
