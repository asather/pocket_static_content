The illusion is real: Pittsburgh Cultural Trust will stream live Liberty Magic shows beginning Friday

Perhaps there’s no magic in the air this spring, but it will be online.

The Pittsburgh Cultural Trust announced it will host Friday evening live-streaming events featuring Liberty Magic talent through its YouTube channel as well as its Facebook page. The shows are free, beginning at 7:30 p.m. April 24.

Eric Jones, who in early 2019 kicked off the Liberty Magic venue series with his “Impossible” night of sleight-of-hand, will perform. Anna DeGuzman, who was here a few months back, will bring her “cardistry” to the table. 

Both are internationally known talents with significant followings on social media. Those who tune in live can ask them questions via chat.

And proving that magic can be bottled, these Liberty Magic shows will be archived to watch online later.
