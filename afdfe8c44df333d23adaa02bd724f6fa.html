Pittsburgh police officer shot in leg on North Side; 1 suspect dead

A city police officer was shot in the leg Thursday evening on the North Side and a suspect in the incident has died, authorities say.

Police spokesperson Chris Togneri confirmed the officer is in stable condition.

One of the suspects was shot. Public safety director Wendell Hissrich confirmed the suspect died, and there are multiple people in custody.

“I think this is an example of how dangerous the officers’ jobs are day to day,” Mr. Hissrich said.

A large police presence has shut down East Ohio Street between Madison Avenue and Chestnut Street.

County police are investigating the incident since it is an officer-involved shooting, Mr. Hissrich said.

This story is developing and will be updated.
