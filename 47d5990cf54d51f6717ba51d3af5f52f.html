21-year-old woman hospitalized after falling off cliff at McConnells Mill State Park

A woman was hospitalized Sunday afternoon after falling off of a cliff at a state park.

Firefighters said the 21-year-old was a Slippery Rock University student, according to Post-Gazette news partner KDKA-TV. She was walking in McConnells Mill State Park with a group of students when she fell down an 80-foot cliff near Breakneck Ridge.

Lawrence County dispatchers said the call came in at 3:23 p.m.

KDKA-TV reports the woman was flown to UPMC Presbyterian in Pittsburgh. Details on her condition were not available.
