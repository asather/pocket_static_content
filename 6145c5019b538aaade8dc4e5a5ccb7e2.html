Police looking for help finding McKeesport teen

McKeesport police are seeking help in finding a 15-year-old girl who was last seen Monday, the department said in a Facebook post Thursday evening.

Police said Daisha Reyes-Burgos, a black female nearly 5 feet tall, left her McKeesport home in the 700 block of 6th Street around 6 p.m. Monday. She has not been seen since, police said.

Officials said the girl takes medication but left without her prescription.

Authorities are asking anyone with information on her whereabouts to contact the McKeesport Police Department at 412-675-5015 or call 911.
