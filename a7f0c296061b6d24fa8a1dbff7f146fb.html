Inmate at Allegheny County Jail tests positive for COVID-19

An inmate at the Allegheny County Jail has tested positive for COVID-19, the county announced Wednesday.

The county said the prisoner and a cellmate have been quarantined “since the onset of symptoms.”

According to the county, “physical distancing continues to be stressed to employees and inmates across the facility. All pods are now on split recreation to reduce potential interactions.”

No further information has been released about the inmate.
