Driver found dead after crashing into pole on W. Carson Street

A male was found dead after his vehicle collided with a pole early Sunday on West Carson Street in the South Side.

Authorities did not say what caused the crash but said the male — who identity has not been released — was the only occupant of the vehicle.

After hitting the pole around 1:45 a.m., the vehicle caught fire. Firefighters extricated the driver from the vehicle, but he was pronounced dead at the scene.

The crash happened in the 1600 block of West Carson, which sits between Station Square and the West End Bridge.
