Man involved in head-on collision in Baldwin dies

One of the five people involved in head-on collision between two trucks Saturday in Baldwin Borough has died, according to the Allegheny County Medical Examiner’s office.

Reyon Harris, 21, of Charleroi died at 2:30 p.m. Saturday at UPMC Mercy hospital, authorities said.

Four other people were hurt when the two trucks collided around 5:30 a.m. on Clairton Boulevard/Route 51 near the intersection with Streets Run Road, according to Pittsburgh Post-Gazette’s news partner KDKA-TV.

It’s unclear which vehicle Mr. Harris was traveling in. Police said four people were in a southbound truck that collided with another truck headed north in which the driver was the only rider. One of the vehicles caught fire, but no one was injured by the flames, police said.

The crash is under investigation.
