Allderdice takes down Altoona on last-minute shot in regional semifinal

Trailing by one in the final seconds, Allderdice turned to its most consistent perimeter shooter to try to extend its season.

Blake Haber delivered.

Haber connected on a 3-pointer just before the buzzer to give Allderdice a 51-49 win against Altoona in a Class 6A Districts 6-8-10 regional semifinal at Brashear.

Tony Henderson scored a team-high 19 points for Allderdice (19-6), which made nine 3-pointers and advanced to play McDowell (20-3) in the regional final.

Girls

Allderdice 58, Erie 36: Nautica Burwell and Sidney Lightning each scored 14 points to propel City League champion Allderdice (23-2) past Erie (14-9) in a Class 6A Districts 6-8-10 regional semifinal at Hagerty Events Center in Erie.

Villa Maria 58, Westinghouse 32: Dejanae Glover scored 14 points, but Westinghouse (15-7) saw its season end against Villa Maria (19-4) in the Class 4A Districts 8-10 regional championship at Fairview.
