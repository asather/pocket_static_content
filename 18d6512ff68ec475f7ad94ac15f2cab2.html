Man wanted for Westmoreland County homicide arrested in Florida

A man who was wanted for homicide in Westmoreland County was arrested on Sunday in Florida.

Mario Matthew Gatti, 30, of Arnold was accused of killing 33-year-old Michael Coover Jr., of Brackenridge, who was shot multiple times on the 200 block of 17th Street in Arnold.

The Jacksonville Beach Police Department took Gatti into custody when they spotted him on the beach.

Gatti is charged with homicide, burglary and illegal possession of a firearm, plus multiple counts of recklessly endangering another person and terroristic threats. Pennsylvania court records indicate he has not yet been arraigned as of Sunday night.
