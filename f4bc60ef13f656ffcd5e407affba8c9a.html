Police: Missing man, 27, last seen in Hazelwood found safe

A 27-year-old man who on Thursday was reported missing and last seen in Hazelwood has returned home safely, according to Pittsburgh police.

Police on Friday morning said Mr. McPherson was found overnight. 

Nicholas “Niko” McPherson was reportedly missing since around noon Wednesday.
