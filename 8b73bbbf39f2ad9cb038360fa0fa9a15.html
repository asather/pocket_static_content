Southwest passenger dies after medical event diverts NYC-Chicago flight to Pittsburgh

A Southwest flight from New York City to Chicago was diverted Wednesday to Pittsburgh International Airport after a passenger on board became unresponsive and later died.

The passenger was identified by authorities as Thomas Guziak, 48, of Tinley Park, Ill., a Chicago suburb. 

The Allegheny County Medical Examiner’s Office said the incident occurred around 12:45 p.m. on board Flight 859 from New York’s LaGuardia Airport to Chicago Midway International Airport.

The flight landed around 1 p.m. Allegheny County police officers responded to a call for someone in cardiac arrest on board an incoming plane. The county Airport Authority’s fire and rescue team gave first aid before Mr. Guziak was taken to the hospital by ambulance, authorities said.

Mr. Guziak was pronounced dead at Heritage Valley Sewickley hospital at 1:49 p.m.
