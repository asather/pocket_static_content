Three vehicles moving at 'high rate of speed' involved in Duquesne crash

Three vehicles, including a motorcycle, were involved in a crash early Sunday in Duquesne, but there were no major injuries, according to Allegheny County police.

Police said the three vehicles were traveling together “at a high rate of speed” around 3:45 a.m. in the 200 block of Route 837. The lead vehicle, a sedan, lost control. The motorcycle and another sedan both struck the lead vehicle. The second sedan also struck a pole, police said.

All three drivers sustained “minor non life-threatening injuries” and are in good condition, police said.
