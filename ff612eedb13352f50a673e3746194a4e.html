Sibling rivalry leads to trophy catch

Ben Varhola, 6, of North Huntingdon struck out at Twin Lakes while his brother Nick Varhola, 8, landed four trout April 10. Accompanied by his uncle, Justin Petrozza of West Mifflin, Ben returned to the Greensburg waters the next day and wrestled in a 20-inch rainbow trout, taken on live minnows.
