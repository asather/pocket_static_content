PPG to acquire Italian paints business

PPG said Wednesday it plans to buy Italian paints producer Industria Chimica Reggiana.

Financial terms were not disclosed. 

ICR makes automotive refinish coatings and coatings for light industrial applications.

It employs about 180.

Downtown-based PPG, which had net sales of $15.4 billion in 2018, said the deal is expected to close in the first quarter.
