TRAFFIC: Crash closes Route 28 southbound at Millvale exit

Route 28 southbound is closed due a truck crash at the Millvale exit early Thursday, according to PennDOT.

There were no immediate reports of serious injuries.

The crash occurred before 3:30 a.m.

Motorists heading inbound can take the Millvale exit or the 62nd Street Bridge to Butler Street, then to the 40th Street Bridge to Route 28, .
