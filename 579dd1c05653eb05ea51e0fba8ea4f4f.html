Water main break restricts Route 65 traffic in Avalon

A water main break has shut down northbound traffic on Route 65 in Avalon.

Northbound traffic on Ohio River Boulevard between South Ohio Street and Fisk Street crosses into a single lane on the southbound side, according to a release from PennDOT.

West View Water Authority crews were on site to fix the break Sunday afternoon.

PennDOT urges caution when driving in the area.
