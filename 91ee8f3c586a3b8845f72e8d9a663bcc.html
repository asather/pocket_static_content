Police issue warrant for suspect in McKeesport shooting

Allegheny County police are looking for a man they say is responsible for a shooting in McKeesport that left another man in critical condition.

An arrest warrant was issued for Kiesean Brown, 19, who is charged with aggravated assault, criminal attempt homicide, and other offenses, county police said Thursday.

McKeesport police responded to a shooting around 2:30 p.m. Sunday in the 2900 block of Jenny Lind Street where they found a 19-year-old man who had been shot. The victim was taken to the hospital where he was listed in critical condition.

Investigators believe Mr. Brown was the shooter, according to a news release. Police said Mr. Brown is black and about 6-feet tall with a medium build. He has ties to the McKeesport area, police said.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Allegheny County Police at 1-666-255-8477. Callers can remain anonymous.
