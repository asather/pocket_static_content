Steelers discontinue business travel, take other precautions amid COVID-19 crisis

The Steelers have discontinued business travel and are having their staff, except for “essential crews,” work from home as the government continues to encourage business to shut down.

All public events at the UPMC Rooney Sports Complex and Heinz Field have been canceled. Steelers pro shops will close for the next two weeks.

“We will continue to be in contact with our partners at UMPC as well as local health officials to gather information,” team spokesman Burt Lauten said in a statement. “Our primary focus is to ensure the safety and health of our employees and families, and the communities around the Pittsburgh area.”
