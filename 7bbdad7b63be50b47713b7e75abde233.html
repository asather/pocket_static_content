Health department confirms Pennsylvania's first COVID-19 death

The Pennsylvania Department of Health on Wednesday confirmed the state’s first COVID-19 related death.

A Northampton County adult died while being treated at a hospital, the health department said. No personal information about the patient’s age or gender was given.

As of noon Wednesday, Northampton County had only one reported case of COVID-19. Statewide, a total of 133 people have tested positive for the virus.
