Authorities ID man killed in McKeesport shooting

A 31-year-old man died early Thursday and a woman was injured after a shooting in McKeesport on Wednesday night.

The Allegheny County Medical Examinerâ€™s Office identified the man as Niko Dawson of McKeesport. He died at UPMC McKeesport hospital just before 12:30 a.m.

Police said they responded to the 1200 block of Soles Street for reports of a shooting around 11:22 p.m. Wednesday.

A 24-year-old woman was grazed by a bullet and wounded on her leg. 

She was treated by medics at the scene, police said. Her identity has not been released.

Officers learned Mr. Dawson had been taken to the hospital prior to their arrival by private means with gunshot wounds to his torso, Allegheny County police said.

No arrests have been made..
