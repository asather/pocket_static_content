Rt. 910 in Hamar closed after vehicle struck gas line

Peoples Gas is responding to the scene of a gas leak along Route 910.

Post-Gazette news partner KDKA said a vehicle struck a gas line near Armina Stone shortly after 2 a.m. Wednesday. 

Route 910 has been closed at the intersection of Locust Hill Road and Rich Hill Road.

PennDOT workers were doing work in the area and reported the accident.

Peoples Gas say that it will take 4 to 5 hours to repair the gas line, KDKA reported.
