Police searching for missing teen

Police on Saturday are searching for a missing 15-year-old last seen walking towards Mount Oliver.

Authorities described Kennia Johnson as 5 feet, 1 inch tall, weighing 119 pounds with brown eyes and long, braided hair.

She was last seen around 1:30 p.m. walking on Brownsville Road towards Mount Oliver. She was wearing a purple T-shirt with black leggings and blue glasses.

Police asked anyone with information on her to call 911.
