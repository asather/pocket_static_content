Outbound Fort Pitt Tunnel reopens after car crash

A multiple-vehicle car crash in the Fort Pitt Tunnel has stopped traffic headed outbound on the bridge.

The Pennsylvania Department of Transportation reports all lanes are closed following a multiple-vehicle crash around 5:23 p.m. on I-376 between the exit for the Fort Duquesne Bridge and North Shore exit and Exit 69A for U.S. 19 South and Banksville Road.

Police, fire and emergency medical services were on the scene, but there were no medical transports, according to information from the Allegheny County 911 Center. Status on any other injuries was unknown.

The crash was cleared around 6 p.m., according to state police.
