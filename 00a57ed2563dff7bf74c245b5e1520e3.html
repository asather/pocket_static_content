Peters Lake Park re-opens after discovery of unattended kayak

Emergency responders re-opened Peters Lake Park in Peters after closing it around 7 a.m. Saturday to investigate an unattended kayak found in the middle of the lake. Crews pulled a blue kayak from the water and did a detailed search of the shore, parking lot and water by foot, boat and drone and found nothing of note, according to the Peters Township Fire Department.

Anyone with information on the kayak is asked to call Washington County 911.
