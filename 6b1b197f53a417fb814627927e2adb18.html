COVID-19 exposure closes Greensburg YMCA

The YMCA in Greensburg has been temporarily closed because of COVID-19 exposure.

YMCA leadership was notified by state health officials late Friday afternoon that someone associated with the summer camp was confirmed positive with the virus, according to interim CEO Suzanne Printz.

The individual was last on site and in contact with campers on Wednesday, she said.

“Upon receiving notification, all campers were moved outside while YMCA staff began executing cleaning and sanitization protocols indoors,” Ms. Printz said in a statement. “Under the direction of local health officials and following CDC guidelines, the process will continue until we are confident our facilities are safe for all to return,” she said.

According to the YMCA’s website, the facility will remain closed at least through Monday. An update on the expected opening will be posted by noon Monday, the site said.
