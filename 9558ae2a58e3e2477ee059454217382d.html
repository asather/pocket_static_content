A 23-year-old man was shot while driving a car in McKeesport around 11 p.m. Friday and is hospitalized with non-life-threatening injuries.

Allegheny County homicide detectives are investigating the incident in which they believe the victim, whose name was not released, had just gotten into the car and was driving when unknown individuals began shooting at him. He crashed the car after being wounded.

Anyone with information concerning this incident is asked to call the County Police Tip Line 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477). Callers can remain anonymous.
