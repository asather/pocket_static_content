Police investigating shots fired in Homestead

Police on Thursday are investigating a shooting in Hazelwood that left houses and vehicles hit by gunfire.

Authorities responded to a ShotSpotter notification of multiple shots fired on the 5000 block of 2nd Avenue around 8:50 p.m.

Officials said responding officers did not find any suspects or victims.
