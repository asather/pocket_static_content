Man shot in stomach in McKees Rocks during argument; police recover two guns

A man was shot in the stomach and hip Saturday night in McKees Rocks.

The victim was taken to the hospital.

McKees Rocks police officers responded around 9:30 p.m. to a reported shooting on Rosamond Street and found the wounded man.

Investigators learned that the victim got into an argument with another man and shots were fired. The shooter fled, police said.

Police found two guns at the scene.

Allegheny County police detectives are investigating.

Anyone with information can call 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477).
