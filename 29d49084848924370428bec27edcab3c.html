Mock draft roundup: Experts split on who Steelers will target in Round 2

The Steelers don’t have a pick in the first round of the NFL draft this year, but there are plenty of mock draft predictions about whom they’ll take in the second round. Here’s a roundup that will be updated after the first round is completed Thursday night. As you can see, there’s little consensus.

Ray Fittipaldo, Post-Gazette: Florida State running back Cam Akers

Brian Batko, Post-Gazette: Mississippi State linebacker Willie Gay

Josh Edwards, CBS: Texas A&M defensive lineman Justin Madubuike

Mel Kiper, ESPN: Oklahoma quarterback Jalen Hurts

Ryan Wilson, CBS: Wisconsin running back Jonathan Taylor

Chris Trapasso, CBS: Colorado receiver Laviska Shenault Jr.

Luke Easterling, Draft Wire: Ohio State running back J.K. Dobbins

Matt Miller, Bleacher Report: Jalen Hurts

Chad Reuter, NFL.com: Cam Akers

Vinnie Iyer, Sporting News: Jonathan Taylor
