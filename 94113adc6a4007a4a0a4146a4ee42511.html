Police investigating after woman’s partially dismembered body found in McKees Rocks

The body of a woman found dead in a McKees Rocks apartment complex Monday morning was first discovered within an abandoned refrigerator, according to residents of the building.

The Allegheny County Medical Examiner’s Office identified the woman as Kristy Jefferson, 38, of McKees Rocks. The cause and manner of Ms. Jefferson’s death are still under investigation.

Allegheny County Police said that Daryl Jones, 40, has been arrested and charged with abuse of a corpse.

Ms. Jefferson was reported missing on April 29, county police said. 

The residents of the building, who did not wish to be named, said they found the woman’s partially dismembered body in the fridge on the second-floor hallway, after they noticed a strong smell emanating from it.

County homicide detectives are investigating and are asking anyone with information to call 1-833-ALL-TIPS.
