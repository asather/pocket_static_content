Penn State adds 4-star athlete Ken Talley to 2022 class

Penn State hasn't allowed the lack of a 2020 season to stop the momentum in 2022 recruiting.

Ken Talley, a four-star athlete who played linebacker/defensive end at Northeast High School in Philadelphia, became the sixth player of the Nittany Lionsâ€™ 2022 class when he committed Tuesday.

Talley is the third in-state recruit in the class, joining four-star offensive tackle Drew Shelton from Downingtown and three-star quarterback Beau Pribula of York. Talley is the first defensive player to join the class. Four-star tight ends Holden Staes and Jerry Cross from Atlanta and Milwaukee, respectively, and four-star receiver Kaden Sanders from Ohio round out the group.

Talley chose Penn State over Arizona State and Tennessee.
