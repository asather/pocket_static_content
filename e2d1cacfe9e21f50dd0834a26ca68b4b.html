Avett Brothers coming to Petersen Events Center in June

You never know where The Avett Brothers are going to turn up next.

The North Carolina band’s spring tour will hit Pittsburgh on June 7 at the Petersen Events Center in Oakland, where they played in 2014.

The presale starts at noon on Tuesday and the public on-sale is at 10 a.m. Thursday, Feb. 7 at ticketmaster.com.

The Avett Brothers, who made their Pittsburgh debut at Club Cafe in 2006, came as part of the Outlaw Music Festival with Willie Nelson at S&T Bank Music Park last year and were on the Farm Aid bill in 2017. They’ve also been regulars at Stage AE.
