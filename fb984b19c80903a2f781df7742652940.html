Streets Run Road reopens after crash downed utility poles

Streets Run Road is reopened following a Saturday evening crash in Baldwin that downed utility poles, police said.

Around 6 p.m, Baldwin Borough police released a statement saying a traffic collision Saturday shut down a section of Streets Run Road.

The street was closed from Brentwood Road to Prospect Road for nearly three hours. A collision caused multiple utility poles to fall down.

Police did not say if there were any injuries or how many vehicles were involved.
