State police searching for 2 missing Greene County teens, believed to be still in area

State police are searching for two missing teenage girls from Greene County.

Stephanie Ward, 15, of Wind Ridge was last seen at her residence on Hewitt Run Road on Tuesday at 5 p.m., according to state police in Waynesburg. 

Stephanie was wearing a dark colored hooded sweatshirt with a red tank top underneath, black sweatpants and a gold necklace with a heart pendant.

She has freckles and red hair and stands about 5 foot 3 inches tall.

Destiny, 16, of Waynesburg, was reported missing on Monday. Police said she was last seen wearing a black and grey sweatshirt and black and gray sweatpants. 

Police told the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette Friday morning that they had information that leads them to believe both teens are still in Greene County after surveilling social media activity. 

Trooper Lucas Borkowski said the two cases are unrelated.
