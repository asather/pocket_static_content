Driver killed, another critically injured in head-on crash in Hampton

A 23-year-old woman was killed late Friday and a second 23-year-old woman was critically injured in a head-on collision in Hampton.

The fatal victim of the 11:51 p.m. crash was identified as Kaitlyn Eckelberry by the Allegheny County Medical Examiner’s office. She was pronounced dead at 12:03 a.m. Saturday at the scene, the 3800 block of Route 8. Her hometown was listed as Canton but a state was not included in a news release.

The identify of the injured woman was not released.

Allegheny County Homicide detectives and the department’s Collision Reconstruction Unit reported it appeared the injured woman, driving a Jeep Cherokee, crossed the center line and struck a Hyundai Elantra driven by Ms. Eckelberry, killing her.

No further details were immediately available. Anyone with information concerning this accident is asked to call the county police tip line at 1-833-ALL-TIPS. Callers can remain anonymous.
