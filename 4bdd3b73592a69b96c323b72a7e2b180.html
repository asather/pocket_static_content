Wilkinsburg man arrested for armed robbery, fleeing police

Police arrested a man for armed robbery Tuesday in Wilkinsburg, according to Post-Gazette news partner, KDKA.

Wilkinsburg police said they found Shawn Michael Perry of Wilkinsburg, 23, robbing a juvenile in the street after responding to a call concerning a man with a gun on the 800 block of Walnut Street.

Police also said the two appeared to be fighting and money was falling around them. Mr. Perry fled into a Walnut Street residence, where he was apprehended by police.

The gun recovered from the incident was a .38-caliber revolver, which was reported stolen from Pittsburgh, according to police.

Mr. Perry is charged with robbery, assault by menace, false identification to police, resisting arrest, receiving stolen property and carrying a firearm without a license.
