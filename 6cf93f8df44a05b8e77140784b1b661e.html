West Mifflin man charged in fatal shooting

Allegheny County police on Wednesday requested a warrant for a West Mifflin man charged with killing another man late Monday night.

Authorities said Daymond “Day Day” Wiggins, 37, shot and killed 45-year-old Thomas Johnson outside the Mon View Heights apartment complex.

According to a police affidavit, Mr. Johnson, also known as “T Raw,” was hanging out in an area called “the tunnel,” located between two buildings in the apartment complex.

Wiggins approached Mr. Johnson and fired multiple shots, the affidavit reads. West Mifflin police arrived and found Mr. Johnson shot in the head; he was pronounced dead at the scene.

The affidavit does not describe a motive for the shooting.

Wiggins is facing homicide and firearm charges.
