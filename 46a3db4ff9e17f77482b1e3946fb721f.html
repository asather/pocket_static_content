Funeral services set Friday and Saturday for Jefferson Hills officer killed in crash

Funeral services are scheduled Friday and Saturday for a Jefferson Hills police officer who died following a head-on crash on June 3.

Officer Dale T. Provins, Jr., 50, of Belle Vernon, died on Saturday, 10 days after being seriously injured when an oncoming vehicle crossed the center line and struck the officer’s police cruiser in the 200 block of Old Clairton Road.

A public visitation is planned for Friday from noon to 7 p.m. at the Jefferson Hills Municipal Complex, rear entrance, 925 Old Clairton Road, police Chief Gene Roach said.

Borough officials said social distancing and masks are encouraged for all visitors.

The funeral will take place on Saturday, beginning with a small procession to accompany Officer Provins from the funeral home to Round Hill Cemetery in Elizabeth Township.

A graveside service that will also be open to the public will begin at 11 a.m.

Officer Provins, a U.S. Marine Corps veteran, worked 15 years with the Jefferson Hills Police Department and was a second-generation police officer.

He is survived by his daughter, fiancée, parents, and siblings.
