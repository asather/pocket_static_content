Man injured in Swissvale shooting

A man was critically injured Thursday in a shooting in Swissvale.

Allegheny County police said the shooting occurred around 10:40 a.m. in the intersection of Woodstock Avenue and Ardmore Street, where a 21-year-old was found shot in the abdomen.

Police said he was taken to a hospital in critical condition.

No other information was immediately available.

Anyone with information is asked to call the County Police tip line 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477). Callers can remain anonymous.
