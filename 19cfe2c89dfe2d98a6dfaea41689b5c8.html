Crash knocks down power line tower in Harmar

A truck crashed Saturday afternoon into a tower carrying power lines in Harmar, knocking it over and creating a power outage in the area.

The accident occurred in the 900 block of Russellton Road about 12:25 p.m., according to Allegheny County 911 dispatch. One passenger involved in the single-vehicle crash was transported to a local hospital.

No additional information was immediately available.
