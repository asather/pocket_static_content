No injuries in Mount Washington structure fire

Residents were able to escape a structure fire late Friday in Mount Washington, according to a city report.

City firefighters were summoned about 10:30 p.m. to the 100 block of Ruth Street, where they found flames in the lower level of the building.

Three resident were able to escape the building without injury. Soon after the fire was raised to three alarms, with firefighters able to prevent flames from spreading to neighboring homes.

Salt was needed when ice formed on Ruth Street. The Fire Investigations Unit is investigating the cause, the report said.
