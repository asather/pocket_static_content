One dead, one injured in Homewood shooting

One person is dead and one is injured after a shooting late Wednesday in Homewood.

Pittsburgh Bureau of Police officers responded to a ShotSpotter alert indicating gunfire near the intersection of Frankstown Avenue and Collier Street around 11:30 p.m. Wednesday.

Zone 5 officers arrived and found two shooting victims.

Pittsburgh EMS transported a female victim to a local hospital in critical condition with multiple gunshot wounds. She was pronounced dead at the hospital around 1 a.m. Thursday.

Medics also transported a male with a gunshot wound to his lower extremity. He was listed in stable condition.

The Mobile Crime Unit and Violent Crime Unit responded to the scene to investigate.

Police were searching for a white Kia Sorento that was seen on surveillance camera footage near the shooting location.  

Anyone with information on this homicide is asked to call VCU detectives at 412-323-7161.

This story will be updated.
