Charges pending for teens who gave 2-year-old a vaping device

Charges are pending against individuals who gave a 2-year-old child a vaping device and posted it to Snapchat, according to State Police.

“The involved persons and child have been identified and charges are pending,” Pennsylvania State Police Troopers Steve Limani and Cliff Greenfield said on Twitter. That tweet also says that Troop A’s Indiana Criminal Investigation Unit “is aware of the video and is investigating ....”

Post-Gazette partner KDKA-TV reported that the Snapchat video shows two teenagers giving a 2-year-old child a vaping device. State Police said they’ve yet to issue a news release but more information is expected to be available Monday.
