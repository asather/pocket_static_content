Fishing Report: Fall trout stocking set for Oct.19-24

Lake Erie and tributaries (Erie County) On Twentymile Creek, steelhead were reported to be locked above damming at the mouth in low, clear water. Walleye were taking spoons and crawler harnesses at 100-115 feet under rough waters.

Lake LeBouef (Erie County) Muskellunge were caught on spoons and bucktails.

Woodcock Creek Lake (Crawford County) Muskellunge, walleye and catfish were taken. Muskies, walleye, smallmouth bass and bowfin were caught in the tailrace.

Canadohta Lake (Crawford County) Good-sized muskies and walleye were caught.

Allegheny Reservoir (Warren County) Northern pike went for spinners and jerkbaits in the bays. Smallmouths were taken on crank baits and plastic worms in 4-30 feet of water and walleye on spoons and crawler harnesses in 20-60 feet. The pool level was down and boaters were cautioned to avoid unexpected hazards.

Buffalo Creek (Butler, Armstrong counties) The state was expected to stock trout Oct. 19-24.

Raccoon Lake (Beaver County) Adult trout were expected to be stocked Oct. 19-24 by the state Fish and Boat Commission.

Deer Lakes (Allegheny County) The upper, middle and lower lake were scheduled to get trout during the week of Oct. 19-24.

Allegheny River (Allegheny County) Using a worm as bait, Ezra Goldberg, 13, of Squirrel Hill pulled a 19-inch channel catfish from under the floating Pittsburgh River Rescue station below the Clemente Bridge during the final moments of Venture Outdoors’ TriAnglers fishing program on the North Shore on Wednesday. John Force of McKees Rocks released a 10½-inch smallmouth taken on a shiner. The fishing program continues 11:30 a.m.-1:30 p.m. Wednesdays through October. Loaner rods and bait are free. State license requirements apply.

Pine Creek (Allegheny County) The Delayed Harvest Artificial Lures Only section was expected to get a state stocking of trout Oct. 19-24

Deer Creek (Allegheny County) Late-season trout stocking was scheduled in the Delayed Harvest Artificial Lures Only section Oct. 19-24.

Meadow Run (Fayette County) The Delayed Harvest Artificial Lures Only section was expected to get trout Oct. 19-24.
