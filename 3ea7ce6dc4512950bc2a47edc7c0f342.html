Unity Brewing Co. opens Saturday near Latrobe

Unity Brewing Co. is opening Saturday near Latrobe in Westmoreland County.

The new brewpub, which has a five-barrel brewhouse and its own kitchen, is to be open from noon to 11 p.m.

Musician Alec Henderson will perform from 8 to 11 p.m. Saturday and Skero N Jingles from 2 to 5 p.m. Sunday.  

 

The brewery at 3862 U.S. 30 is in the same building as owner Kevin Ridilla’s Cliff’s Flooring Solutions. Learn more on its Facebook page. 

Already open in Unity is Four Seasons Brewing Co., 745 Lloyd Ave. Ext., Latrobe 15650 (fsbrewing.com).
