Justin Bieber concert at PPG Paints Arena rescheduled for June

Justin Bieber has rescheduled his PPG Paints Arena for July 1, 2021.

It was initially set to hit the arena on Aug. 4 but was postponed due to the pandemic.

The pop star’s World Tour 2021 will now begin June 2 in San Diego.

“I can’t wait to get out there and connect with my fans on this tour,” he said in a statement. “We’ve been through so much this year. More than ever, we’ve come to understand how much we need each other, and how meaningful these moments can really be.”

Tickets for new shows go on sale to the general public starting Thursday, Aug. 6.
