Three men found shot in Homewood North

Pittsburgh police discovered three men shot in Homewood North around 5:30 p.m. Tuesday, according to police spokesman Maurice Matthews.

Police responded to an alert for a shooting on the 1200 block of Mohler Street and discovered a man shot in the collarbone area. He was transported to the hospital in critical condition.

Shortly after, two more victims were discovered on the 7500 block of Upland Street. One man was shot in the chest and arm. Medics transported him to the hospital in critical condition.

The third man was shot in the leg and transported in stable condition.

One vehicle was towed from the Upland Street scene.

Police are investigating.
