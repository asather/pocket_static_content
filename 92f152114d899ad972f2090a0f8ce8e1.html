Jason Isbell and the 400 Unit coming to UPMC Events Center in June

Here’s the concert announcement a lot of Americana fans were waiting for:

Jason Isbell and The 400 Unit will return to play the UPMC Events Center in Moon on June 26 with Strand of Oaks.

Tickets go on sale at 10 a.m. Friday at ticketmaster.com. Prices are $88.50, $68.50, $58.50, $48.50.

The band will release its sixth album, “Reunions,” on May 15.
