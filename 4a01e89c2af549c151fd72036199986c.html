Human remains found along Bethel Park trail, police say

A set of human remains was discovered in Bethel Park on Monday, Allegheny County Police announced in a statement.

Police responded to the scene at approximately 11:24 a.m., after the remains were found by a person walking along a trail near trolley tracks.

The Allegheny County Police Homicide Unit was called to assist at the scene, but at this point foul play is not suspected, the statement said.

County Police note, however, that this information could be subject to change as the investigation progresses.

Anyone with information concerning this incident is asked to call the County Police Tip Line at 1-833-ALL-TIPS.
