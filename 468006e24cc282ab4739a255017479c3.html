Fallen tree closes Woodruff Street in Mount Washington

Woodruff Street is closed at Merrimac Street in Mount Washington on Friday morning due to a tree falling down.

According to Pittsburgh Post-Gazette news partner KDKA, the tree fell across the roadway and brought down some wires with it, as well.

The closure is between Route 51 and Merrimac Street. Crews have the area blocked off.
