Memorial Day closings 2020

All city of Pittsburgh, Allegheny County, commonwealth of Pennsylvania and federal offices and courts will be closed Monday for the Memorial Day holiday. Banks will be closed. State liquor stores normally open Mondays will be open from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. Post offices will be closed with no pickup or delivery of regular mail.

The Port Authority of Allegheny County will run holiday bus, incline and T service, and the customer service line (412-442-2000, or the TTY number, 412-231-7007) will operate from 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. The Downtown Service Center will be closed. Garbage pickup will be delayed one day.
