Minor injuries reported in 4-vehicle crash on Parkway East

Several people suffered minor injuries in a four-vehicle crash on the Parkway East/Interstate 376 Monday.

The crash was reported eastbound around noon near the Grant Street exit, according to Allegheny County emergency officials. Several individuals were taken to the hospital.

It was unclear what caused the crash, which was cleared within the hour.
