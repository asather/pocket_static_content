City aligns student meal distribution with PPS schedule

The city of Pittsburgh said Tuesday it would align the schedule of the student meal distribution locations it opened in conjunction with the sites being run by the Pittsburgh Public Schools.

Meals and optional activity packets will be handed out at the five city-run distribution sites from 11 a.m. to 1 p.m. on Monday, Tuesday and Friday. Children will receive enough meals on Tuesday to last them until Friday, according to the city.

That schedule will begin Tuesday, the city said.

The five distribution sites being run by the city are:

Arlington Recreation Center — 2201 Salisbury St.

Paulson Recreation Center — 1201 Paulson Ave.

Warrington Recreation Center — 329 E. Warrington Ave.

Salvation Army Homewood location — 8020 Frankstown Ave.

Salvation Army Westside location — 1821 Broadhead Fording Road.

The Pittsburgh Public Schools have also started meal distribution at 25 other locations throughout the city.
