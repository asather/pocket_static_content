Gordmans, off-price apparel and home decor chain, to open stores in February

Gordmans, a Houston-based apparel and home decor retailer, plans to open 23 stores in Pennsylvania, with grand openings scheduled for Feb. 18.

Locally, the off-price chain will open stores in the following locations: Belle Vernon; Ellwood City; Leechburg; Mount Pleasant; and New Castle.

Gordmans is operated by the Houston-based Stage Stores, which also operates Bealls, Goody’s, Palais Royal, Peebles, and Stage. Gordmans targets “mid-sized, Midwest markets,” according to the company’s website.
