Medic hospitalized for cardiac arrest while responding to accident in Swisshelm Park

A medic responding to a bicycle accident in Swisshelm Park on Wednesday was transported to the hospital after going into cardiac arrest at the scene.

Police officials said a cyclist struck a tree and fell down a hillside in Nine Mile Run Hollow near the 1200 block of Commercial Street around 6 p.m.

First responders removed the fallen man with a basket and transported him to UPMC Presbyterian in critical condition.

A responding medic from Rescue 1 went into cardiac arrest while at the scene, and was resuscitated and taken to a local hospital.

Officials said the medic was in “serious but stable” condition.
