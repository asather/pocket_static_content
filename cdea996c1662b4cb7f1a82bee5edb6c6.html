I-79 reopened following crash

Interstate 79 is reopened after a multivehicle crash Sunday night temporarily closed all southbound lanes, PennDOT says.

The shutdown closed traffic between the Crafton and Carnegie exits. PennDOT announced the crash at 10:42 p.m. The road was reopened with a lane restriction around 11:11 p.m.

The crash occurred at mile marker 59.
