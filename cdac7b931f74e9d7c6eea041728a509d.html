Police look for driver who hit Port Authority bus on North Side

Police are looking for the driver of a vehicle that struck a Port Authority bus on the North Side and then fled Friday morning.

The incident occurred around 7:45 a.m. at Cedar Avenue and Pressley Street when an SUV exited a side street and struck the side of a 12-McKnnight bus heading inbound, according to Port Authority officials. The SUV drove off but was later located. The driver was not found, police said.

The bus driver was taken to Allegheny General Hospital with minor injuries.

About a dozen passengers on the bus were not hurt.

Police continue to investigate.
