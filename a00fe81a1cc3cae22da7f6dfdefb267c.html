Biden’s choice

I take umbrage at the tone of the Aug. 13 editorial about Joe Biden’s pick for his running mate (“Why Biden Blew It”) and the nasty opinion piece the same day by right-winger Ben Shapiro (“Joe Biden’s Awful Vice Presidential Pick”). Since when does the editorial writer have entree to the inner workings of Mr. Biden’s mind?

I am confident that Mr. Biden, having interviewed a large number of potential candidates, personally chose the one he felt was best suited for the position. It was his choice.

Kamala Harris is an intelligent, articulate and experienced candidate. Why are old white men so fearful of strong women that they feel they must denigrate them? I happen to appreciate strong women.
