Driver airlifted to hospital after crash in Westmoreland County

A driver was airlifted to a Pittsburgh hospital on Thursday night after crashing in Westmoreland County.

The single-vehicle crash happened at the intersection of Beatty County Road and Meadow Spring Road in Unity, according to the Lloydsville Fire Department.

The driver of a pickup truck was ejected from the vehicle, sustaining traumatic injury. Authorities shut down that area of the road to investigate the crash.
