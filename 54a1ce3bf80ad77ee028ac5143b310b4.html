Fire at Springdale generating station sends one person to hospital

One person was transported to a hospital Friday afternoon after a fire inside the Cheswick Generating Station in Springdale.

According to dispatchers, the call for the fire came in around 5:40 p.m.

Crews from municipalities responded including West Deer, Harrison Township, East Deer, Arnold and Frazer Township.

A supervisor for the plant said he was unable to answer any questions. It was not immediately clear if the injured person was an employee or a first responder.

The Cheswick Generating Station is the last coal-powered plant in Allegheny County. It began operating in 1970.
