Duquesne Light, West Penn Power say almost all electrical power restored after storms, high winds early this week

The remaining few Duquesne Light and West Penn Power customers who have been without power since early Wednesday should have their electricity restored on Saturday.

Duquesne Light, which had about 44,000 without power Thursday, said only one customer in Moon remained without power as of 9:30 a.m. Saturday.

West Penn Power reported five locations in Allegheny, Washington and Westmoreland counties with 20 or less customers without power in each location. Those areas all were expected to have power restored by early Saturday afternoon.

Larger outages in Armstrong and Cambria counties also were expected to be fixed by early Saturday afternoon.
