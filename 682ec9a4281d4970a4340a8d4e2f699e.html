Pittsburgh animal rescue group, food nonprofit to distribute pet and people food Friday

A regional animal rescue organization and a nonprofit food organization will distribute food for both people and pets Friday as a way to help pet owners in need who are struggling to pay for essentials during the COVID-19 pandemic.

The Pittsburgh Aviation Animal Rescue Team, or PAART, has teamed up with 412 Food Rescue for a People and Pups event from 1 to 3 p.m. Friday at McKeesport Area High School on Eden Park Boulevard.

412 Food Rescue will be providing Farm to Family boxes of produce and dairy, and PAART will be providing 30,000 pounds of dog food during the no contact drive-thru distribution.
