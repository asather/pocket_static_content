Man, 70, killed in ATV crash in Washington County

A McDonald man was killed Sunday afternoon when he lost control of a recreational vehicle he was driving.

The Washington County coroner’s office said Angelo Quaresima, 70, was operating a side-by-side ATV near Swihart Road in Cecil when, between 11 a.m. and noon, the vehicle rolled over.

The coroner’s office said Mr. Quaresima was not wearing a seatbelt.

Mr. Quaresima was pronounced dead at 1:35 p.m. 

A cause and manner of death are pending. A cause for the crash was not provided.
