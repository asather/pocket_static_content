Waterworks Cinema private rentals prove popular in first week

MovieScoop, owners of Waterworks Cinema in Fox Chapel, has sold out of its first week of renting out the entire theater for private parties of up to 19 people at a time.

Not to worry if you feel you have missed out: The "$50 luxury rental" offer is being renewed for next week, according to the Waterworks website, so "stay tuned."

The movie theater also is opened for screenings of classic films. For example, there currently are two showings daily of "Back to the Future," "Jaws," "Gremlins," "Ghostbusters" (1984), "The Invisible Man," "The Matrix," "Jurassic Park" and the new film "Irresistible" by Jon Stewart. Waterworks also offers cash-only curbside popcorn pickup for $5, or $7 for gourmet popcorn.

MovieScoop's Indiana Mall Cinema is offering $1 summer movies — plus $3 for mini popcorn and drink — including sensory-friendly showings. Contact-free online ticket purchases are encouraged, with fees waived. 

For a list of safety precautions and requirements, visit https://moviescoop.com/cranberry/covid-19.
