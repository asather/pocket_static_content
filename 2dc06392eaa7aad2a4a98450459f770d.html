Fourth Port Authority employee at West Mifflin garage tests positive for COVID-19

A fourth Port Authority employee at the West Mifflin garage has tested positive for COVID-19. The employee works in maintenance and has been self-quarantining at home since Monday, according to a release from the agency. Another maintenance worker at the West Mifflin facility tested positive for COVID-19 on Friday.

An outside company will perform a deep cleaning of the facility over the weekend. The maintenance team will move to a holiday schedule during that time, said the Port Authority. Transit service will not be impacted.
