Motorcyclist killed after striking guard rail in Washington County

The body of a motorcyclist who crashed into a guard rail in Washington County was found early Saturday morning.

Zachary J. Greyshock, 28, of Monongahela, was discovered around 12:35 a.m. by a passing motorist on Gearing Road in Carroll.

He was pronounced dead at 1:50 a.m., according to the Washington County Coroner’s Office.

An autopsy is pending, and the crash is under investigation by Carroll police.

Authorities said Greyshock was wearing a helmet.

The coroner’s office said it did not know when the crash occurred.

Greyshock was headed north on Gearing Road when he lost control and struck a guard rail, the office said.
