Two hurt —one critically — in Homewood shooting

Police on Wednesday are investigating after two people were shot in Homewood.

The two male victims were shot around 8:30 a.m. in the 7300 block of Bennett Street when they were sitting in a parked vehicle. One of the victims was shot in the head and was privately taken to a local hospital in critical condition. 

Police responding to the ShotSpotter alert encountered the other victim, who had a gunshot wound in his right hand. Medics transported him to a hospital in stable condition.

Violent Crime Unit detectives are investigating the incident.
