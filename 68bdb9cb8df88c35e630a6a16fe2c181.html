Highmark Inc. to affiliate with New York insurer

Highmark Inc., the insurance arm of Highmark Health, on Tuesday announced plans to affiliate with insurer HealthNow New York Inc., the parent company of BlueCross BlueShield of Western New York and BlueShield of Northeastern New York.

Under the agreement, Highmark Health will become the primary licensee of the Blue Cross and Blue Shield Association for HealthNow, which will be renamed Highmark Blue Cross Blue Shield of Western New York and Highmark Blue Shield of Northeastern New York.

Highmark officials, in a release, said the combined entities offer insurance plans for “nearly six million members.”

The transaction also will include HealthNow Administrative Services, with locations in Blue Bell, Montgomery County, and Sacramento, Calif., which performs third-party administrative services for more than 100,000 people.

Last year, HealthNow, with 2,000 employees, reported revenues of $2.8 billion with $702 million in reserves.
