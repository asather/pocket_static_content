Ross commissioners hire township manager/​secretary

Ross commissioners hired Ronald L. Borczyk as township manager/​secretary during Monday’s reorganization meeting.

Mr. Borczyk was most recently the manager in Bellevue and previously served as manager or secretary in Springdale and Sharpsburg. He also served as a commissioner in West Deer from 1997-2007.

“It is really an honor to help guide the business of this great community,” he said.

In other business, Steve Korbel and Dan DeMarco were re-elected as president and vice president of the commission.

New commissioners William McKellar and Sarah Poweska were sworn into office, as well as returning commissioners Patrick Mullin, Jack Betkowski and Mr. DeMarco. North Hills High School junior Katie Duerig was sworn in as “junior commissioner.”

PJ Murray was reappointed as solicitor.

Commissioners will meet on the first and third Mondays of each month at 7 p.m. Exceptions are the Tuesday after a township holiday and the Wednesday following the general election.
