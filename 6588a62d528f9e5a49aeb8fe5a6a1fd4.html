Woman found shot dead in McKeesport

Allegheny County homicide detectives are investigating after a woman was found dead Sunday morning at an intersection in McKeesport.

Police said they received a 911 call around 5:30 a.m. about a body at the intersection of Rogena Street and Riverview Avenue. When police arrived, they found the 32-year-old woman with an apparent gunshot wound to the head.

She was pronounced dead at the scene.

Anyone with information concerning this incident is asked to call the County Police tip line 1-833-ALL-TIPS. Callers can remain anonymous.
