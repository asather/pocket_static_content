Woman arrested for allegedly stabbing a man in Homewood

Pittsburgh Police arrested a woman involved in an alleged stabbing Monday morning in Homewood North.

Police and EMS responded to a report of a male stabbed around 1 a.m. on the 7100 block of Mount Vernon Street.

First responders were met by a male with a cut to his left arm. Medics treated the victim for the non life-threatening injury.

A female suspect was arrested on scene.

Police are investigating.
