Female driver dies after car rolls down hillside in East Hills

A woman died early Sunday after her car rolled down a hillside in the East Hills, according to Pittsburgh public safety.

First responders were sent around 12:15 a.m. to hillside between Oakwood and Haverhill streets where a car had rolled over. The female driver was unconscious.

She was extricated and taken to a hospital.

Medics attempted life-saving procedures, officials said. She was pronounced dead shortly after arriving at the hospital.

Police are investigating the cause.
