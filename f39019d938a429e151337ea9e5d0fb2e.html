Police arrest man in Northview Heights critical shooting

A man has been charged with critically wounding another man in a shooting Monday in Northview Heights.

Ombrah Lane, 32, of Northview Heights was arrested Tuesday by Pittsburgh police.

He is charged with shooting the victim multiple times in the 400 block of Mount Pleasant Road around 12:10 a.m.

The victim was taken to the hospital.

Lane is charged with aggravated assault, tampering with evidence and firearms violations.

He was arraigned and taken to the Allegheny County Jail. Bail was set at $75,000.
