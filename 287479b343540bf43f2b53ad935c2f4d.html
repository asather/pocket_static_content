Employee tests positive for coronavirus at Paramount Senior Living facility in Bethel Park

A staff member at a Paramount Senior Living facility in Bethel Park has tested positive for the coronavirus.

The staff member worked primarily in a secured dementia care unit, the senior care organization told Post-Gazette news partner KDKA-TV. The employee is now quarantined at home as instructed by their doctor.

Paramount officials could not be reached for further comment.

Paramount operates four senior living communities in Western Pennsylvania.
