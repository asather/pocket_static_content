Girl Talk will return to Stage AE for 10th anniversary show

As part of its 10th anniversary, Stage AE goes back to the first artist to play the club with a show by Pittsburgh electronic artist Girl Talk on May 1.

Tickets go on sale at 10 a.m. Thursday for $10 with no service fees, at ticketmaster.com.

Gregg Gillis, aka Girl Talk, played Stage AE in December 2010 after releasing his breakthrough albums “Night Ripper,” “Feed the Animals” and “All Day,” which imaginatively patched together samples from rock and hip-hop songs. He went on to play Coachella and festivals all over the world.

His first North American tour in eight years begins in Cleveland on April 29. Pittsburgh is the second date on the tour.
