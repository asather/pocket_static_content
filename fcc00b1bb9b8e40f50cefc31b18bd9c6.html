Demonstration being held in heart of Downtown

A protest is scheduled to begin Downtown at 2 p.m. and many businesses in the area will be closed.

The demonstration — to draw attention to police brutality, racism and social justice in the wake of the death of George Floyd, a Minneapolis man who died while in police custody — will be held at Wood Street and Liberty Avenue.

Many businesses along Smithfield and Wood streets, and Liberty and Seventh avenues are closed. Other businesses, including a GNC, Domino’s Pizza, KeyBank and 7-Eleven are boarded up.

The driver’s license center at Smithfield and Seventh is closed, as is the entrance to the K&L Gates Center on Sixth Avenue.

As of 1:35 p.m., there were about a dozen protesters gathered and a group of police officers had arrived at Wood Street around 1:45 p.m.
