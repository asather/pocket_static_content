Police investigate Duquesne shooting that left victim hospitalized

County homicide detectives are investigating a shooting that happened Wednesday evening in the city of Duquesne.

Police were notified around 6:49 p.m. of a shooting on the 100 block of Apricot Alley. First responders met a 19-year-old victim who had run to the 200 block of Hamilton Avenue.

The victim was taken to the hospital in stable condition.

Police asked anyone with information about the incident to call their anonymous tip line at 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477).
