South Side Slopes road closed as crews remove car over hillside

South 18th Street is closed indefinitely as crews remove a car which fell over the hillside following an accident early this morning.

Emergency crews were summoned to 2359 S. 18th St. shortly before 3:30 a.m. for a two-vehicle accident.

According to reports, a red Jeep ran into another vehicle, which ran into a car parked along the roadway. The parked car was pushed over the hillside.

The Jeep involved in the accident fled the scene.

There were no injuries reported during the crash.
