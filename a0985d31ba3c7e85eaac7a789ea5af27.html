County releases database of restaurants open for takeout and delivery

The Allegheny County Treasurer’s Office released on Friday an online database of all restaurants in the county providing takeout, curbside pickup and delivery during the COVID-19 crisis.

The 10-page PDF guide is organized by municipality and can be viewed available on the Treasurer’s Office website. It will be updated on a regular basis. 

“In difficult times the small things can go a long way,” Treasurer John Weinstein said in a release.

“It’s so incredibly important that we all do everything we can to minimize person-to-person interactions and reduce the spread of coronavirus. That kind of disruption to our normal lives isn’t an easy thing, though. Something as simple as a different meal can really help prevent senses of isolation or tedium. At the same time, we can help our neighbors in the food service industry by supporting local business.”

The list was compiled by Treasurer’s Office personnel reaching out to establishments registered with the Special Tax Division, which administers the County’s Alcoholic Beverage Tax.

Businesses not currently listed, or those that would like to update their information, are encouraged to contact the County’s Special Tax Division at 412-350-4196 or webmaster.treasurer@AlleghenyCounty.us.
