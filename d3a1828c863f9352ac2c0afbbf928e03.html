Man dies after shooting himself, stepfather in South Fayette

A 49-year-old man died Wednesday after shooting his stepfather and then himself at a home in South Fayette, according to the Allegheny County police department. 

Police responded before 11 p.m. to Caledonia Court following a domestic incident where a man had shot his stepfather, 67, in the face, police said.

The two men have not been identified.

Authorities said the suspect ran from the home after the shooting. When police arrived, he refused to surrender his gun and shot himself in the head.

Both men were taken to a hospital where the 49-year-old died was pronounced dead, officials said. The stepfather is expected to survive.

A motive for the shooting was not immediately known.

Police said they interviewed the surviving man and his wife, who was present at the time of the shooting.
