Train, car collide in Forward Township

Allegheny County has reported minor injuries were sustained following a train and car collision Tuesday.

The county said the collision happened on Peach Street at Gallatin Tigers Road in Forward Township. The rail crossing is near Gallatin-Sunnyside Park.

Police, fire and emergency medical services were dispatched to the scene. Rail traffic has been stopped.
