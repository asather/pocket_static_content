Crews respond to crash on Parkway East after police chase

Police responded to the scene of a car crash Tuesday evening near the Swissvale exit of the Parkway East inbound, according to a county 911 Center official.

According to Monroeville police, the crash occurred because an officer was in pursuit of the vehicle, Post-Gazette news partner KDKA reported.

The vehicle rolled over as police were chasing the suspect on the Parkway East. The chase involved a criminal investigation.

Emergency medical services and firefighters were also dispatched to the location.

The suspect was taken to the hospital and is being treated for non-life-threatening injuries.

The car crash completely blocked the Edgewood-Swissvale off-ramp from the Parkway East inbound, according to Total Traffic and Weather Network, a broadcast content service.
