Man dies in overnight house fire in Troy Hill

A man died in a two-alarm house fire early Friday in Troy Hill.

Firefighters responded around 12:30 a.m. to the 1500 block of Gardner Street near Hatteras Street, and observed flames coming from a first-floor window. 

They entered the house and found an elderly man, who was removed and pronounced dead at the scene.

The fire was under control around 12:45 a.m. No other residents were in the home.

The cause of the fire is under investigation.
