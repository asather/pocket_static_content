Police investigate shooting in Knoxville

Police are investigating an incident in Knoxville where a man was shot Friday morning.

Pittsburgh Police from Zone 3 responded to a Shot Spotter alert around 1:05 a.m. for multiple shots fired in the 300 block of Moore Avenue.

Around the same time, police received a 911 call for someone who had been shot in the same block.

Responding officers found an adult male victim with gunshot wounds to the back and the arm. He had ran inside his residence after being shot.

He was transported to the hospital in stable condition.

Police are investigating.
