Federated Investors to change name and ticker symbol

Downtown-based global investment manager Federated Investors Inc. is set to change its name and stock market ticker symbol at the end of this month to reflect the company’s acquisition of a majority interest in London-based  Hermes Fund Managers Limited.

Effective Jan. 31, Federated’s name will change to Federated Hermes Inc. and its NYSE ticker symbol will switch from FII to FHI. The company’s Class B Common Stock will begin trading under the FHI symbol on Feb. 3.
