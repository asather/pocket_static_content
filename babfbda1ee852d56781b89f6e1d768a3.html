Crash shuts down part of Route 8 in Shaler

A crash shut down a section of Route 8 in Shaler Wednesday morning.

The crash was reported shortly after 8:30 a.m. between Butler Plank Road and Spencer Lane Extension, according to police. It was unclear if there were any serious injuries.

The accident was cleared and Route 8 reopened around 10 a.m.
