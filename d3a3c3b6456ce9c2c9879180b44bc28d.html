Authorities respond to duplex fire in Perry North

Pittsburgh fire, police and EMS responded to a two-alarm fire at a duplex in Pittsburghâ€™s Perry North neighborhood Monday afternoon.

When first responders arrived about 2:20 p.m., they saw a minivan and the house on fire at the corner of Ivory Avenue and Peachtree Way. They said the fire started from a vehicle, which belonged to a neighbor, and then caught onto the house.

Responders also said a family that was home at the duplex when the fire started escaped without injuries. The Red Cross later arrived at the scene to assist the family.

Firefighters were able to extinguish the fire without incident, but a firefighter was later taken to a hospital for an apparent back injury.

The Fire Investigation Unit will determine the cause of the fire.
