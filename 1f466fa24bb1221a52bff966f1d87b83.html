Police investigate early morning Homewood shooting

Pittsburgh police are investigating after a woman walked into a hospital Saturday morning with a gunshot wound to her foot.

The woman, who was brought to the hospital by private means, told police that she was shot overnight on Stranahan Street in Homewood.

Zone 5 officers responded to the 7400 block of Stranahan about 1:15 a.m. Saturday after a ShotSpotter alert picked up more than 20 rounds of gunfire at the location. Police found blood and multiple shell casings at the scene but could not find any shooters or a victim.

The woman, whose name was not released, was admitted to the hospital in stable condition, according to police.
