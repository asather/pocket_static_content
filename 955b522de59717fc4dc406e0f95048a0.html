COVID-19 Update: Allegheny County reports 20 new cases, 1 new death from virus

Allegheny County reported 20 new cases of COVID-19 on Saturday for a total of 2,158 cases. The increase comes on the heels of 16 new cases reported on Friday.

The county also recorded one new death on Saturday following two straight days of zero deaths.

Since the beginning of the pandemic, the county has counted a total of 178 deaths, which includes 166 confirmed deaths and 12 probable.

The number of hospitalizations rose by one for a total of 362 past or present hospitalizations.

Also on Saturday, Pennsylvania reported 504 news cases of COVID-19, for a total of 81,266. The death toll rose by 20 statewide to 6,419.
