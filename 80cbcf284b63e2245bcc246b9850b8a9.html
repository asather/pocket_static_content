Male shot early Saturday on North Side

Pittsburgh police responded to a victim who was shot multiple times early Saturday morning on the North Side.

Police said ShotSpotter initially alerted to a shooting around 1 a.m. in the 2600 block of Perrysville Avenue.

When Zone 1 officers arrived, they found a male victim who had been shot multiple times near a night club. The victim said he did not know the male suspect who shot him and told them he then fled in a vehicle.

The victim was in stable condition at an area hospital, police said.
