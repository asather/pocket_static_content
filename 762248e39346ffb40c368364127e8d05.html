Police: Man injured during carjacking in parking garage of East Liberty’s Target

Police on Wednesday are investigating a carjacking that occurred in the parking garage of the Target store in East Liberty.

Authorities said they responded to a 911 call at the Target in the 6200 block of Penn Avenue around 6 p.m.

When they arrived on the scene, they found a man with “a significant wound” on the back of his head, police said in a release. The victim told police multiple men, possibly teenagers, attacked him. Two of the attackers stole his blue 2011 Jeep Patriot, while at least two others fled on foot.

The victim was transported to a hospital for treatment, the release states.

Police said they are interviewing witnesses and reviewing all available surveillance video.
