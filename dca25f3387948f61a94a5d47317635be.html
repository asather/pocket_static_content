Mid-Atlantic NATAS chapter to honor former KDKA newsman Iannotti, WPXI's Wallace

Former KDKA-TV newsman Ralph Iannotti and WPXI-TV reporter Renee Wallace will be among the 2020 Silver Circle Society member recipients when the Mid-Atlantic Chapter of the National Academy of Television Arts & Sciences celebrates its annual awards program virtually Sept. 19.

The five others are from outside of the Western Pennsylvania area; NATAS Mid-Atlantic draws from Pennsylvania, Delaware and New Jersey.

The Silver Circle Society is made up of journalists with at least 25 years of service.

“It is our pleasure to honor these seven professionals who have kept us informed and engaged in local news for so many years. Their dedication is inspiring,” NATAS chapter president Eric Heisler said in a statement Monday.

In addition, winning entries in professional, college and high school categories will be announced in September.
