Finleyville man killed in ATV accident

A 21-year-old Finleyville man was killed early Saturday when he was struck by an SUV while operating an ATV on the Mon–Fayette Expressway in Union Township, the Washington County Coroner’s office reported.

The victim, Tyler James Gereshenski, was pronounced dead at the scene of the accident at mile marker 44.4 of Route 43, a toll road and part of the Pennsylvania Turnpike. In Pennsylvania, it is illegal to operate an ATV on a public road or highway.

The incident was reported by a passing motorist at approximately 12:15 a.m., the coroner’s office reported. No information was available whether the SUV stopped after the accident.

The manner of death is pending the ongoing investigation by state police. The cause of death is pending an autopsy.
