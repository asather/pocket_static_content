A 4-year-old angler wrestles in carp almost her size

While fishing with her father at Evergreen Pond in Ross, Addyson Hawk, 4. of Ross landed a carp whose length was nearly the same as her height.

“It was really fun watching her do battle with that fish,” said her dad, Dave Hawk.

The big fish was released.
