Sheraden shooting kills at least one person

This story will be updated.

At least one person was killed Saturday night and another was injured after a shooting in Sheraden.

Both victims were male, but a police spokesperson could not confirm their ages. The injured person was transported to the hospital in stable condition.

Police public information officer Maurice Matthews said there was no information on if anyone was in custody.

Police and EMS responded just after 10 p.m. to a ShotSpotter alert that indicated shots were fired in the 3000 block of Bergman Street.

The intersection of Bergman Street and Sherwood Avenue was taped off as police placed evidence markers and investigated the scene.

Neighbors said they heard multiple distinct gunshots.
