Drive-in Easter services offered on Armstrong County big screen

As the global pandemic continues into the weekend, an Armstrong County church found a way to still preach to the masses while practicing social distancing for Easter. 

Harvest Baptist Church in Harrison will be broadcasting Easter services at the Riverside Drive-In theater in Parks Township this weekend, allowing families to view a recorded sermon from their vehicles on the big screen. 

The church is offering six viewing times with two shows a night between Friday and Sunday. Two showtimes were already sold out Friday afternoon as the church warned of limited space.

Harvest Baptist encouraged people in cars to attend a free 7:45 p.m. or 9:15 p.m. time slot each night. 

“We can't wait to celebrate the resurrection of Jesus with you at Riverside Drive-In,” read a post on Harvest Baptist Church’s Facebook page.

A call to Harvest Baptist Church for comment was not immediately returned.
