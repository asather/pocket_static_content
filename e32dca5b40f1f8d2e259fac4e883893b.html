Tuned In: Former Steeler James Harrison lands role in Starz series

Former Steelers linebacker James Harrison has made the move into acting in his post-NFL career, first as a guest star on CBS’s “S.W.A.T.” in 2019 and now as a series regular on Starz’s upcoming original scripted series “Heels.”

Written by Michael Waldron (“Rick and Morty”) and executive produced by Mike O’Malley (“Shameless”), “Heels” follows pro-wrestlers in a small Georgia town.

Harrison, who also acted in the 2019 indie feature “Remember Amnesia,” will play a veteran wrestler with no illusions of fame or glory.

Other cast members announced include Chris Bauer (“True Blood”) and Allen Maldonado (“The Last O.G.”).

In other NFL-to-entertainment news, TheWrap.com reports former new England Patriots tight end Rob Gronkowski, who played high school football for Forest Hills, has signed with WWE and will make his debut on the March 20th episode of Fox’s “SmackDown.”
