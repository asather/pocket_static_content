Fishermen land over 100 slab crappies at Lake Arthur

Joe Fazio of Mount Washington and a friend boated more than 100 slab crappies up to 14 inches on Lake Arthur in Butler County. Mr. Fazio runs the “Crappie Crushin’ TV” YouTube channel and Facebook page.
