Nova chief executive Todd Karran to retire Aug. 1

Nova Chemicals Corp. said Todd Karran, its president and chief executive, will retire on Aug. 1.

A search for his successor is being finalized and an announcement is expected soon, said Musabbeh Al Kaabi, chairman of Novaâ€™s board of directors.

Mr. Karran, 56, joined Nova in 1985 and has served in several executive positions including as chief information officer and chief financial officer prior to being named president and chief executive in 2015.

The company makes chemical and plastic resins and has about 500 employees in the Pittsburgh region at an executive center in Moon, and a plant in Monaca, Beaver County.

Most of its manufacturing operations are in Canada.

The company is owned by Mubadala Investment based in the United Arab Emirates.
