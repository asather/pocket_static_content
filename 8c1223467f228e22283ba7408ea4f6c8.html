Five Finger Death Punch at PPG Paints Arena concert moved to October

The second major PPG Paints Arena concert has been postponed due to concerns about the spread of coronavirus.

Five Finger Death Punch, scheduled for May 14, has been moved to Friday, Oct. 30. That bill was also to include Papa Roach,​Ice Nine Kills and ​I Prevail. Tickets will be honored for the rescheduled date. For any further ticket inquiries, reach out to point of purchase.

Previously, the Rebe McEntire show was moved from March 26 to July 16.

The March 13 Celine Dion was rescheduled for Nov. 18 due to the singer having a cold.
