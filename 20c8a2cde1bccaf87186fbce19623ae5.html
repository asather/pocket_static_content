At least 4 people transported to hospital after accident on Highland Park Bridge

At least four people were injured and the outbound lane on the Highland Park Bridge was shut down for more than an hour Thursday after a vehicle crash, according to Allegheny County.

An Allegheny County 911 Shift Commander said at least four people were transported to a hospital to treat injuries. Details of the injuries were not provided.

The county tweeted the shutdown announcement at 4:33 p.m. and said there were responders on the scene. The lane was cleared at 5:53 p.m.
