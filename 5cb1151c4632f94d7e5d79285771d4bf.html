Report: SWAT unit responds to Manchester home

Pittsburgh police and SWAT units responded Wednesday afternoon to a home in Manchester, where there was a report that a man barricaded himself with a woman and child, according to KDKA-TV, the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette's news partner.

The house is located in the 1100 block of W. North Avenue.

KDKA has since reported that the man was not in the house when SWAT entered and that the suspect is at large.

According to KDKA, an employee with Allegheny County's Office of Children, Youth and Families attempted to enter the home around 11:45 a.m. The worker escaped with another child.
