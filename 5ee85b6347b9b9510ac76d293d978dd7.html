Plea entered in 7-year-old’s Carrick drowning death

A Baldwin woman will serve five years of probation after pleading no contest to endangering the welfare of children after a 7-year-old girl she was babysitting drowned in her care.

Bhima Acharya, 26, was initially charged with involuntary manslaughter, which was withdrawn Monday in exchange for her plea before Allegheny County Common Pleas Judge Thomas E. Flaherty.

Ms. Acharya had been on house arrest since her arrest.

According to police, Ms. Acharya was babysitting Jessica Basnet of Carrick on July 19, 2018, when the girl was found unresponsive in a neighbor’s above-ground swimming pool on Elmwood Drive.
