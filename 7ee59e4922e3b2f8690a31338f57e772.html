Male injured in Hill District shooting

A male was injured in a shooting Sunday morning in the Hill District.

According to Pittsburgh police, the male — no age was provided — was found in the 600 block of Elmore Street with gunshot wounds to his chest and leg. He was taken to a hospital in critical condition.

Police were alerted to the incident by a ShotSpotter notification around 3:30 a.m.

Police said there were not other reports of injuries. They also do not have any information about a suspect.
