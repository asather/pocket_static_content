Represent all

In my humble opinion, I think the November election is a black-and-white election. On one side, I see the forces of Donald Trump, a performer, trying to make our country a dominant white-male society, as it was in the past.

On the other side, I see Joe Biden, a legislator, representing people of every color and background, who want to expand the application of civil rights for all in this country, the future.

This election will be decided by how many people of all backgrounds want to fulfill the promise of our Constitution and vote.
