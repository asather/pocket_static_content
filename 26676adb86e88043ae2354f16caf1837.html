Police investigating shooting in Brighton Heights

A 30-year-old man was shot Monday night in Brighton Heights, Pittsburgh police said.

Police responded about 7:30 p.m. to the intersection of Brighton Road and Woods Run Avenue after receiving an alert from the ShotSpotter gunfire detection system nearby.

Officers found broken glass and blood at the scene, but they did not find a victim. 

A short time later, a man walked into a local hospital with at least one gunshot wound to the leg. He was listed in critical condition.

No arrests have been made as police continue to investigate.
