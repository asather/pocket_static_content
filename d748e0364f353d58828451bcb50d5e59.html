Police searching for two missing teen brothers last seen on North Side

Police are asking for the public’s help in finding two teenage brothers who were last seen Thursday night on the North Side.

Taesean Gibson, 12, is 5’4, weighs 145 pounds, and has brown eyes and black hair. He was last seen wearing blue and white shoes, black pants and a blue jean jacket. His brother, 14-year-old Keisean Gibson, is described as 5’7, weighs 160 pounds and has brown eyes and black hair.

He was last seen wearing blue shoes, black pants and a black/white/gray hooded sweatshirt, police said.

They were last seen at 8:30 p.m. Thursday.

Anyone with information on their whereabouts is asked to call Special Victims Unit detectives at 412-323-7141.
