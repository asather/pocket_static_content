Seth Rogen shot-in-Pittsburgh movie 'An American Pickle' gets Aug. 6 release on HBO Max

“An American Pickle,” the Seth Rogen movie shot in Pittsburgh, has been given an Aug. 6 release date on the new streaming service HBO Max.

Brandon Trost directs Simon Rich’s adaptation of his 2013 New Yorker novella about a laborer (Rogen) who comes to America. After falling into a vat of pickles, he is preserved in the brine for 100 years and in current-day Brooklyn meets his great-grandson (also played by Rogen).

“An American Pickle” is the first HBO Max original film to premiere under the Warner Max label. The service is scheduled to launch May 27.
