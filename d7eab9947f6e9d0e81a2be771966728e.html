Love is in the air as dads and daughters take to the dance floor

Love is in the air with dads, daughters and dance.

More than 150 attended the Ties & Tiaras father/daughter dance at the Bethel Park Community Center Saturday night, featuring a DJ, treats and prizes. The Bethel Park Recreation Department sponsored the event near Valentine’s Day as a way to bring everyone closer together.

“I just wanted to give my girls a good time and to show them how a man should be treating them when they grow up,” said Eric Pinero, of Bethel Park, who danced with his daughters Ava Lynn, 7, and Mari Belle, 5.

Another father, Jason Hill, of West Mifflin, said his 5-year-old daughter Ireland has “talked about this since we attended last year and she’s been looking forward to it ever since, so I couldn’t say no.”
