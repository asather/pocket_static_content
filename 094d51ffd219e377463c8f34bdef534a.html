Nine people taken to hospital after two-vehicle crash in Knoxville

Nine people were taken to the hospital after a car carrying eight people crashed into a van and a house in Knoxville Monday morning.

The accident happened on the 100 block of Zara Street around 11:30 a.m., according to a spokesperson for the city of Pittsburgh. 

Pittsburgh police, EMS and fire all responded to the scene where they found a car with front end damage that had run into the van, then a vacant house and broke a window. 

All eight occupants of the car and the driver of the van were taken to the hospital with minor injuries and in good condition. 

The accident temporarily closed off a portion of Zara Street that has since reopened after the vehicles were towed from the scene. 

Pittsburgh police are investigating the cause of the crash.
