Police seeking arrest of former Pittsburgh residents wanted on multiple charges of child sex abuse

An Ingram woman and New Castle man have been charged with multiple felonies for alleged sexual abuse involving minors that happened several years ago in Pittsburgh.

Trista Price and Seth Aaron Price, both age 36, formerly of Carrick, are accused of more than 15 felonies each including rape, sexual abuse of children and indecent assault.

Police issued warrants for their arrests Tuesday.

Police did not release details on the exact years of the alleged abuse.
