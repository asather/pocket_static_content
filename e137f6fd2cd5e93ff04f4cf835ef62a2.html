Police looking for man last seen in Penn Hills

Authorities are asking for the public's help in locating a 22-year-old man who went missing last month in Penn Hills.

Christian Moore-Rouse was last seen Dec. 21 at the Dollar Tree on Rodi Road.

Police described Mr. Moore-Rouse as black with a light complexion, 5-foot-9 and 135 pounds.

Police said Mr. Moore-Rouse frequents Verona, Penn Hills and Sheraden and often uses public transportation.

Anyone with information is asked to call the Allegheny County Police tip line at 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477). Callers can remain anonymous.
