North Park, South Park golf courses to open Friday

Golf courses at North Park and South Park will open Friday, announced Allegheny County Parks Department.

Daily operating hours of the courses are 6 a.m. to dusk. 

The following operational changes will be in effect at both courses:

• Golf cart usage will be limited to one person per cart.

• All practice greens and driving ranges will be closed.

• Restrooms will be open, but shower facilities will be closed.

• Sand trap rakes and ball washers have been removed.

• Green cups will be blocked, so players will play to the pin.

Snack bars at both courses will be open. Golfers are asked to pay with debit cards and credit cards if possible.

Golfers are asked to observe the following COVID-19 guidelines to help contain the spread of the virus, including wearing a face covering in and around clubhouses and maintain physical distancing.

For fees and additional information on the golf courses, visit www.alleghenycounty.us/golf.

For information, resources and operational changes related to COVID-19, visit www.alleghenycounty.us/coronavirus.
