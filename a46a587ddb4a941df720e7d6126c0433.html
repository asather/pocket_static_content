Airport custom agents seize counterfeit merchandise coming in from Hong Kong

Federal customs agents said they recently seized two shipments of counterfeit merchandise worth an estimated $90,000 from Hong Kong at Pittsburgh International Airport.

Agents said they initially examined the shipments on Dec. 18, the first listed as men's shoes with a destination address in Pittsburgh and the second listed as phone cases and headed for an address in Blawnox.

The first package contained a Rolex watch, Louis Vuitton bracelet and various high-end clothing. The second one had designer jewelry and similar items.

After determining that the items were all counterfeit, Customs and Border Protection seized the shipments last week.
