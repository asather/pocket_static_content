Man, 20, killed in motorcycle accident along Route 130 in Penn Township

A 20-year old was killed Sunday evening in a motorcycle accident along Route 130 in Penn Township, Westmoreland County, according to Post-Gazette news partner KDKA.

The accident happened around 9 p.m. Sunday.

Penn Township Police told KDKA the driver lost control of the bike and hit a guardrail while trying to pass an SUV.

He was pronounced dead at the scene of the crash.
