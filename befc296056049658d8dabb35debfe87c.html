WPIAL basketball playoff clinchings after Feb. 3

Section play in WPIAL basketball ends this weekend and 133 teams have clinched playoff spots.

After Monday night’s games, 66 boys teams and 67 girls teams have earned playoff berths. Some spots are still open.

The top four teams in each section make the playoffs. If there is a tie for a playoff spot among two or more teams, head-to-head competition will break the tie. If head-to-head doesn’t break the tie, all teams will qualify.

The WPIAL will announce playoff pairings and seedings Feb. 11. Here are the teams that have clinched playoff spots:
