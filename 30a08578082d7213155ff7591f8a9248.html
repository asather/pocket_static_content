Carrick teen girl missing since Saturday; Pittsburgh police seek assistance

Pittsburgh police are asking for assistance in searching for a 14-year-old girl missing since Saturday.

Karen Dubon-Varela is 5 foot, 5 inches, 130 pounds, with shoulder-length black hair and brown eyes, and was last seen leaving her home in Carrick.

Police also say that Ms. Dubon-Varela may have traveled to New York, Virginia or Florida.

Authorities are asking anyone with information about her whereabouts to call 911.
