Man in critical condition after being shot multiple times in Hill District

A man is in critical condition after being shot multiple times in the Hill District Friday.

Pittsburgh police responded to a ShotSpotter alert and multiple 911 calls for gunfire around 6:45 p.m. at the corner of Roberts Street and Bedford Avenue, according to police.

Zone 2 officers found a man on the sidewalk with multiple gunshot wounds and rendered aid until medics arrived.

The man was taken to a local hospital.

The investigation continues, according to police.
