Vandergrift man arrested, charged with committing multiple crimes during East Liberty protest

A Vandergrift man has been arrested on charges related to the June 1 protest of racism and police brutality in East Liberty.

Pittsburgh police reported that Benjamin Reynolds, 28, faces charges of aggravated assault, riot, propulsion of missiles, failure to disperse, disorderly conduct, and obstruction of highways or other public passages in relation to the protest. He was arraigned Friday evening on the charges and released on his own recognizance pending a July 20 preliminary hearing.

The arrest was made by investigators with the police bureau’s Damage Assessment and Accountability Task Force with assistance from the Westmoreland County Sheriff’s Department and the Vandergrift Police Department.
