Traffic alert: Inbound side of Fort Pitt Tunnel to close briefly Thursday morning

Scheduled maintenance will force a 15-minute shutdown the inbound side of the Fort Pitt Tunnel on Thursday morning, according to PennDOT.

Traffic moving eastbound along Interstate 376 will be stopped at the entrance to the tunnel beginning at 10 a.m. and last approximately 15 minutes so that crews can do lighting repair work.
