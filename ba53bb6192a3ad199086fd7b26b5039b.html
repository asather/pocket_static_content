Mt. Lebanon commissioners approve several road-related projects

Mt. Lebanon Commissioners approved several road-related projects at its April 14 regular meeting, which was held virtually due to COVID-19.

Improvements are coming to the intersection of Hoodridge Drive and Rockwood Avenue.

A $67,220 bid from Baiano Construction Inc., of Pittsburgh, was unanimously approved for the intersection project which includes removal of the existing curb and sidewalk and the installation of concrete sidewalks and ramps. The project also includes replacement of the roadway as well as signage and traffic control.

Commissioners also awarded a $372,700 contract with A. Folino Construction Inc., of Oakmont, for the municipalityâ€™s street maintenance program, which includes roto-milling of existing pavement and resurfacing to various depths on streets throughout Mt. Lebanon.

The streets included in the project are: Anawanda Avenue from Parkside Avenue to Broadmoor Drive; Coolidge Avenue from Oak Way to Shady Drive West; Country Club Drive from Sleepy Hollow Road to the municipal line; Inglewood Drive from Seneca Drive to 164 Inglewood; North Meadowcroft Avenue from McMonagle Road to Greenlawn Drive; Overlook Drive from Akron Avenue to Beverly Road; Shady Drive West from Mapleton Drive to Coolidge Avenue; and Sleepy Hollow Road from the dead end to Fruithurst Drive.

A $2 million road reconstruction program bid was awarded to Niando Construction of Penn Hills.

Some of the roads set to be reconstructed in 2020 include: Firwood Drive from 1225 Firwood to 1145 Firwood; Helen Drive from Holly Lane to 1739 Helen; Hemlock Street from Hazel Avenue to 15 Hemlock and Parker Drive from Overlook Drive to Hillcrest Place, among others.

Commissioners will next meet for a virtual meeting on April 29.
