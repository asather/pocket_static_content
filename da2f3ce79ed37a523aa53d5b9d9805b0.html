Traffic alert: Elizabeth Bridge will be closed for 45 minutes Wednesday morning

Crews are planning a short-term shutdown of the Elizabeth Bridge on Wednesday.

PennDOT said the closure — weather permitting — is expected to last approximately 45 minutes and will begin at 9 a.m.

The bridge carries traffic on Route 51 across the Monongahela River.

The closure will allow workers to do cable replacement work.

PennDOT has posted the following detours:

Motorists traveling northbound on Route 51

• Approaching the Elizabeth Bridge, take the ramp to Elizabeth/Glassport

• Turn right onto N. Third Avenue

• N. Third Avenue becomes McKeesport Road

• McKeesport Road becomes Lincoln Boulevard

• Lincoln Boulevard becomes Glassport-Elizabeth Road

• Turn left and cross the Clairton-Glassport Bridge

• Turn left onto southbound Route 837 (N. State Street)

• Take the ramp toward Elizabeth/Pittsburgh

• Merge onto northbound Route 51 toward Pittsburgh

• End detour

Motorists traveling southbound on Route 51

• Take the ramp to North Route 837 toward Clairton

• Follow Route 837 (State Street) northbound to the Clairton-Glassport Bridge

• Turn right and cross the bridge

• Turn right onto Glassport-Elizabeth Road

• Glassport-Elizabeth Road becomes Lincoln Boulevard

• Lincoln Boulevard becomes McKeesport Road

• McKeesport Road becomes N. Second Avenue

• Turn left onto Market Street

• Follow straight onto the ramp back to southbound Route 51

• End detour
