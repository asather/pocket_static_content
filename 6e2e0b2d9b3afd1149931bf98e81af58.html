Man dies after being shot in vehicle at Wilkinsburg intersection

A 27-year-old man who was shot inside his vehicle Sunday at a Wilkinsburg intersection died after being taken to the hospital, Allegheny County police said.

Police said, based on witness accounts, that two vehicles were side by side at Ardmore Boulevard and Marlboro Avenue when the shooting took place. 

The shooting happened around 11:26 a.m. The victim’s identity has not been released.

Police said they are looking for “a greenish silver midsize sedan with either temporary or out of state plates fleeing the area on Marlboro Avenue.”

Anyone with information is asked to call the County Police tip line 1-833-ALL-TIPS. Callers can remain anonymous.
