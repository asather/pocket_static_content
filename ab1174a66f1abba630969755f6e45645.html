Man in stable condition after Saturday afternoon Downtown shooting

A man was shot Downtown on Fort Duquesne Boulevard Saturday afternoon.

Pittsburgh Police responded to a report of a shooting on Fort Duquesne Boulevard between the 7th and 9th Street bridges around 11:15 a.m. and discovered a man shot on the 100 block of 7th Street. He was conscious and alert with two gunshot wounds, according to police.

Medics transported him to a hospital in stable condition.

Police are searching for the shooter, who fled the scene.
