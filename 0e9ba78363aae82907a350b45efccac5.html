City will collect trash on Good Friday; yard debris pickup canceled

Trash collection will continue as usual this week in Pittsburgh, including on the Christian holiday of Good Friday.

However, the springtime yard debris collection scheduled for April 25 has been canceled “due to precautions for the crews during the COVID-19 pandemic,”  the city announced Wednesday.

Any bagged yard debris and “bulky waste” items left out at the curb will be left behind by Environmental Services crews.

The city is reminding residents that regular trash must be bagged in tied-up, leak-proof bags and without tears.
