Man, 48, killed in Route 28 crash in Harrison

The Allegheny County Medical Examiner’s Office on Wednesday identified a 48-year-old man who died in a car crash Tuesday night on Route 28 in Harrison.

Charles Crowe, of Natrona Heights, was pronounced dead around 8 p.m., the medical examiner’s office said.

Police responded around 7:40 p.m. to crash near Exit 15. They said Mr. Crowe had been traveling north on Route 28 when the accident occurred. He was pronounced dead at the scene, officials said.

It was not immediately clear if another vehicle was involved.

State police are investigating.
