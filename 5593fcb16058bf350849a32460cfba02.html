City Theatre names Clare Drobot associate artistic director

City Theatre has restructured some of its top jobs following the recent exit of Reginald Douglas as artistic producer.

That title, created when he came to work for then artistic director Tracy Brigden, is no more. Current artistic director Marc Masterson on Wednesday announced a new staff structure for the 45-year-old South Side theater.

Clare Drobot, previously the director of new play development, has been promoted to the role of associate artistic director, and Katie Trupiano is now education director, a role previously held by Dennis Robinson. Filling new new positions are Monteze Freeland, a former Post-Gazette Performer of the Year, as associate producer, and Rebekah Diazjoining as community engagement officer.
