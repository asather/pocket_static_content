Giant Eagle recalls onions, deli products due to salmonella outbreak

Onions sold at Giant Eagle are included in a list of products recalled due to a salmonella outbreak, Post-Gazette news partner KDKA reported.

The Food and Drug Administration says that more than 400 people have gotten sick from onions sold across 31 states.

Thomson International, a produce company from Bakersfield, Calif., is likely the source of the contamination, KDKA said.

Giant Eagle is recalling the onions, as well as several deli items that contain the items.
