One man dead, another critical in Westmoreland County shooting

One man is dead and another is in critical condition after an early morning shooting on Sunday in Westmoreland County.

Michael Brown, 24, of Rostraver and 44-year-old David Hoffman of Ruffs Dale shot at each other at a property on Mendon Road in South Huntingdon, according to state police. Mr. Hoffman was killed in the exchange, and Mr. Brown was flown to a Pittsburgh hospital for his injuries.

Troopers are investigating the case as a homicide. The incident occurred just before 3 a.m. Sunday morning.
