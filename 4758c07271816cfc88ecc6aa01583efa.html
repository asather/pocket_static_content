World struggles to comprehend X Æ A-12 — the name of Elon Musk’s new baby

Welcome to planet Earth, little X Æ A-12 Musk.

The first child of SpaceX and Tesla boss Elon Musk and his musician girlfriend Grimes apparently has a name so out of this world, fans were left puzzling over how to pronounce it Tuesday.

Musk announced the birth late Monday and posted a photo of the sweet infant with a filter that gave him tiny face tattoos.

“Mom & baby all good,” he said in another tweet.

Asked to reveal the baby’s name, Musk tweeted, “X Æ A-12 Musk.”

The futuristic name, real or not, sent fans racing to decipher a possible pronunciation.

One follower guessed “X Ash Archangel?” while giving screenshots of evidence gathered to support the proposal.

Musk liked the tweet, apparently hinting it was at least on the right track.

The billionaire Tesla CEO has been making nonstop headlines recently with his call for an end to pandemic stay-at-home orders and a vow to sell off most of his possessions, including his homes.

He sent Tesla stock tanking when he said Friday he considered the shares overvalued.

Two of Musk’s pricey properties showed listings on the real estate industry website Zillow Tuesday that apparently were posted Monday.

His seven-bedroom, 11-bathroom house bordering the Bel-Air Country Club was listed for a whopping $30 million, though it wasn’t immediately clear if Musk was actually entertaining offers.

His five-bedroom house directly across the street, which previously belonged to actor Gene Wilder, was listed on Zillow for $9.5 million.
