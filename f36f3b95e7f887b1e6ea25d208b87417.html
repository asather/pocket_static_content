Westmoreland County commissioner tests positive for COVID-19

A member of the Westmoreland County board of commissioners has tested positive for COVID-19, county officials said Tuesday.

Commissioner Doug Chew told the board of his diagnosis on Tuesday and is following “quarantine recommendations,” officials said.

The county’s human resources staff has begun contact tracing, and officials have started taking the temperatures of visitors who enter the county government building on North Main Street in Greensburg.

Also, board meetings have been canceled this week as a precaution.
