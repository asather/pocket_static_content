Male found shot on Arlington Avenue

A male is hospitalized in critical condition after he was found with a gunshot wound early Sunday on the border between South Side Slopes and Mount Oliver.

Pittsburgh police said a Zone 3 officer was flagged down by another person around 1:20 a.m. The person directed the officer to the driveway of an American Legion in the 1700 block of Arlington Avenue.

The officer found the victim with a gunshot wound to his side. Police have not specified where the shooting took place.

The victim was taken to a hospital.

Police said there were not other reported injuries. They added that there is no description of suspects.
