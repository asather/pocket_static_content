Bethel Park school superintendent resigns

Bethel Park School District Superintendent Joseph W. Pasquerilla will leave the district on May 3 to become superintendent of the Ambridge Area School District.

The Bethel Park school board unanimously accepted Mr. Pasquerilla’s resignation at its Tuesday night meeting.

Earlier this month, Mr. Pasquerilla signed a five-year contract to be superintendent of the Ambridge district, replacing Joan Welter, at a salary of $165,000.

Mr. Pasquerilla worked at Ambridge from 2009 to 2012 and served as director of curriculum, instruction and assessment and assistant to the superintendent.

He has been Bethel Park’s superintendent since 2017 and prior to that he was superintendent at Northgate School District.

In other business, the board unanimously hired Nicholas Fierst as the district’s director of facilities and services at a salary of $75,000 per year. He previously worked as facilities director in the West Jefferson Hills School District.

Mr. Fierst takes over for William Keith, who is retiring from the position in March.
