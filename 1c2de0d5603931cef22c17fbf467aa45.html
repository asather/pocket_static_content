Suspects sought in McKeesport shooting that left two 15-year-olds critically injured

Police are searching for two suspects believed to be “armed and dangerous” in connection with a shooting in McKeesport on Tuesday night that left two 15-year-old boys critically injured.

The suspects were driving a bronze 2020 Chevy Malibu, according to Allegheny County police Lt. Venerando Costa. The car has a Wisconsin license plate with the number ABT-6967.

Police have not provided a description of the suspects.

Authorities responded just before 9:30 p.m. Tuesday to the 900 block of Craig Street and found the two teens shot, police said. They were taken to a local hospital in critical condition, but are expected to survive their injuries, Mr. Costa confirmed Wednesday morning.

No arrests have been made. 

Anyone with information concerning the shooting is asked to call the Allegheny County police Tip Line at 1-833-ALL-TIPS. Callers can remain anonymous.
