TRAFFIC: Clemente Bridge closures, Route 28 restrictions scheduled

Motorists will experience travel restrictions with the closing of the Roberto Clemente Bridge for two Saturdays and for Route 28 roadwork on Route 28.

The Clemente Bridge will be closed between 7 a.m. and 5 p.m. this Saturday and on Sept. 19 for a bridge inspection, according to Allegheny County officials. Traffic will be detoured using Fort Duquesne Boulevard, the Andy Warhol Bridge, and Isabella Street.

 

Also, there will be single-lane closures on Route 28 in both directions this weekend — from 7 p.m. Friday through 5 a.m. Monday — between the Millvale interchange (Exit 3B) in Shaler and the Highland Park Bridge. Crews will be working on concrete patching, the bridge deck, paving operations, and guide rail and drainage upgrades, utility adjustments, and signing and pavement marking improvements.

Ongoing work on that stretch of Route 28 will take place through spring 2021.
