Allegheny County reports fourth COVID-19 death

A fourth person in Allegheny County has died from COVID-19, health officials reported Sunday.

The number of confirmed novel coronavirus cases in the county also rose to 605, up 53 cases from Saturday.

The fourth death was a person who was in their 80s and had been hospitalized.

County health officials also gave an updated breakdown of diagnosed COVID-19 cases by age:

Ages 0 to 4 — 3 (less than 1%)

Ages 5 to 12 — 4 (less than 1%)

Ages 13 to 18 — 9 (1%)

Ages 19 to 24 — 56 (9%)

Ages 25 to 49 — 263 (43%)

Ages 50 to 64 — 164 (27%)

Ages 65 and up — 106 (18%)
