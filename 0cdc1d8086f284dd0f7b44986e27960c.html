Girl, 15, killed by train in South Fayette

A 15-year-old girl was killed Wednesday after she was hit by a train in South Fayette.

Allegheny County police said the girl was with at least four other teenagers — two girls and two boys — during the incident, which happened around 11:07 a.m. near Miller’s Run Road and Wabash Avenue.

One of the girls, a 17-year-old, was injured and taken to the hospital. She is expected to survive, police said. 

Neither victim has been identified.

The other witnesses include a 14-year-old girl and two boys, ages 15 and 17.

No other information was immediately available.

Anyone with information concerning this incident is asked to call the County Police tip line at 1-833-ALL-TIPS. Callers can remain anonymous.
