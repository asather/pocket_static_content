Male shot in stomach in South Side Slopes

A male was shot in the stomach Monday afternoon in the South Side Slopes.

The victim was taken to the hospital in critical condition. His age was not known.

There were no reports of other victims, and Pittsburgh police had no suspect descriptions.

The shooting happened around 1 p.m. at Eleanor and Eccles streets.

Police responded to an alert by the ShotSpotter gunfire detection system in the 1400 block of Eleanor Street.

Officers found the victim, who was conscious and alert.

A structure was also struck by gunfire, police said.
