Fire causes Westmoreland County home to partially collapse

A home has partially collapsed due to a fire in Mount Pleasant, Westmoreland County.

Fire companies were called to the scene at 12:58 a.m. Thursday to the 1600 block of Kecksburg Road.

The family was able to make it out safely as heavy smoke billowed from the home, according to Pittsburgh Post-Gazette news partner KDKA.

Many area fire companies were called to help battle the blaze, KDKA said. 

The cause of the fire has not yet been determined.
