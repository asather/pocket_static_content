Man charged with DUI in fatal pedestrian accident in Westmoreland County

A man has been charged with driving under the influence in the death of a female pedestrian in New Stanton last week. 

Susan Michelle Clawson, 44, of Uniontown, Fayette County, was struck by a vehicle driven by a 40-year-old man, also from Uniontown, around 1 a.m. on Sept. 10, Pennsylvania State Police said. According to authorities, the man crashed in the roundabout at Bair Boulevard and Rachel Drive, and was driving in reverse when he struck Ms. Clawson, who was walking in the roundabout.

She was pronounced dead at the scene.

State police did not identify the suspect. They could not be reached for comment Tuesday.
