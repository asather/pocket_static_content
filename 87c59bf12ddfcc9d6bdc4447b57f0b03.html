Taylor Gang adds Northview Heights rapper Fedd the God to the roster

Taylor Gang Entertainment has a new artist on the roster.

The label founded in 2008 by Pittsburgh rap star Wiz Khalifa has added 26-year-old Northview Heights rapper Fedd the God, whose real name is James William Fedd Jr.

He joins a Taylor Gang crew that also includes Juicy J, Ty Dolla $ign, Berner and Chevy Woods.

With the announcement comes his debut single “King James,” produced by Sledgren and Chris Dreamer.

“We are extremely excited to bring Fedd into the family and can’t wait to see him grow!” Taylor Gang president Will Dzombak said in a statement.

According to a Taylor Gang release, Fedd the God “earned a reputation for throwing wild parties” and he formed a bond with Dzombak after the rapper got into a fight with a friend of the TG president.

Fedd the God’s project “The Feddko Zone” will arrive later this summer.
