Retransmission dispute between WPXI, Dish, leaves subscribers in the dark

As of Wednesday, Pittsburgh-area Dish subscribers will not be able to watch WPXI-TV until a retransmission dispute is settled between the satellite provider and Apollo-controlled Cox Media Group.

Such legal disputes are not uncommon. In this case, nine markets are affected in addition to Pittsburgh: Atlanta (WSB-TV), Boston (WSOC-TV), Charlotte (WAXN-TV), Dayton (WHIO-TV), Jacksonville (WFOX-TV), Memphis (WHBQ-TV), Orlando (WFTV), Seattle (KIRO-TV) and Tulsa (KMYT-TV).
