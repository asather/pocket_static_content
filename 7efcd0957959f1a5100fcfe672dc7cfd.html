Two people rescued from Greenfield house fire

Two people were rescued early Friday morning from a house fire on Bigelow Street.

Several crews responded to the call at a two-story home at 637 Bigelow St. around 3:45 a.m. 

Crews were needed to extract a person from the second floor, according to reports from Pittsburgh Post-Gazette news partner KDKA.
