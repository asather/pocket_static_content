Six injured in crash involving Port Authority bus on Highland Park Bridge

A crash involving a Port Authority bus Saturday afternoon on the Highland Park Bridge injured six people.

Port Authority officials said victims have been transported to local hospitals and that none of the injuries are life-threatening.

An inbound Route 75 bus and a passenger vehicle collided on the on-ramp of the bridge at Freeport Road around 3 p.m. Saturday, forcing the southbound right lane to be shut down for about half an hour, according to PennDOT and Port Authority officials.

The lane reopened shortly before 4 p.m.

The incident is currently under investigation by port authority police.
