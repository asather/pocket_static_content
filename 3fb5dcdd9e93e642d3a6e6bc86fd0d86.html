Allegheny County again reports 0 deaths from COVID-19

For the third day in a row, Allegheny County is reporting no new deaths from COVID-19, according to data released Monday by the county health department.

The number of deaths stands at 168 — 156 are confirmed and 12 are probable.

The county is reporting five new cases of COVID-19, bringing the total number of cases to 2,008 — 1,881 are confirmed cases and 127 are probable.

Of the total cases, 279 — 14% — are in healthcare workers, the county said.

Pennsylvania on Monday reported 351 new cases of COVID-19 and 10 new deaths, according to the state Department of Health.

The state has gone 29 consecutive days of reporting less than 1,000 cases. The total number of cases is now 75,943 and the total number of deaths is 5,953.
