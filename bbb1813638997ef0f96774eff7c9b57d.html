Dr. Chong S. Park named president of two AHN hospitals

Long-time Allegheny Health Network surgeon Dr. Chong S. Park has been named president of AHNâ€™s Jefferson and Canonsburg hospitals. He succeeds Louise Urban, who has been appointed AHN senior vice president of Operations.
