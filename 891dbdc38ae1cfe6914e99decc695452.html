Allegheny County police investigate Pitcairn shooting

Allegheny County police on Thursday said they are investigating a Wednesday evening shooting in Pitcairn.

Pitcairn police officers responded around 7:10 p.m. to reports of shots fired at the intersection of Fourth Street and Highland Avenue. County police said officers found evidence of the shooting but did not locate any victims.

Authorities later learned that a 16-year-old boy had been shot in the arm and he was taken to a local hospital by a family member. Police said he is in stable condition.

County police asked anyone with knowledge of the incident to call their tipline at 1-833-ALL-TIPS (1-833-255-8477). Callers can remain anonymous.
