WPIAL 4A-3A roundup: Chartiers Valley stays unbeaten

Anthony Mackey threw three touchdown passes and ran for two more to lead Chartiers Valley to a 41-14 win over Blackhawk in the Parkway Conference.

The Colts, ranked No. 4 in WPIAL Class 4A, are 4-0 for the first time since 2009 and 2-0 in the conference. The visiting Cougars (2-2, 1-1) suffered their first conference loss.

Mackey connected with Abe Ibrahim on scoring passes of 9 and 7 yards and with Socrates Boulis on a 4-yard scoring pass. He also ran for touchdowns of 17 and 7 yards.

Blackhawk’s Zach Ours returned a kickoff 90 yards for a touchdown.

More Class 4A

Plum 20, Highlands 7: Eryck Moore rushed for 156 yards on 23 carries and scored on a 32-yard run as the No. 5-ranked Mustangs (4-0, 4-0) defeated the visiting Golden Rams (1-3, 1-3) in the Greater Allegheny Conference.

Belle Vernon 49, Laurel Highlands 0: Devin Whitlock ran for two touchdowns, threw for two more, and returned an interception for another to lead the No. 2 ranked Leopards (3-1, 3-1) to a road victory over the Mustangs (1-3, 1-2) in the Big Eight Conference.

Trinity 32, West Mifflin 5: Connor Roberts threw three touchdown passes to Micah Finley to lead the host Hillers (2-2, 2-2) to a convincing win over the Titans (1-3, 1-3) in the Big Eight Conference. Finley also scored on a 39-yard run.

Hampton 42, Greensburg Salem 9: Gage Galuska returned a kickoff 94 yards and an interception 40 yards for touchdowns as the visiting Talbots (3-1, 3-1) defeated the Golden Lions (1-3, 1-3) in the Greater Allegheny Conference.

Class 3A

North Catholic 66, Burrell 8: Joey Prentice threw three touchdown passes and ran for three more as the No. 3 ranked Trojans (4-0, 4-0) dominated the visiting Buccaneers (1-3, 1-2) in the Allegheny Seven Conference. Prentice threw for 232 yards and rushed for 112.

Keystone Oaks 20, Avonworth 13: Logan Shrubb became the fifth player in WPIAL history to throw for 5,000 yards and rush for 2,000 in his career as the No. 5-ranked Golden Eagles (3-1, 2-0) edged the visiting Antelopes (2-2, 1-1) in the Northwestern Six Conference. Shrubb ran for two touchdowns and threw for another.

Mount Pleasant 43, Yough 32: Pete Billey rushed for 138 yards and three touchdowns, as the host Vikings (3-1, 3-0) outscored the Cougars (1-3, 0-3) in the Interstate Conference. Asher O’Conner connected with Donavin Bair on two touchdowns.

East Allegheny 35, Deer Lakes 0: Michael Smith threw for 118 yards and two touchdowns to lead the visiting Wildcats (3-1, 2-1) to an Allegheny Seven Conference win over the Lancers (0-4, 0-4). Smith also scored on a 3-yard run.
