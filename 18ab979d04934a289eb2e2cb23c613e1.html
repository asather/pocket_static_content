One injured in motorcycle, car crash near Kittanning

At least one person was injured when a motorcycle and vehicle collided in Armstrong County late Thursday.

The crash was reported at 9:30 p.m. on Route 422 near the Sheetz convenience store in Manor, just outside Kittanning, according to county emergency officials.

Police continue to investigate the cause of the crash.
