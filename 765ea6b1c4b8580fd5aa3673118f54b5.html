Police search for missing Hill District teen

Police are searching for a missing 19-year-old from the Hill District.

William Battle was last seen Wednesday wearing a blue denim jacket and black jeans. He is 5 feet, 6 inches tall, weighs about 125 pounds and has medium-length dreadlocks with blonde tips.

Officials asked anyone with information on his whereabouts to call the Special Victims Unit at 412-323-7141.
