PennDOT begins work Monday on Forbes Road in Monroeville, Trafford

The Pennsylvania Department of Transportation announced on Friday morning that slide remediation work on Forbes Road in Monroeville and Trafford will begin on Monday, July 27.

The announcement said that the road work, which begins at approximately 9 a.m. on Monday, will close Forbes road to traffic between Haymaker Road and Westmoreland Road/Stewart Station Drive through to mid-November.

Through traffic will be detoured via Route 130 (7th Street/Forest Avenue/Broadway Boulevard) through Trafford.

An official at PennDOT said that average daily traffic on the roadway is about 5,700 vehicles, but that traffic is still down due to COVID-19. PennDOT therefore does not expect any major traffic disruptions due to the reduced traffic volume and straightforward detour route.

The announcement also said that slide remediation, shoulder reconstruction, paving and other miscellaneous construction work will occur on the $1.34 million project.
