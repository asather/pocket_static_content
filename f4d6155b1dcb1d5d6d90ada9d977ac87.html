Show us your Pittsburgh snow day

Sledding at Blue Slide Park.

A parking chair guarding a shoveled-out space.

Grocery store shelves bereft of milk, break and TP.

Pittsburgh has plenty of idiosyncratic ways we observe a snow day. And we want to see yours. 

Snap a pic of how you’re enjoying — or maybe not enjoying — today’s snow and email it to socialmedia@post-gazette.com. Please include your name and the location of the image.

We’ll pick our favorites and post them on the site later today.
