Couple fined for disregarding stay-at-home order

A Westmoreland County couple have been cited for violating the state’s stay-at-home order that went into effect in late March.

According to Pennsylvania State Police, a trooper pulled over the couple — Kara Lawson, 27, and Scott Renwick, 28, both of Loyalhanna — who were in a vehicle on Route 819 in Bell on April 11.

State police said Lawson and Renwick admitted they were at a friend’s house before they got into an argument and not at home.

They’re facing up to $300 in fines for the summary offenses.
