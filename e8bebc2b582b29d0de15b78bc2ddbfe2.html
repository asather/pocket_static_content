People on the move: 4/13//2020

Architecture

Meghan Strahler joined Bergmann as a project engineer, Atlantic Infrastructure group.

Associations and Organizations

Pittsburgh Center for Creative Reuse elected the following officers and board members: president: Adam Nye, Winchester Thurston School; vice president: Brian Savolskis, Highmark Health; treasurer: Sarah Simkin, Pennsylvania Office of the Attorney General; and secretary: Rick Soria, Soria & Associates Consulting. Board members include: Alicia Donner, Advantage Credit Counseling Service; and Candace Johnson, Comcast Business Services.

Banking

Cfsbank hired John C. Gill as president and CEO.

Marketing

BLD Marketing appointed Amy Meyers, director of media, and hired Rachel Vigliotti, account manager, and Michael Hogan, digital strategist.

Nonprofit

The Crohn’s & Colitis Foundation elected Rhonda Bolding to its national board of trustees, following six years of service on the Western Pennsylvania/West Virginia chapter board. The foundation’s national board of trustees oversees the control and management of the policies, practices, funds and property of the organization.

Awards and Honors

CoStar Group, a provider of commercial real estate information, analytics and online marketplaces, recognized the industry’s brokers, power players and deal-makers who closed the highest transaction volume in commercial real estate deals in their markets in 2019. Top industrial leasing brokers: Evan Cicirello, Cushman & Wakefield | Grant Street Associates Inc.; Jack O’Donoghue, Cushman & Wakefield | Grant Street Associates Inc.; Jonathan Bonime, Fischer & Co.; Greg Hopkins, JLL; and Nick Jacobs, JLL. Top office leasing brokers: Scott Diguglielmo, Burns Scalo Brokerage LLC; Jeremy Kronman, CBRE; Dan Adamski, Joe Goodman and Nick Francic, JLL. Top retail leasing brokers: Mark Anderson, Colliers International | Pittsburgh; Brian Kerr, Jon Knudsen and Tommy McCafferty, Hanna Langholz Wilson Ellis; and Rob Lang, Kamin Realty Co. Top leasing firms: Burns Scalo Brokerage LLC, CBRE, Cushman & Wakefield | Grant Street Associates Inc., Hanna Langholz Wilson Ellis and JLL. Top sales brokers: Kyle Prawdzik and Richard Gasperini, CBRE; Randall McCombs and Sam McGill, Cushman & Wakefield | Grant Street Associates Inc.; and Mark Popovich, JLL. Top sales firms: CBRE, Cushman & Wakefield | Grant Street Associates Inc., Hanna Langholz Wilson Ellis, JLL and SVN: Three Rivers Commercial Advisors.
