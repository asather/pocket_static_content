Man hospitalized after being shot in McKees Rocks

Allegheny County police on Sunday are investigating after a McKees Rocks man was hospitalized with a gunshot wound.

McKees Rocks police responded around 3:23 p.m. to reports of a shooting on the 500 block of Meyers Street.

Authorities found a man with a gunshot wound in his lower leg. He was taken to the hospital in stable condition.

Investigators learned that the man was seen handling a gun outside a residence on Meyers Street when the gunfire was heard. Officials said there were “conflicting accounts of the incident.”
