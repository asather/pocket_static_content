Man charged in shooting death outside restaurant in Fayette County

A Fayette County man has been charged with homicide in the shooting death of another man outside the Long John Silvers eatery in South Union over the weekend.

Pennsylvania State Police arrested Justin D. Rittacco, 21, of Fairchance, Fayette County, in the killing of Robert Lukehart, 39, also of Fairchance.

Police said they were called to the restaurant on Morgantown Road shortly before 4:30 p.m. Sunday and found Mr. Lukehart suffering from multiple gunshot wounds outside. He was pronounced dead at the scene.

Troopers took Mr. Rittacco into custody. He is being held in the Fayette County Prison awaiting arraignment on the charges.
