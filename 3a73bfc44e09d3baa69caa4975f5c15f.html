People on the move: 4/6/2020

Law

Leech Tishman Fuscaldo & Lampl LLC said Matthew J. Burne joined as an associate in its bankruptcy and creditors’ rights practice group. Mr. Burne focuses his practice on representing clients in commercial transactions, restructuring and bankruptcy matters.

Nonprofit

Sojourner House MOMS named new board members: Karla Barham, a service coordinator at National Church Residences and a member of Delta Sigma Theta sorority; Demi Kolke, a senior program manager of Corridor Revitalization at Neighborhood Allies; and Norma Raiff, who was executive director of Sojourner House for 12 years and is now an adjunct professor of the division of business and entrepreneurship at Chatham University.

Technology

Uniguest promoted Michael Rethage to senior vice president, head of senior living division

Business notes

Youngjae Chun, associate professor in industrial and bioengineering at the University of Pittsburgh, received a grant from the Children’s Heart Foundation to develop a heart valve for infants born with congenital heart defects. The new device can be placed without a risky open surgery, and it can also shift and grow with the baby’s heart as it develops. 

Awards and Honors

Monique DeMonaco was approved for certification as Women's Business Enterprise National Council, Women-Owned Small Business, and Economically Disadvantaged Women-Owned Small Business. Ms. DeMonaco's business includes executive and life coaching and training.
