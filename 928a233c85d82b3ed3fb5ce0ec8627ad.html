Woman dies in early-morning house fire in Munhall

An elderly woman who was pulled from a house fire early Wednesday morning has died, according to Pittsburgh Post-Gazette news partner KDKA.

Emergency crews were dispatched to the 1200 block of Ravine Street around 1 a.m. for reports of heavy smoke coming from the home.

The woman was found in a back bedroom. She died shortly after, according to KDKA.

One firefighter was treated for heat exhaustion.

The cause of the fire is being investigated.
