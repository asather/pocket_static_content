Report: Son of Penguins co-owner Ron Burkle found dead in Los Angeles

The son of Penguins’ co-owner Ron Burkle has reportedly been found dead in Los Angeles.

People Magazine is reporting Burkle’s 27-year-old son, Andrew, was found dead Monday in his Beverly Hills apartment. The cause of death has not been released.

While authorities have yet to officially identify him, People reports, the Burkle family released a statement to the magazine asking for privacy and remembering Andrew as a “young man full of life and entrepreneurial energy.”

People Magazine said police believe Andrew Burkle died early Monday in his apartment but was found later that evening. Ron Burkle, 67, rushed to the scene and was there with police, according to the magazine.

Andrew Burkle worked as a producer with a film company in Los Angeles.

“Everyone he met commented on his humble demeanor and hard work,” the family’s statement said.

He is survived by his father, his mother, a sister Carrie, and a brother John.
