Police investigating after man shot and killed in Beltzhoover

Pittsburgh Police are investigating after a man was shot and killed in Beltzhoover on Wednesday.

Police responded to a call of a shot fired in the 200 block of Taft Avenue at approximately 11:45 a.m. When they arrived, they found a male victim in the street with a gunshot wound to his upper body.

Officials say he was transported to a hospital and was later pronounced dead.

The victim was not identified.
