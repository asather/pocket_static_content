2 dead from crash in Forward Township; Allegheny County homicide investigating

Allegheny County Police reported Friday night two people have died after a single vehicle crashed in Forward Township. 

At around 7:36 p.m., Allegheny County 9-1-1 was notified of a single vehicle crash in the area of Bunola River Road and Oliver Drive. When first responders arrived at the scene, police say they found a 37-year-old male victim deceased at the scene. Police say a 28-year-old woman was transported to a local hospital where she died. 

County homicide detectives were called to assist and determined through their initial investigation that the vehicle was traveling south on Bunola River Road when it crossed the center line, went up a hillside and rolled over multiple times. Detectives say they believe speed was a factor in the collision.

This investigation is ongoing. Anyone with information concerning this incident is asked to call the County Police Tip Line: 1-833-ALL-TIPS. Callers can remain anonymous.
