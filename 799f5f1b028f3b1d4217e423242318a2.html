North Side helicopter traffic related to film production

Helicopter traffic on the North Side is part of the filming of the Netflix movie, “Sweet Girl,” and also involves the closing of some roadways, although that information wasn’t available Sunday evening, said City Police spokesman Chris Togneri.

However, two streets were closed throughout the evening — the 10th Street Bypass and a portion of Fort Duquesne Boulevard.

The Netflix revenge drama, led by Jason Momoa, stars Oscar-winner Marisa Tomei, whose credits include “My Cousin Vinny,” “The Wrestler” and Aunt May in “Spider-Man: Far From Home,” the Post-Gazette reported Dec. 20.

“Sweet Girl” is among the movies that have been filming around town, along with “I'm Your Woman,” starring Rachel Brosnahan (“The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel”).
