Green Day, Weezer, Fall Out Boy stadium tour postponed

The Green Day, Weezer, Fall Out Boy stadium tour, set to play PNC Park in August, has been postponed.

The bands have decided to push the Hella Mega Tour to the summer of 2021 due to concerns about the coronavirus.
