Jason Isbell concert at UPMC Events Center moved to May 2021

Jason Isbell and the 400 Unit, who were scheduled to play the UPMC Events Center on June 26, have moved the date to May 30, 2021 due to the coronavirus. 

Purchased tickets will be valid for the new date.

The Americana band will be touring on  “Reunions,” new album released last week.

Isbell and wife Amanda Shires, who plays violin and sings, performed the album last week at a nearly empty Nashville theater.
