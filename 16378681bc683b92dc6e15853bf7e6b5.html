A man’s body is discovered in Shaler, prompting a police investigation

The Allegheny County Police is investigating the discovery of a body in Shaler, it was confirmed Monday night.

Shaler police Chief Sean Frank said that hikers found the body, a male, over a hillside off Marzolf Road Extension at approximately 6:20 p.m.

Chief Frank also said that the Allegheny County Homicide Unit was called to the scene and are currently handling the investigation.

No other details about the case were readily available.
