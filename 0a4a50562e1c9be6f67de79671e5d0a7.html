Michael Bloomberg planning to visit Pittsburgh on Thursday

Democratic presidential candidate Mike Bloomberg will visit Pittsburgh on Thursday as he prepares for the Pennsylvania primary in April, Bloomberg 2020 communications associate Rosie Lapowsky confirmed Monday.

The announcement came one day after Mr. Bloomberg’s campaign opened a new field office in Pittsburgh.

Ms. Lapowsky said that although she could not share more information at this time, updates on Mr. Bloomberg’s visit will be given by their communications team over the next few days.
