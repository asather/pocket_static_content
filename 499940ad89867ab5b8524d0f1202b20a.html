Nickelback added to the S&T Bank Music Park schedule

It’s going to be a Nickelback summer at S&T Bank Music Park.

The Canadian rock band and popular punchline will return on June 30 with Stone Temple Pilots and Tyler Brant & The Shakedown.

Tickets go on sale at 10 a.m. Thursday at Livenation.com.

Nickelback will celebrate the 15th anniversary of “All The Right Reasons,” which had seven radio singles including “Photograph,” “Rockstar,” “Far Away,” “If Everyone Cared,” and “Savin’ Me,” by performing the album in its entirety, in addition to other hits.
