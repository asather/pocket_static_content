1 hurt in Point Breeze shooting

A male was injured Tuesday in a shooting in Point Breeze.

Pittsburgh police were called around 12:19 p.m. to the 7300 block of Penn Avenue, where they found the victim with a gunshot wound to the upper thigh.

The officers applied a tourniquet to the victim, who was conscious and alert, police said.

The victim, whose identity and age have not been released, was taken to a hospital in stable condition.

Police said they have no description of the suspects.
