Westmoreland County man accused of child sexual abuse

A 20-year-old man from Westmoreland County has been arrested on charges of sexually abusing minors.

Kilian A. Thomas, address unknown, was arrested Thursday by Pennsylvania State Police out of Greensburg on multiple felony charges of raping a child, statutory sexual assault and corruption of minors, all stemming from an incident on Nov. 29, according to court records.

Mr. Thomas is accused of raping two girls, ages 11 and 13, one of whom he met on the social media platform Snapchat, according to KDKA-TV, Pittsburgh Post-Gazette news partner.
