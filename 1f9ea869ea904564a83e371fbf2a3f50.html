Wilkinsburg man sentenced for shooting man armed with a sword

A Wilkinsburg man will be immediately paroled after being sentenced Thursday for involuntary manslaughter.

Alonzo Glenn, 28, admitted to killing Marion White, 49, of Wilkinsburg after White threatened him with a sword. Police said the two men were in a home in the 1500 block of Laketon Road on June 11 when there was a confrontation between the two, and White retrieved a sword from upstairs.

Glenn fled to the dining room at first, a witness told police, then went to the stairs and shot White, who had gunshot wounds to the head and chest.

Glenn pleaded guilty before Common Pleas Judge Susan Evashavik DiLucente in February.
