McKeesport man arrested on weapons charges after allegedly trying to flee police

A McKeesport man was arrested after allegedly running from police Thursday.

Police say they first started chasing Christopher Flewellen, 28, around 5:50 p.m. after he committed traffic violations. He began trying to escape by driving away.

Authorities say they later found him exiting the car on Pirl Street and trying to elude capture on foot. He tried to throw away a stolen firearm before police took him into custody, officials said.

Flewellen was arrested on felony firearms charges, as well as charges of receiving stolen property and fleeing police.

County police assisted the McKeesport Police Department.
