Joe Rogan show at PPG Paints Arena moved to November

The performance by comedian/podcaster Joe Rogan, scheduled for May 16 at PPG Paints Arena, has been moved to Nov. 20.

Tickets for the May show will be honored for the rescheduled date. For further ticket info, reach out to point of purchase.
