Police: North Side man wanted on child sex assault charges

Police have issued an arrest warrant for a North Side man accused of sexually assaulting a child.

Delrio Demetrius Ivy, 43, faces charges of rape of a child, involuntary deviate sexual intercourse of a child, aggravated indecent assault of a child, endangering the welfare of a child, unlawful contact of a minor and indecent assault, according to Pittsburgh police.

He is described as 6 feet tall, 210 pounds with brown eyes. Police said he is known to frequent the Manchester area.

Anyone with information on his whereabouts is asked to call city police at 412-323-7141.
