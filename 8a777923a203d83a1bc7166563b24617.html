Catering company, several establishments closed for operating without health permit in February

The Allegheny County Health Department issued closures to several establishments in the month of February. 

Trap Yoga Studio/Unforgettable Edibles/Mz T's, all operating at 5132 Butler St., Lawrenceville were ordered closed on Feb. 28 due to operating without a health permit. A cease-and-desist order was also issued, according to the ACHD. 

The following also were ordered closed due to operating without a valid health permit:

— Indian Flavors on Hinsdale Lane in McDonald on Feb. 27.

— Beyond Blessed Catering 18 & LLC on Arlington Avenue in Pittsburgh on Feb. 26.

— Naomi Malone, dba Nay's Parfaits on Jennylind Street in McKeesport on Feb. 18.

— Lovin' Spoonful on Prospect Street in Bellevue on Feb. 14.

Inspection reports for food facilities in the county are available here.
