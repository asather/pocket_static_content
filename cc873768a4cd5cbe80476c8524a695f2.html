Thoms Run Road closed in Collier after large water main break

A main road in Collier Township is shut down Tuesday after a large water main break sent water shooting several feet into the air.

The break closed Thoms Run Road between Presto-Sygan and Forsythe roads at around 3:30 p.m.

A spokesperson for Penn-American Water said Tuesday evening that the break impacted a 30-inch main and left approximately 60 customers without service. The company expects that service will be back on by Wednesday evening.

In the meantime, the spokesperson said, a water tanker will be stationed this evening at the Presto Fire Department on Thoms Run Road. Customers should bring their own containers.

Once crews remove debris caused by a water main break, Thoms Run Road will be restricted to one lane while repairs are made.
