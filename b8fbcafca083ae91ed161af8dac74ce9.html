Fire marshal's office investigating cause of fatal fire in Fayette County

Fire officials are investigating the cause of a mobile home fire that killed a woman in Fayette County on Tuesday.

The fire was reported shortly before 1 p.m. in the 1800 block of Fourth Street in South Connellsville.

The county Coroner’s Office identified the victim as 58-year-old Patricia Ringer.

Pennsylvania State Police fire marshal’s office is looking into the cause.
