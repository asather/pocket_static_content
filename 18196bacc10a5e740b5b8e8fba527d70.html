Man critically wounded in Mount Washington shooting

Police are investigating after a man was shot multiple times Saturday night in Mount Washington.

Pittsburgh police said the shooting happened around 10:30 p.m. in the 100 block of Wyoming Street near E. Sycamore Street.

When first responders arrived, they found the victim near the sidewalk with gunshot wounds.

He was taken to a hospital in critical condition.

Police have not released a motive in the shooting.
