One dead, three injured after fall from roof in Butler County

At least one person has died after four people fell from a roof in Butler County Monday morning.

County emergency officials said the coroner has been called to the scene on Huch Road in Adams Township.

The three others who fell were transported to the hospital. The extent of their injuries was not immediately available.

Emergency crews were dispatched to the scene after receiving a call shortly before 8 a.m.
