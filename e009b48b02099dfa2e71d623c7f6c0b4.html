A second Giant Eagle worker in Beaver County tests positive for COVID-19

A Giant Eagle worker in Beaver County has tested positive for coronavirus, according to Pittsburgh Post-Gazette news partner KDKA.

The person worked at the Rochester Store on West Madison Street.

The worker was last in the store on April 6. The store has been cleaned and sanitized, KDKA reported.

According to the company, an employee who worked at the Chippewa store on Constitution Boulevard tested positive for COVID-19 on April 7. The employee last worked on March 30.
