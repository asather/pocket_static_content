Police searching for driver who hit cyclist in Friendship

Pittsburgh police say they are searching for a driver who Wednesday evening hit a cyclist and drove off.

The collision occurred at the intersection of Baum Boulevard and South Negley Avenue in Friendship around 8:15 p.m, police said. The cyclist told police and EMS she was hit by a white vehicle that did not stop.

The woman was transported to the hospital and is reportedly in stable condition.
