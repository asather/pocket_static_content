Allegheny County treasurer's office to reopen May 18

Allegheny County Treasure John Weinstein announced Monday that his office will return to normal business hours on May 18 as the county moves into the yellow phase.

The office has been closed since March 24 as a precaution against the spreading novel coronavirus.

The extended county property tax deadline remains at June 30.
