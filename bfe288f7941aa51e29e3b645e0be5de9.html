Pirates' final exhibition game moved up to 5:05 p.m.

Due to the threat of rain, the Pirates have changed the time of Wednesday's exhibition game against the Cleveland Indians at PNC Park.

The game will now start at 5:05 p.m., and it will not air on AT&T SportsNet as originally anticipated. It will be broadcast on 93.7 The Fan and the Pirates Radio Network.

Wednesday marks the last of three exhibition contests against Cleveland. The Pirates have lost the first two. They open their season Friday in St. Louis.
