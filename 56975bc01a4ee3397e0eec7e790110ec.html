South Fayette park and ride to close

The South Fayette Park and Ride, located at 100 Hickory Grade Road, is set to close on July 4.

The owner of the park and ride has decided to end the agreement with Port Authority so that a new development can move into the space, according to the authority. As a result, the 31-Bridgeville and the G31-Bridgeville Flyer will not serve that park and ride. No other stops will be missed.

As an alternative to the South Fayette location, Port Authority is encouraging customers to park in the South Hills Village Garage. That garage has a daily fee of $2 or $22 for a monthly pass.

The 31-Bridgeville and the G31-Bridgeville Flyer will continue to serve the Woodville Park and Ride in Carnegie, however Port Authority warns that this lot is quickly filled on most weekday mornings.

Port Authority is continuing to look for alternate parking options in that area.
