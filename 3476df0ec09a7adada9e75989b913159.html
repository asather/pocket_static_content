List of WPIAL basketball postponements, schedule for Friday

The snow caused a number of postponements in WPIAL basketball Friday night, but many games are still being played.

This is the final weekend of play and a number of top games are still on, including Upper St. Clair at Fox Chapel and North Allegheny at North Hills. Fox Chapel is the only undefeated team in the WPIAL and ranked No. 1 in WPIAL Class 6A. Upper St. Clair is ranked No. 4.

The winner of the North Allegheny-North Hills game makes the playoffs and the loser is out. The two rivals have played in the final section game for decades, but this is the first time since 1982 that they play with the winner making the playoffs, and the loser failing to qualify.
