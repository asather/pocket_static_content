State, city extend income tax filing deadline to July 15

The income tax filing deadline for Pennsylvania and Pittsburgh residents will now align with the federal deadline of July 15.

The commonwealth’s Department of Revenue will also waive fees and interest on 2019 personal income tax payments until the deadline. 

The extension applies to both final 2019 tax returns and payments owed in Pennsylvania, and estimated payments for the first and second quarters of 2020.

The city’s Finance Department will continue real estate billings, but will stop all real estate delinquent billings and late fee charges.

The department has stopped all treasurer’s sales of city-owned properties.
