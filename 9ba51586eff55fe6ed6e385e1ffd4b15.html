The Clarks move to drive-in concert from Moon to Butler

The Clarks concerts, scheduled for June 26-27 at Dependable Drive-In in Moon, have been moved to the Starlight Drive-In in Butler and reduced to a single night, June 27.

Gene the Werewolf and The Hawkeyes will also perform.

Tickets purchased from both nights will be transferred and accepted at the new venue and date. Refunds are available upon request (via point of purchase). Refund request deadline for customers is Tuesday. Visit your Eventbrite profile or use the "Contact the Organizer" tool to submit a refund request.

According to Drusky Entertainment, the venue change was due to “a scheduling conflict.” The Saturday event had already been sold out.

General admission car tickets (up to four occupants per vehicle) are $100 at DruskyEnt.com or Eventbrite.com. Limited VIP Car (up to four occupants per vehicle) will be available for $130. VIP allows access in the first three rows in front of the stage.

For both ticket types, parking will be general admission (first come, first serve). There will not be any reserved parking. If arriving day of show, there will be a charge of $30 per person over the four-person limit, if over four people are in the car.

These are the guidelines for show-goers:

• If you are sick, running a fever, or have been exposed to COVID-19, please stay home until you are healthy

•For proper social distancing guidelines, only eTickets will be available.

• Patrons will be subject to a mandatory trunk check upon entry

• Patrons are allowed to watch the show from outside of their vehicle, but are only permitted to in front of their car, between the car and the stage.

• For the courtesy of other vehicles and patrons, large trucks and SUVS may be directed to the back of the lot or ends of the aisles, so as to not block the sight-lines of other concert-goers.

• Event will continue rain or shine. Patron safety is our priority, so in the event local safety officials deem it unsafe to continue to the show, we will communicate that to everyone via video wall, FM transmission, or loudspeaker.

• Audio from the show will be transmitted via PA System & FM transmitter.

• Although a facemask is not required to be worn when checking in, facemasks must be worn if you are out the vehicle for any reason.

• If out of your vehicle for restrooms or concessions, we ask that patrons please maintain a safe and social distance of 6 feet

• At the conclusion of the concert, please remain in your parking space until directed by event staff to exit the lot. In the case of an emergency, please remain in your space until directed by event staff. Drive slowly and follow all directions

Restrictions

• No outside food or beverages will be permitted into the theatre grounds

• Coolers and firearms will not permitted into the theater

• No tall and/or large vehicles (i.e. trucks, campers, busses, Winnebago's, etc.) will be permitted

• For the safety of fans, tailgating will not be permitted

• Smoking is not permitted in the drive-in theatre
