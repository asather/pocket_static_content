Man drowns while boating with friends

A man died Friday night after falling into the Allegheny River while boating with friends.

As the operator of the boat pulled into a dock at the Washingtonâ€™s Landing marina around 9:45 p.m., the man either fell or jumped into the river and did not resurface, according to Pittsburgh police.

Rescue divers from Pittsburgh River Rescue searched the water for two hours, in heavy debris and dark conditions with little visibility. Just before midnight, the EMS-certified divers located the man about 40 feet downriver and pronounced him dead at 12:01 Saturday morning.

Pittsburgh police and the PA Fish and Boat Commission are investigating.
