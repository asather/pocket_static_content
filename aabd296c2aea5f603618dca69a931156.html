Water main break closes portions of Becks Run Road

Police have shut down Becks Run Road, between Wagner Street and East Carson Street, due to a water main break Wednesday morning.

The break happened shortly before 2:30 a.m. on Wagner Street near the intersection of Mountain Avenue in the St. Clair neighborhood of Pittsburgh.

Water could be seen shooting out of the water main "like a geyser," police said, and spilling onto the road.

It was unclear when the road would reopen.

According to Pittsburgh Post-Gazette news partner KDKA, the water main break is about a half-mile from East Agnew Avenue, where a massive water main break occurred in September of last year.
