17-year-old flown to hospital after fall from Buttermilk Falls in Beaver County

A 17-year-old boy was flown to a Pittsburgh hospital Wednesday night after falling from Buttermilk Falls in Beaver County.

Emergency crews responded around 8:06 p.m. to the scene, dispatchers said. He was transported in stable condition, officials from Big Beaver Volunteer Fire Company said.

The waterfall is located near Route 18 in Beaver Falls and is west of the Beaver River. 

Medic Rescue and Homewood Volunteer Fire Department also responded to the incident.
