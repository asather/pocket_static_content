Winners of the 77th annual Golden Globes

Winners of the 77th annual Golden Globes, presented Sunday at the Beverly Hilton in Los Angeles.

Motion pictures

Drama: “1917”

Actress, Drama: Renée Zellweger (“Judy”)

Actor, Drama: Joaquin Phoenix (“Joker”)

Musical or Comedy: “Once Upon a Time in Hollywood”

Actress, Musical or Comedy: Awkwafina (“The Farewell”)

Actor, Musical or Comedy: Taron Egerton (“Rocketman”)

Director: Sam Mendes (“1917”)

Supporting Actress: Laura Dern (“Marriage Story”)

Supporting Actor: Brad Pitt (“Once Upon a Time in Hollywood”)

Animated: “Missing Link”

Foreign Language: “Parasite”

Screenplay: Quentin Tarantino (“Once Upon a Time in Hollywood”)

Original Score: Hildur Guonadottir (“Joker”)

Original Song: “I’m Gonna Love Me Again” (“Rocketman”)

 

Television

Drama: “Succession”

Actress, Drama: Olivia Colman (“The Crown”)

Actor, Drama: Brian Cox (“Succession”)

Musical or Comedy: “Fleabag”

Actress, Musical or Comedy: Phoebe Waller-Bridge (“Fleabag”)

Actor, Musical or Comedy: Ramy Youssef (“Ramy”)

Limited Series or TV movie: ““Chernobyl”

Actress, Limited Series or TV Movie: Michelle Williams (“Fosse/Verdon”)

Actor, Limited Series or TV Movie: Russell Crowe (“The Loudest Voice”)

Supporting Actoress. Series, Limited Series or TV Movie: Patricia Arquette (“The Act”)

Supporting Actor, Series, Limited Series TV Movie: Stellan Skarsgard (“Chernobyl”)

 

Previously announced

Cecil B. DeMille Award: Tom Hanks

Carol Burnett Award: Ellen DeGeneres
